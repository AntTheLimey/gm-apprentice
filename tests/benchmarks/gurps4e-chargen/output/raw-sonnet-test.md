# SERGEANT CALLUM "CAL" REEVES
**22 SAS, D Squadron — 175 points**

Callum Reeves joined the Parachute Regiment at 18, completed selection for 22 SAS at 24, and has since run operations across Central Asia, West Africa, and the Balkans. He is steady, methodical, and deeply loyal to the men he fights alongside — but the work has left marks that only show up in the small hours of the morning.
