# MARCUS HOLDEN
**Former SAS Sergeant / PMC Operator — 175 points**

---

## Background

Holden served 14 years with the British Army, the last eight with 22 SAS, rising to Sergeant before a disputed engagement in Mali triggered a quiet administrative discharge. He now works under contract for Proteus Group, a private military consultancy operating across North Africa and the Gulf, handling direct-action raids, personnel recovery, and close-protection assignments for clients who need results without paperwork. He is methodical, professionally lethal, and perpetually one bad day from the anger that ended his military career.
