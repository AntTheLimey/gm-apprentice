## COLE WHITFIELD

**Concept:** Former SAS Sergeant / Private Military Contractor
**Point Total:** 175 points | **TL:** 8 (Modern) | **Realism:** Realistic

### Background

Cole Whitfield served twelve years in 22 SAS, rising to
Sergeant before a classified operation in the Sahel went
sideways and left him with nightmares and a file full of
redactions. He took a medical discharge and signed on with
Meridian Risk Group, a PMC operating in conflict zones across
Africa and the Middle East. Cole is methodical, lethal, and
haunted -- a professional soldier who does the job but pays
for it when the lights go out.
