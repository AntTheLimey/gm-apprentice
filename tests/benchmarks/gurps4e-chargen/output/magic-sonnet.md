# MIRA ASHVANE
**Hedge Mage and Apothecary — 175 points**
*TL3 Fantasy*

Mira Ashvane learned her craft from a wandering mage who recognised her latent gift when she was a girl, spending three years as his reluctant apprentice before he moved on and she stayed. She has run "Ashvane's Remedies" in the market town of Millhaven for twenty years: a cramped shopfront stocked with herbal tinctures, magelock seals for merchants' strongboxes, and the occasional enchanted household trinket. She is simultaneously the most trusted healer in the district and the subject of its most persistent gossip.
