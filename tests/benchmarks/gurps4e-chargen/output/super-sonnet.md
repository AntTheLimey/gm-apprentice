## MIRA SABLE
*Program designation: Echo-7 — Psionic Operative — 175 points*

Removed from her family at age nine when a telekinetic event destroyed her school gymnasium, Mira spent eleven years inside Project Chimera — a classified government program — where her abilities were systematically enhanced and weaponized through methods she refuses to discuss. Eighteen months ago she fled during a transport transfer, leaving four incapacitated guards and a crumpled armored van in her wake; she now drifts between cities on cash work, staying one step ahead of the program's retrieval teams and quietly searching for other subjects who may have escaped.
