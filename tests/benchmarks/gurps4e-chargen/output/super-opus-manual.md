## VERA KESSLER, "KINESIS"

**Psionic Operative** — 175 points

### Background

Vera Kessler was a neuroscience graduate student who volunteered
for a classified neuro-enhancement study run by the Prometheus
Institute. The treatments activated latent psionic potential,
granting her telekinetic and telepathic abilities — but when the
Institute moved to weaponise the subjects, Vera escaped. Now she
works as a freelance troubleshooter, using her powers to help
people while staying one step ahead of Prometheus recovery teams.
