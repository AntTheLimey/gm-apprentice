# CORPORAL DANIEL "BRICK" HARTLEY
**22 SAS Regiment, D Squadron, Mountain Troop — 175 points**

*"He doesn't talk about what he's done. He doesn't need to."*

---

## Concept

A ten-year veteran of 22 SAS with tours in Afghanistan, Mali, and several
operations that will never appear in any public record. Hartley passed
Selection on his second attempt, having originally served as a Royal Green
Jackets rifleman. He is methodical, professionally aggressive, and quietly
haunted. Hartley's primary role is HALO insertion and close-quarters battle;
his secondary qualifications cover demolitions and trauma care. He trusts his
team absolutely, mistrusts anyone wearing red tabs, and makes the best cup of
tea in any bergen.

**Role in the group:** Primary shooter, tactical point-man, and field medic.

## GM Notes

**Hooks and Connections**

- The Regiment can recall Hartley at any time (Duty, 15 or less) — missions can
  arrive mid-campaign via encrypted message.
- His Nightmares and Flashbacks trace to a specific incident (GM to define) —
  perhaps a hostage rescue that went wrong, or a friendly fire event. This is
  deliberately left vague for campaign integration.
- Code of Honor (Soldier's) creates friction in morally grey operations:
  he will not execute prisoners, will not harm civilians, and will report
  clear violations of the laws of armed conflict.
- Overconfidence means he occasionally pushes into situations where a more
  cautious approach was warranted — useful for plot complications.
- His Sense of Duty (Teammates) is a strong hook: threaten his people and he
  will act, even without orders.

**In Combat**

Hartley operates at Guns (Rifle)-16: against an unmoving target in good
conditions, he hits on 3-16 (probability ~97.7%). Against a moving target in
cover with a snapshot, he still hits comfortably. His Dodge of 9 (10 with CR,
9 under light load) is solid, and he benefits from Combat Reflexes' +6 to
surprise resistance — he is extremely difficult to ambush. High Pain Threshold
means he ignores shock penalties from wounds, keeping him functional after
being hit.

**Social and Narrative**

Hartley is no blunt instrument. IQ 12 and skill in Tactics, Intelligence
Analysis, and Observation make him a useful asset in planning phases. His
Savoir-Faire (Military)-13 allows him to navigate inter-service politics
when needed, though his Distrust of brass (Quirk) will colour these interactions.
