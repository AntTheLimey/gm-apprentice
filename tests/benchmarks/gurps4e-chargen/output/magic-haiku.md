## **ELARA THIMBLESPARK**
### Hedge Mage & Potion Crafter — 175 points

Elara is a practical mage who scratches out a living brewing healing potions, crafting minor enchantments, and selling herbal remedies in the market square of Millhaven. Though not wealthy, her genuine care for the townspeople and honest craftsmanship have made her indispensable to the community. She lives above a small shop that doubles as her alchemical laboratory, constantly muttering incantations and rearranging bottles.
