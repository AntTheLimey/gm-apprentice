## Feature Descriptions

### Level 1

**Lay On Hands**
A pool of healing equal to **5 × Paladin level** HP (refreshes on Long Rest). As an Action, touch a creature to restore any amount of HP from the pool, or spend 5 HP from the pool to cure one disease or neutralize one poison affecting the creature.

**Spellcasting**
Half-caster. Uses CHA as the spellcasting ability. Prepares spells from the Paladin spell list after each Long Rest. Spell slots follow the half-caster progression (see below).

**Weapon Mastery**
Can apply a Mastery property to 2 weapons you are proficient with. Change the chosen weapons after a Long Rest.

### Level 2

**Fighting Style**
Choose one Fighting Style option (e.g., Defense, Dueling, Great Weapon Fighting, Protection, Blessed Warrior, Blind Fighting).

**Paladin's Smite**
When you hit a creature with a melee weapon or an Unarmed Strike, you can expend one spell slot to deal extra Radiant damage. The extra damage is **2d8** for a 1st-level slot, +1d8 per slot level above 1st. Doubles against Undead and Fiends. This replaces the old verbal "Divine Smite" — it is now a Bonus Action decision *after* the hit (no longer always-on).

### Level 3

**Channel Divinity (2/rest)**
Gain 2 uses per Short or Long Rest. The specific Channel Divinity options are determined by your Oath (subclass). Every Paladin gains access to their subclass Channel Divinity options at this level.

**Subclass (Sacred Oath)**
Choose your Sacred Oath at level 3. The SRD subclass is **Oath of Devotion** (see below). All subclasses also provide bonus spells always-prepared.

### Level 5

**Extra Attack**
Attack twice whenever you take the Attack action.

**Faithful Steed**
You can cast *Find Steed* without expending a spell slot. Once used, you must finish a Long Rest before using this feature again. The steed gains benefits scaling with your level.

### Level 6

**Aura of Protection**
While you are conscious, you and friendly creatures within **10 feet** add your CHA modifier (minimum +1) to all saving throws. This is one of the strongest features in the entire game.

### Level 9

**Abjure Foes**
As an Action, expend one use of Channel Divinity. Up to a number of creatures equal to your CHA modifier (min 1) within 60 feet must make a WIS saving throw. On a failure, the creature is Frightened of you for 1 minute and its speed is 0. On a success, its speed is halved for 1 minute. Frightened creatures repeat the save at the end of each of their turns.

### Level 10

**Aura of Courage**
While you are conscious, you and friendly creatures within **10 feet** are immune to the Frightened condition.

### Level 11

**Radiant Strikes**
Your weapon attacks and Unarmed Strikes deal an extra **1d8 Radiant damage** on a hit.

### Level 14

**Restoring Touch**
When you use Lay On Hands to restore HP to a creature, you can also remove one of the following conditions from them at no extra cost from the pool: Blinded, Charmed, Deafened, Frightened, Paralyzed, or Stunned. You can also remove one Attunement (curse removal).

### Level 18

**Aura Expansion**
The range of your Aura of Protection and Aura of Courage expands to **30 feet**.

## SRD Subclass: Oath of Devotion

**Bonus Spells (always prepared):** Protection from Evil and Good, Shield of Faith (3rd); Lesser Restoration, Zone of Truth (5th); Beacon of Hope, Dispel Magic (9th); Freedom of Movement, Guardian of Faith (13th); Commune, Flame Strike (17th).

**Channel Divinity Options (Level 3):**
- **Sacred Weapon** — As a Bonus Action, imbue one weapon with holy energy for 1 minute. The weapon becomes magical, emits bright light in a 20-foot radius, and you add your CHA modifier to attack rolls made with it.
- **Turn the Unholy** — Undead and Fiends that can see or hear you within 30 feet must make a WIS saving throw or be Turned for 1 minute.

**Level 7 — Aura of Devotion:** You and friendly creatures within 10 feet are immune to the Charmed condition while you are conscious.

**Level 15 — Holy Nimbus:** As an Action, emanate an aura of sunlight for 1 minute. Bright light fills a 30-foot radius. Undead and Fiends that start their turn in this light take 10 Radiant damage. Once used, you must finish a Long Rest.

**Level 20 — Holy Nimbus (capstone):** You can use Holy Nimbus without expending a use; it recharges on a Long Rest.

## Key Changes from 2014 to 2024

- **Paladin's Smite** is now a **Bonus Action** triggered after landing a hit (not an instant free addition). This removes the ability to "fish" for crits and then smite — you decide before knowing if it's a crit, but after hitting.
- **Weapon Mastery** is new — gives martial flexibility similar to Fighter.
- **Lay On Hands** now also removes conditions (via Restoring Touch at 14).
- **Abjure Foes** replaces Abjure Enemy and affects multiple targets.
- Subclass gained at **Level 3** (same as 2014).
- **ASI levels:** 4, 8, 12, 16, 19.
