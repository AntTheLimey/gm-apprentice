# Paladin — D&D 5e 2024 Class Features

*Source: SRD 5.2 (CC-BY 4.0)*

## Core Traits

- **Hit Die:** d10
- **Primary Abilities:** STR + CHA
- **Saving Throw Proficiencies:** WIS, CHA
- **Skills (choose 2):** Athletics, Insight, Intimidation, Medicine, Persuasion, Religion
- **Weapon Proficiencies:** Simple + Martial
- **Armor Proficiencies:** Light, Medium, Heavy, Shields
- **Spellcasting:** Half caster (spell slots up to 5th level)

## Feature Descriptions

**Level 1**
- **Lay On Hands:** Pool of HP equal to 5 × Paladin level. Use an action to restore HP from the pool to a creature you touch, or spend 5 HP to cure one disease or poison.
- **Spellcasting:** Prepare spells from the Paladin list using CHA as your spellcasting modifier. Half-caster spell slot progression (slots up to 5th level).
- **Weapon Mastery:** Gain the Mastery property of certain weapons.

**Level 2**
- **Fighting Style:** Choose a Fighting Style (options include Defense, Dueling, Great Weapon Fighting, Protection, etc.).
- **Paladin's Smite:** When you hit with a weapon attack, you can expend a spell slot to deal extra radiant damage (2d8 per slot level, +1d8 if target is undead or a fiend). This replaces the old separate Divine Smite and now works as a reaction, meaning it triggers when you hit rather than before you roll damage.

**Level 3**
- **Channel Divinity (2 uses/rest):** Gained alongside your Oath subclass. Uses the subclass's specific Channel Divinity options in addition to any class-wide ones.
- **Subclass (Sacred Oath):** Chosen at level 3. Oaths grant Channel Divinity options and expanded spell lists. SRD option: Oath of Devotion.

**Level 5**
- **Extra Attack:** Attack twice when you take the Attack action.
- **Faithful Steed:** You can cast *Find Steed* without expending a spell slot, and it doesn't count against your prepared spells.

**Level 6**
- **Aura of Protection (10 ft radius):** While conscious, you and friendly creatures within 10 ft add your CHA modifier (minimum +1) to all saving throws. One of the most powerful aura features in the game.

**Level 9**
- **Abjure Foes:** Use Channel Divinity to frighten and potentially paralyze a group of enemies (targets up to CHA modifier in creatures).

**Level 10**
- **Aura of Courage (10 ft radius):** You and friendly creatures within 10 ft are immune to the Frightened condition while you are conscious.

**Level 11**
- **Radiant Strikes:** Your weapon attacks deal an extra 1d8 radiant damage on a hit.

**Level 14**
- **Restoring Touch:** When you use Lay On Hands to restore HP, you can also end one condition (Blinded, Charmed, Deafened, Frightened, Paralyzed, or Stunned) affecting the target at no additional HP cost.

**Level 18**
- **Aura Expansion:** Aura of Protection and Aura of Courage expand to 30 ft radius.

## SRD Subclass: Oath of Devotion

Gained at level 3. Devotion focuses on honor, virtue, and fighting the forces of evil.

- **Sacred Weapon (Channel Divinity):** As a bonus action, imbue a weapon with radiant energy (+CHA to attack rolls, emits bright light 20 ft / dim light 20 ft further) for 10 minutes.
- **Holy Nimbus (level 20 capstone):** Emit an aura of sunlight. Each hostile creature that starts its turn within 30 ft takes 10 radiant damage. You also have advantage on saving throws against spells cast by fiends or undead.

**Oath of Devotion Expanded Spells** (always prepared, don't count against Prepared total):
- 3rd: *Protection from Evil and Good*, *Shield of Faith*
- 5th: *Aid*, *Zone of Truth*
- 9th: *Beacon of Hope*, *Dispel Magic*
- 13th: *Freedom of Movement*, *Guardian of Faith*
- 17th: *Commune*, *Flame Strike*

## Key Notes

- **Half caster:** Paladin spell slots follow the half-caster table — you gain your first slot at level 2, and max out at 5th-level slots. For the exact slot counts, cross-reference with half-caster progression (roughly half the full-caster table rounded down by level).
- **Smite synergy:** Paladin's Smite triggering on hit (rather than being declared before rolling damage as in the 2014 rules) means you wait to see if you hit before spending the slot — a significant quality-of-life improvement.
- **Aura of Protection** is widely considered one of the strongest class features in the game; a +5 CHA modifier means +5 to every save for your whole party in a 10 ft radius.
- **ASI levels:** 4, 8, 12, 16, 19 (standard for most classes).
