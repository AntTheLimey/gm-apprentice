## New Regency Occupation Stat Blocks

**Gentleman** — Landed gentry, lives by wealth and privilege.
- Formula: EDU×2 + APP×2
- CR: 50–90 (Band 4)
- Skills: Charm, Dancing, Etiquette, Fashion, Gaming, Ride, one weapon skill, one hobby skill.

**Gentlewoman** — Gentry woman; accomplishments define social standing.
- Formula: EDU×2 + APP×2
- CR: 50–90 (Band 4)
- Skills: Art/Craft (Embroidery, Fine Art, Instrument, or Singing), Dancing, Etiquette, Fashion, Language (Other), Persuade, Ride, one hobby skill.

**Nouveau Riche** — Wealthy by trade or industry; seeks gentry acceptance.
- Formula: EDU×4
- CR: 50–90 (Band 4)
- Skills: Accounting, Appraise, Art/Craft (Business), Dancing, Fast Talk, Persuade, Ride, one weapon skill.

**Servant (Footman)** — Male household servant; errands and public duties.
- Formula: INT×2 + POW×2
- CR: 10–30 (Band 1)
- Skills: Animal Handling, Art/Craft (Any), Dodge, Etiquette, Fast Talk, Listen, Ride, Spot Hidden.

**Servant (Housemaid)** — Female domestic servant; cleaning and personal service.
- Formula: INT×2 + POW×2
- CR: 10–30 (Band 1)
- Skills: Art/Craft (Cooking or Sewing), Dodge, Etiquette, Fashion, Fast Talk, Listen, Spot Hidden, Stealth.

## Hobby Careers for Gentry

Occupations marked with * in the master list may be taken as a secondary "hobby career" by a Gentleman or Gentlewoman. The PC uses those occupation's skills but retains the Gentleman/Gentlewoman Credit Rating band (50–90%). This reflects the period reality that gentry might pursue antiquarianism, artistic endeavour, exploration, or even smuggling as a serious pastime without losing their social standing.

## Skill Point Formulas (Summary)

All formulas are inherited from the base CoC 7e framework. The most common Regency formulas:

| Formula | Typical occupations |
|---|---|
| EDU×4 | Accountant, Doctor, Lawyer, Nouveau Riche, Professor, Researcher |
| EDU×2 + APP×2 | Gentleman, Gentlewoman, Entertainer, Gambler |
| EDU×2 + (STR or DEX)×2 | Soldier/Marine, Military Officer, Sailor |
| INT×2 + POW×2 | Servant (Footman), Servant (Housemaid) |

For occupations not in the Regency list, use the nearest base CoC 7e equivalent with period-appropriate skill substitutions (Keeper approval required). For example: no Drive Auto — substitute Ride or Drive (Carriage). No Firearms (modern) — substitute Firearms (Flintlock/Musket).
