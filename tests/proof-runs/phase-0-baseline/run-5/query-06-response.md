## Feature Descriptions

**Lay On Hands (Level 1):** A pool of healing equal to 5 × Paladin level in HP. As an action, restore any number of HP from the pool to a creature you touch, or expend 5 HP to cure one disease or neutralize one poison.

**Spellcasting (Level 1):** Half-caster using CHA as your spellcasting ability. Prepare a number of spells from the Paladin list (see Prepared column above). Spell slots recharge on a Long Rest.

**Weapon Mastery (Level 1):** You can use the mastery property of Simple and Martial weapons you are proficient with. The number of mastery weapons usable increases as you level.

**Fighting Style (Level 2):** Choose one Fighting Style (e.g., Defense, Dueling, Great Weapon Fighting, Protection, Blessed Warrior, Blind Fighting).

**Paladin's Smite (Level 2):** When you hit with a melee weapon, you can expend a spell slot to deal extra radiant damage: 2d8 per slot level (or 3d8 against undead/fiends). This replaces the old Divine Smite — it is now a reaction-triggered choice after the hit, not a declared-before-roll action.

**Channel Divinity (Level 3):** Gain 2 uses (3 at level 11), recharging on a Short or Long Rest. Two uses are determined by your Oath (subclass). All Paladins also gain access to their Oath's specific Channel Divinity options.

**Subclass / Sacred Oath (Level 3):** Choose your Oath, which defines your Channel Divinity options, Oath Spells (always prepared, don't count against limit), and higher-level features.

**Extra Attack (Level 5):** Attack twice when you take the Attack action.

**Faithful Steed (Level 5):** You can cast *Find Steed* without expending a spell slot. Once cast this way it requires a Long Rest to do so again.

**Aura of Protection (Level 6):** While conscious, you and friendly creatures within 10 ft add your CHA modifier (minimum +1) to all saving throws.

**Abjure Foes (Level 9):** As an action, expend one Channel Divinity use to frighten a number of creatures you can see within 60 ft (WIS save). Affected creatures are Frightened for 1 minute and have their speed reduced to 0 while Frightened. They repeat the save at the end of each turn.

**Aura of Courage (Level 10):** While conscious, you and friendly creatures within 10 ft cannot be Frightened.

**Radiant Strikes (Level 11):** Your weapon and unarmed strikes deal an extra 1d8 radiant damage on a hit.

**Restoring Touch (Level 14):** When you use Lay On Hands to restore HP to a creature, you can also remove one of the following conditions: Blinded, Charmed, Deafened, Frightened, Paralyzed, or Stunned. No HP cost beyond the normal Lay On Hands expenditure.

**Aura Expansion (Level 18):** The range of Aura of Protection and Aura of Courage expands from 10 ft to 30 ft.

## SRD Subclass: Oath of Devotion

The only subclass included in SRD 5.2.

**Oath Spells (always prepared):** Protection from Evil and Good, Shield of Faith (3rd), Aid, Zone of Truth (5th), Beacon of Hope, Dispel Magic (9th), Freedom of Movement, Guardian of Faith (13th), Commune, Flame Strike (17th).

**Channel Divinity Options:**
- **Sacred Weapon:** As a bonus action, imbue one weapon you hold with holy power for 1 minute. The weapon gains a +CHA bonus to attack rolls (min +1) and emits bright light in a 20-ft radius and dim light for an additional 20 ft.
- **Holy Nimbus (Level 20):** Once per Long Rest, emanate an aura of sunlight for 1 minute. Enemies that start their turn in the aura take 10 radiant damage. Additionally, you have advantage on saving throws against spells cast by fiends or undead.

## Key Notes for GMs

- **Paladin's Smite is now a reaction**, not a pre-declared action. Players choose to smite *after* confirming a hit, making it more flexible (and preventing smite fishing on misses).
- **Aura of Protection** is one of the strongest class features in the game — a +CHA to *all* saves for the whole party in a 10-ft radius is significant, scaling up to 30 ft at level 18.
- **Half caster:** Paladins use the half-caster spell slot table (same as Ranger). They gain 1st-level slots at level 1, up to 5th-level slots at level 17. Refer to the half-caster progression for slot counts.
- **ASI levels:** 4, 8, 12, 16, 19 (standard for most classes).
