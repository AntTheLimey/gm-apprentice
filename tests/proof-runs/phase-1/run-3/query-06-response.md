# Paladin — D&D 5e 2024 Class Features

## Core Traits

- **Hit Die:** d10
- **Primary Ability:** STR + CHA
- **Saving Throws:** WIS, CHA
- **Skills (choose 2):** Athletics, Insight, Intimidation, Medicine, Persuasion, Religion
- **Weapons:** Simple + Martial
- **Armor:** Light, Medium, Heavy, Shields
- **Spellcasting:** Half caster (spell slots up to 5th level)

## Feature Details

**Lay On Hands (Level 1)**
A pool of healing equal to 5 × your Paladin level. As an action, you can restore any number of HP from the pool to a creature you touch, or expend 5 HP to cure one disease or neutralize one poison.

**Spellcasting (Level 1)**
Half caster. You prepare spells from the Paladin list using CHA as your spellcasting ability. Spell slots reach a maximum of 5th level.

**Weapon Mastery (Level 1)**
You can use the mastery property of 2 weapons you are proficient with. You can change which weapons you apply mastery to after a Long Rest.

**Fighting Style (Level 2)**
Choose one Fighting Style option (e.g., Defense, Dueling, Great Weapon Fighting, Protection, etc.).

**Paladin's Smite (Level 2)**
When you hit a creature with a melee attack, you can expend a spell slot to deal additional radiant damage: 2d8 per slot level + 1d8 per slot level above 1st. Doesn't require an action — it's triggered on a hit.

**Channel Divinity (Level 3)**
2 uses, recharging on a Short or Long Rest. The specific Channel Divinity options depend on your subclass (Oath). All Paladins gain access to their subclass's Channel Divinity options here.

**Subclass (Level 3)**
You swear a Sacred Oath, gaining subclass features at levels 3, 7, 15, and 20. The SRD subclass is **Oath of Devotion**.

**Extra Attack (Level 5)**
You can attack twice whenever you take the Attack action.

**Faithful Steed (Level 5)**
You can cast *Find Steed* without expending a spell slot, and it doesn't count against your prepared spells. You can do this once per Long Rest, or by expending a spell slot.

**Aura of Protection (Level 6)**
While you're conscious, you and friendly creatures within 10 feet add your CHA modifier (minimum +1) to all saving throws.

**Abjure Foes (Level 9)**
As an action, you can expend one use of Channel Divinity to terrify your foes. Each creature you can see within 60 feet must make a WIS saving throw or be Frightened of you for 1 minute, with repeated saves at end of each of their turns.

**Aura of Courage (Level 10)**
While you're conscious, you and friendly creatures within 10 feet are immune to the Frightened condition.

**Radiant Strikes (Level 11)**
Your weapon and unarmed strikes deal an extra 1d8 radiant damage on a hit.

**Restoring Touch (Level 14)**
When you use Lay On Hands to restore HP to a creature, you can also remove one of the following conditions from them: Blinded, Charmed, Deafened, Frightened, Paralyzed, or Stunned. No extra HP cost required.

**Aura Expansion (Level 18)**
Your Aura of Protection and Aura of Courage now extend to 30 feet (up from 10 feet).

## SRD Subclass: Oath of Devotion

- **Sacred Weapon (Level 3 Channel Divinity):** As a Bonus Action, imbue one weapon with positive energy. For 1 minute, add CHA modifier to attack rolls with it; it emits bright light 20 ft and dim light 20 ft further; it deals radiant damage instead of its normal type on a hit; it counts as magical for overcoming resistance/immunity.
- **Holy Nimbus (Level 20):** As an action, you emanate an aura of sunlight. For 1 minute, bright light fills a 30-foot radius around you; enemies in that radius take 10 radiant damage when they start their turn there; you have Advantage on saving throws against spells cast by Fiends or Undead.

## Notes for Play

- The **Aura of Protection** is one of the strongest features in the game at level 6 — a high-CHA Paladin can give the whole party +4 or +5 to every saving throw.
- **Paladin's Smite** no longer requires you to decide before rolling (2024 rules clarification) — you trigger it after you know the hit lands.
- As a half caster, Paladin spell slots are precious. Consider saving higher slots for smites in critical moments rather than spending them on utility spells.
- **Lay On Hands** scales with level (5 × level HP pool) and doubles as a disease/poison cure — don't overlook its utility function.
