# Paladin — D&D 5e 2024 Class Features

**Hit Die:** d10
**Primary Ability:** STR + CHA
**Saving Throws:** WIS, CHA
**Skills (choose 2):** Athletics, Insight, Intimidation, Medicine, Persuasion, Religion
**Armor Proficiencies:** Light, Medium, Heavy, Shields
**Weapon Proficiencies:** Simple + Martial
**Spellcasting:** Half caster (spell slots up to 5th level)
**Subclass:** Gained at level 3

## Feature Descriptions

### Level 1

**Lay On Hands**
A healing pool of HP equal to 5 × your Paladin level, refreshed on a Long Rest. You can spend points from the pool to restore HP to a creature you touch, or spend 5 points to cure one disease or neutralize one poison. (No spell slot cost.)

**Spellcasting**
Paladins are half casters — you prepare spells from the Paladin list using CHA as your spellcasting modifier. Spell slots top out at 5th level. See the half-caster slot table below.

**Weapon Mastery**
Gain the Mastery property of weapons you are proficient with. The number of weapons you can apply Mastery to increases as you level.

---

### Level 2

**Fighting Style**
Choose one Fighting Style feat (e.g., Defense, Dueling, Great Weapon Fighting, Protection, etc.).

**Paladin's Smite**
The 2024 revision integrates Divine Smite directly: when you hit with a melee weapon attack, you can expend a spell slot to deal radiant damage. 1st-level slot = +2d8 radiant; each slot level above 1st adds +1d8 (max +5d8 at 4th+). The smite is now a reaction or part of the attack, clarifying that it is optional after seeing the hit land.

---

### Level 3

**Channel Divinity (2/rest)**
Gain Channel Divinity uses, refreshing on a Short or Long Rest. The specific Channel Divinity options come from your subclass.

**Subclass (Sacred Oath)**
Choose your Sacred Oath at level 3. The SRD includes the Oath of Devotion. Subclasses provide:
- Additional Channel Divinity options
- Oath Spells (always prepared, don't count against your limit)
- Higher-level features at levels 7, 15, and 20

---

### Level 5

**Extra Attack**
Attack twice whenever you take the Attack action on your turn.

**Faithful Steed**
You can use *Find Steed* as a ritual (no spell slot required), and the steed benefits from your auras. The steed returns if it dies once per Long Rest without spending a spell slot.

---

### Level 6

**Aura of Protection**
One of the strongest class features in the game: while you are conscious, you and friendly creatures within 10 ft add your CHA modifier (minimum +1) to all saving throws. Scales with your Charisma.

---

### Level 9

**Abjure Foes**
Use a Channel Divinity option to frighten and potentially incapacitate a number of creatures equal to your CHA modifier (minimum 1) within 60 ft. Creatures that fail the WIS save are Frightened for 1 minute; undead/fiends that fail are also Incapacitated.

---

### Level 10

**Aura of Courage**
You and allies within 10 ft are immune to the Frightened condition while you are conscious.

---

### Level 11

**Radiant Strikes**
Your melee weapon attacks deal an additional 1d8 radiant damage on a hit, no spell slot required.

---

### Level 14

**Restoring Touch**
When you use Lay On Hands to restore HP, you can also remove one condition from the target: Blinded, Charmed, Deafened, Frightened, Paralyzed, or Stunned. This costs no additional HP from your pool.

---

### Level 18

**Aura Expansion**
Your Aura of Protection and Aura of Courage expand from 10 ft to 30 ft radius.

## SRD Subclass: Oath of Devotion

Gain Oath Spells (always prepared) themed around protection and holy power.

**Channel Divinity Options:**
- **Sacred Weapon** — As a Bonus Action, imbue one weapon with holy power for 1 minute: add CHA to attack rolls, weapon is magical and emits bright light (20 ft) and dim light (20 ft beyond that).
- **Holy Nimbus** (higher level) — Radiate sunlight; enemies that start their turn within 30 ft take radiant damage; advantage on saves vs. spells from fiends/undead.

---

## Key Design Notes (2024 vs. 2014)

- **Paladin's Smite** is now codified as a reaction-triggered ability when you hit, rather than a free-floating option — this prevents mid-roll ambiguity.
- **Lay On Hands** is unchanged in core function but integrates with Restoring Touch at level 14.
- **Auras still cap at 10 ft** until level 18 (same as 2014).
- **Faithful Steed** replaces the old "bonus spells" and gives free ritual access to *Find Steed*.
- The Paladin remains one of the strongest multiclass bases due to Aura of Protection and Lay On Hands being largely unchanged.
