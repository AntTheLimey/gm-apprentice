# MIRA VOSS
Cat Burglar — 150 points

**Concept:** A freelance second-story thief who specialises in bypassing
physical security — locks, alarms, and high-altitude approaches. Hired
for clean jobs with no violence; gets out before anyone knows she was
there. Keeps no records and trusts almost nobody.

**Assumptions:** TL8 (modern), Basic Set only, realistic (not cinematic),
disadvantage limit −50 pts, quirk limit −5 pts.

  effective rating of 18 for those situations.

- Guns (Pistol) 14 and Knife 14 are adequate self-defense without
  over-investing in combat. She avoids fights; when cornered she shoots
  and runs.

- At short range (≤10 yds, no Aim): effective skill 14. Shoots and runs.
- Fast-Draw (Pistol) 14 — draw as free action.

**Melee backup:** Large Knife
- Knife 14; thrust: thr imp (1d−2 imp); swing: sw−2 cut (1d−2 cut)
- Parry: 10 (Knife 14/2 + 3)

**Active defenses:**
- Dodge 9 (no encumbrance on a job)
- Acrobatics roll for +2 Dodge during chases and escapes

**Combat philosophy:** Mira does not fight. If spotted she bolts —
Stealth 15 for a shadow disappearance, Climbing 16 to go vertical,
Acrobatics for +2 Dodge while sprinting. The pistol is for when someone
physically blocks her exit. She will not shoot a guard who hasn't
drawn on her. Code of Honor stops her shooting first.

## GM Hooks

- **The Job She Can't Refuse:** Her Greed (CR 12) triggers when a
  client offers significantly above market rate — which usually means
  the target is much more dangerous than advertised.
- **Interpol's Long Arm:** Her Secret is someone's investigative
  project. When she starts surfacing in the same city as a high-profile
  theft, old dossiers get dusted off.
- **The One She Left Behind:** Code of Honor (Professional) means she
  has never harmed a civilian — but a past job went wrong and someone
  got hurt indirectly. She believes it wasn't her fault. The family
  disagrees.
- **Overconfidence Failure:** She took the contract because she'd done
  the same building type before. She hadn't read that the security
  was upgraded six months ago.
