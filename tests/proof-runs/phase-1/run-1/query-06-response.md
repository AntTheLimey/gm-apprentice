**Hit Die:** d10
**Primary Abilities:** STR + CHA

### Feature Summaries

- **Lay On Hands** — Pool of HP equal to 5 × your Paladin level. As an action, restore any amount from the pool to a creature you touch. Also cures disease/poison (5 HP per condition).
- **Spellcasting** — Half-caster; you prepare spells from the Paladin list. Spell slots follow the half-caster table (up to 5th level).
- **Weapon Mastery** — Apply a Mastery property to weapons you're proficient with.
- **Fighting Style** (Level 2) — Choose a Fighting Style option (e.g., Defense, Dueling, Great Weapon Fighting, Protection, Blessed Warrior, Blind Fighting, Interception).
- **Paladin's Smite** (Level 2) — When you hit with a melee attack, you can expend a spell slot as a reaction to deal radiant damage: 2d8 + 1d8 per spell level above 1st. Undead/fiends take extra d8.
- **Channel Divinity** (Level 3) — 2 uses per day, recharge on Short or Long Rest. Specific options depend on your Oath (subclass).
- **Extra Attack** (Level 5) — Attack twice when you take the Attack action.
- **Faithful Steed** (Level 5) — Summon a spirit steed (Warhorse or Pony equivalent) as a 1-hour ritual. It shares your Aura of Protection.
- **Aura of Protection** (Level 6) — You and friendly creatures within 10 ft add your CHA modifier (minimum +1) to all saving throws while you are conscious.
- **Abjure Foes** (Level 9) — Channel Divinity option: as an action, attempt to frighten/abjure a number of creatures equal to your CHA modifier (minimum 1); they're Frightened and their speed drops to 0 until end of your next turn (save negates).
- **Aura of Courage** (Level 10) — You and friendly creatures within 10 ft are immune to the Frightened condition while you are conscious.
- **Radiant Strikes** (Level 11) — Your melee weapon attacks deal an extra 1d8 radiant damage on a hit.
- **Restoring Touch** (Level 14) — When you use Lay On Hands, you can also remove one condition (Blinded, Deafened, Paralyzed, Poisoned, or Stunned) from the target.
- **Aura Expansion** (Level 18) — Aura of Protection and Aura of Courage expand from 10 ft to 30 ft.

### SRD Subclass: Oath of Devotion (Level 3)

The only subclass in SRD 5.2:

- **Sacred Weapon** — Channel Divinity: weapon glows, gains +CHA bonus to attack rolls, sheds light (20 ft bright, 20 ft dim), 1 minute.
- **Holy Nimbus** (Level 20) — Once per Long Rest, radiant aura deals 10 radiant to enemies who start their turn within 30 ft; you have advantage on saves against spells from fiends and undead.

Additional Oath spells (from the Paladin spell list) are granted automatically at Levels 3, 5, 7, 9, and 11 — not counted against your prepared limit.

> **Note:** The Paladin's Aura of Protection is one of the strongest class features in the game. A CHA-heavy Paladin (CHA 20 = +5) gives every ally within 10 ft a +5 to all saving throws — invaluable against spells and breath weapons.
