## Feature Descriptions

**Lay On Hands (Level 1):** A pool of healing equal to 5 × your Paladin level. As an action, restore any number of HP from the pool to a creature you touch. Can also expend 5 HP from the pool to cure one disease or neutralize one poison affecting the target.

**Spellcasting (Level 1):** Half-caster using CHA as the spellcasting ability. Spell slots recharge on a Long Rest. Spells are prepared from the Paladin spell list (number shown in table above).

**Weapon Mastery (Level 1):** You can use the Mastery property of two weapons you are proficient with.

**Fighting Style (Level 2):** Choose one fighting style (options include Defense, Dueling, Great Weapon Fighting, Protection, etc.).

**Paladin's Smite (Level 2):** When you hit with a melee or ranged weapon, you can expend a spell slot to deal extra radiant damage (2d8 for a 1st-level slot, +1d8 per slot level above 1st; +1d8 extra if the target is an Undead or Fiend). This replaces the older Divine Smite — it is now a feature rather than a class-wide option.

**Channel Divinity (Level 3):** Gained alongside your Oath subclass at level 3. Two uses per Short or Long Rest (rising to 3 at level 11). The specific Channel Divinity options depend on your Oath.

**Subclass — Oath (Level 3):** All Paladin subclasses (Oaths) are chosen at level 3. The SRD subclass is the **Oath of Devotion**, which provides:
- *Sacred Weapon* — Channel Divinity: imbue a weapon with holy energy, adding CHA to attack rolls for 1 minute.
- *Holy Nimbus* — Channel Divinity: emit bright light and deal radiant damage to enemies that start their turn near you.

**Extra Attack (Level 5):** Attack twice instead of once when you take the Attack action.

**Faithful Steed (Level 5):** You can cast *Find Steed* without expending a spell slot. The steed also gains certain benefits from your Paladin features.

**Aura of Protection (Level 6):** You and friendly creatures within 10 ft add your CHA modifier (minimum +1) to all saving throws while you are conscious. One of the strongest class features in the game at any level.

**Abjure Foes (Level 9):** Channel Divinity option to frighten and potentially incapacitate enemies.

**Aura of Courage (Level 10):** You and friendly creatures within 10 ft cannot be Frightened while you are conscious.

**Radiant Strikes (Level 11):** Your weapon attacks deal an additional 1d8 radiant damage on each hit.

**Restoring Touch (Level 14):** When you use Lay On Hands on a creature, you can also remove one of the following conditions: Blinded, Charmed, Deafened, Frightened, Paralyzed, or Stunned.

**Aura Expansion (Level 18):** Aura of Protection and Aura of Courage expand from 10 ft to 30 ft radius.

## GM Notes

The 2024 Paladin is a significant upgrade over the 2014 version. Key mechanical changes to note:

- **Paladin's Smite is now a feature, not an implied rule.** It works on ranged attacks as well as melee, and triggering it is a free action (not a spell slot expenditure mid-attack-roll resolution). It is no longer possible to "fish" for crits and then decide to smite — the decision is made before the attack.
- **Aura of Protection** still functions identically and remains one of the most impactful party-support features in the game. A CHA 20 Paladin at level 6 is adding +5 to every saving throw for nearby allies.
- **Radiant Strikes at level 11** gives the Paladin consistent extra damage without burning spell slots, making them less feast-or-famine at higher tiers.
- **Aura Expansion at level 18** makes the Paladin dramatically more valuable in large-scale encounters or spaced-out dungeon rooms.
