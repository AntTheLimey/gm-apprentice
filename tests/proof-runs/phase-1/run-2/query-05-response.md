## Character Notes

**Who is she?** Mara Kessler grew up in a touring circus family
(explains Acrobatics and Climbing). When the troupe dissolved, she
discovered her skills were worth considerably more to private collectors
than to ticket-buyers. She has never worked for anyone who told her
what was inside a target — she has her own rules about that.

**What drives her?** Greed is the engine, but Overconfidence is
what will eventually get her caught. She will take a job she rates
as "straightforward" and discover on entry that the intelligence was
wrong. Her Code of Honor (Professional) is the one thing that keeps
her from sliding into uglier work.

**GM hooks:**
- Her fence has started asking questions about specific collections — is
  he working for someone, or has he been flipped by law enforcement?
- The "last big job and retire" score she keeps promising herself keeps
  inflating. The apartment she's casing belongs to someone more dangerous
  than she knows.
- A rival burglar has been leaving evidence at scenes that points to Mara.

**Advancement priorities (earned XP):**
1. DX 15 (20 pts) — the single biggest mechanical upgrade
2. Per 14 (5 pts) — better trap and guard detection
3. Climbing to 16 (2 pts) — for more demanding ascents
4. Electronics Op (Security) to 13 (3 pts) — handles modern systems
