# Coded Letter

## Overview
**Type:** Document — encoded letter written by [[Professor Ashford]]
**Found:** Session 1, in the false bottom of Ashford's desk at [[Ashford Study]]
**Found by:** Mal Bridges (Spot Hidden success)
**Decoded:** Session 2, by Rev. Silas Marsh (Library Use success)
**Status:** Decoded and in investigators' possession

## Physical Description
A single sheet of heavy cream-colored stationery, folded in thirds. One side contains lines of apparent gibberish — letters grouped in blocks of five, the hallmark of a Vigenere cipher. The other side bears a hand-drawn map of the Kingsport waterfront with Warehouse 7 circled in red ink and the notation "17th & 20th" in Ashford's handwriting.

The paper smells faintly of pipe tobacco and ink. The handwriting is recognizably Ashford's, though less steady than his earlier notes — the hand that wrote this was shaking.

## Decoded Text

> *"The deliveries continue on the 17th and 20th. Warehouse 7. They do not know I have seen the inner sanctum — the chamber beneath the old church where they keep the greater texts. I must retrieve the Revelations before the new moon. If I fail, what they intend to summon will not be confined to the angles between. God help us all. — H.A."*

**Cipher type:** Vigenere
**Key word:** GLAAKI (identified through research into the manuscript Ashford was translating)

## Information Revealed
1. **Dock deliveries** — Cargo arrives at Warehouse 7 on specific dates (confirmed in Session 2).
2. **The inner sanctum** — An underground chamber beneath "the old church" where the cult keeps forbidden texts.
3. **The Revelations of Glaaki** — The complete version of the text Ashford was translating. The cult possesses it.
4. **The summoning** — The cult intends to summon something before the new moon (October 20).
5. **Ashford's plan** — He intended to steal the Revelations to prevent the summoning. He presumably failed and was captured.

## Connections
- Points to [[Kingsport Docks]] and Warehouse 7 (confirmed in Session 2)
- References the [[Order of the Silver Twilight]]'s inner sanctum beneath "the old church"
- Written by [[Professor Ashford]] as insurance in case of his capture
- The cipher key (GLAAKI) connects to the manuscript that started Ashford's obsession

## GM Notes
This is the central clue of the campaign — the thread that connects the disappearance to the docks to the cult's hideout. The decoded letter is the investigators' primary lead for finding the inner sanctum. The reference to "the old church" is deliberately vague to require further investigation, but it should ultimately point them to the cult's hidden chambers.

The letter also establishes the stakes and the deadline: something will be summoned on the new moon, and Ashford tried to stop it and failed. The investigators are now the last line of defense.
