# Strange Symbol

## Overview
**Type:** Recurring occult symbol — visual motif appearing across multiple locations
**First Found:** Session 1, in [[Professor Ashford]]'s notes at [[Ashford Study]]
**Also Found:** Session 2, painted on crates at [[Kingsport Docks]]
**Status:** Recognized as connected to the [[Order of the Silver Twilight]], not fully understood

## Description
A stylized eye enclosed within a crescent moon, rendered in a single continuous line. The pupil of the eye is drawn as a vertical slit rather than a circle. Fine radiating lines extend from the crescent's points, suggesting either rays of light or tentacles — the ambiguity appears deliberate.

The symbol has been found in several contexts:
- Sketched dozens of times in Ashford's handwriting on loose papers in his study
- Scratched into the margins of the Revelations of Glaaki manuscript
- Painted in red on the lids of cargo crates at Dock 7
- Carved into the stone tablets recovered from the docks (Session 2)

## Analysis
**Rev. Silas Marsh** (Occult check, Session 1): The symbol does not correspond to any standard occult tradition Marsh is familiar with. It is not Hermetic, Kabbalistic, Rosicrucian, or Masonic. It may be older than any of those traditions. The vertical pupil is disturbing — it suggests the eye belongs to something not human.

**Dr. Voss** (noting the pattern): The symbol's repetition in Ashford's notes has a compulsive quality, like automatic writing. He may have been copying it unconsciously or under some form of influence.

## Information Revealed
1. **Cult identification:** The symbol marks items, locations, and documents connected to the [[Order of the Silver Twilight]]. It is their signature.
2. **Non-human origin:** The symbol appears on stone tablets in a script that predates known languages, suggesting the cult is working with knowledge that is not originally human.
3. **Ashford's obsession:** The professor was drawing the symbol compulsively, suggesting psychological influence from the manuscript or the cult.

## Connections
- Found at [[Ashford Study]] (Session 1) — in Ashford's notes
- Found at [[Kingsport Docks]] (Session 2) — on cargo crates and stone tablets
- Connected to the [[Order of the Silver Twilight]] as their identifying mark
- [[Professor Ashford]] drew it compulsively, suggesting psychological influence
- [[Mrs Whitmore]] found a silver medallion bearing the symbol under Ashford's pillow

## GM Notes
The Strange Symbol serves as a connective thread linking locations and evidence to the Order. It confirms the cult's involvement whenever it appears but does not by itself reveal the location of the inner sanctum or the specifics of the cult's plan. It is evidence of the conspiracy's existence and scope, not a directional clue.

If the investigators research the symbol at the Miskatonic library (Occult or Library Use roll), they can learn that similar symbols have appeared in accounts of pre-colonial structures found along the New England coast — always associated with underground chambers and "gates." This is flavor, not a direct lead to the church.
