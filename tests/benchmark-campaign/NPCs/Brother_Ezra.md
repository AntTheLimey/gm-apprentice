# Brother Ezra

## Overview
**Role:** Primary antagonist — cult enforcer for the Order of the Silver Twilight.
**Age:** Unknown (appears mid-40s, but something is wrong)
**Occupation:** Enforcer/operative for the [[Order of the Silver Twilight]]
**Status:** Active and aware of the investigators

## Physical Description
Extremely tall — six foot four at least — and gaunt to the point of emaciation. His face is long and narrow with prominent cheekbones and a sharp jaw. His skin is sallow, almost gray in certain lights. He wears a dark wool coat that hangs loosely on his frame, a black wide-brimmed hat, and leather gloves. His most disturbing feature, noticed by Dr. Voss during the dock chase, is his eyes: the pupils are elongated vertically, like a cat's or a serpent's. He moves with an unsettling, mechanical precision that suggests his body doesn't quite work the way a normal human's does.

## Personality
Brother Ezra speaks rarely and when he does, his voice is flat and atonal, as if he is reciting words he has memorized rather than forming thoughts. He is utterly devoted to the Order's goals and shows no hesitation, mercy, or doubt. Whether this is fanaticism or something else is unclear.

He is intelligent and methodical. The dock pursuit showed tactical thinking — he tried to cut off escape routes and herd the investigators rather than simply chase them. He broke off the pursuit when the risk of exposure outweighed the benefit of capture.

## Background
The man known as Brother Ezra has been associated with the [[Order of the Silver Twilight]] for at least a decade, according to fragmentary records. Before that, nothing. He has no known address, no employment history, no family connections. He appeared in Kingsport around 1912 and has been a fixture of the Order ever since.

The truth (known only to the Order's inner circle): Ezra is no longer entirely human. A ritual performed in 1912 bound something from beyond to his flesh, giving him enhanced physical abilities at the cost of his humanity. He is the Order's enforcer, guard, and when necessary, executioner.

## Motivations
- Serve the [[Order of the Silver Twilight]] without question.
- Protect the inner sanctum beneath the church.
- Ensure the ritual proceeds on the night of the new moon.
- Eliminate anyone who threatens the Order's plans.

## Secrets
- Brother Ezra is the one who abducted [[Professor Ashford]] from his study on October 12th.
- He has killed before — at least two people who got too close to the Order's secrets. Their deaths were ruled accidents.
- His enhanced physical abilities (speed, strength, perception) come from the entity bound to his flesh. They are real, not hallucinations. Investigators who witness them lose SAN.
- He has a weakness: the binding ritual must be renewed annually. His next renewal is due November 1st. If the investigators can delay or disrupt, his powers will begin to fade.

## Connections
- [[Order of the Silver Twilight]] — His masters. He answers to the Order's leader (the "Grand Master," not yet named in play).
- [[Professor Ashford]] — His prisoner. He visited Ashford the night before the abduction to deliver a final warning.
- [[Mrs Whitmore]] — She saw him on October 11th and can identify his voice.
- The investigators — He has seen their faces and will recognize them.

## GM Notes
Brother Ezra should be genuinely frightening. He is not a mustache-twirling villain — he is something alien wearing a human shape. His presence should make scenes tense. He doesn't monologue; he acts. If the investigators confront him, he fights efficiently and without emotion.

In the final session, he is the primary physical threat. He can be defeated through clever tactics, but a straight fight will be very costly for the investigators. Environmental advantages (collapsing tunnels, fire, trapping him in the ritual circle) are more effective than bullets.
