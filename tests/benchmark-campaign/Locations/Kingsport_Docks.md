# Kingsport Docks

## Overview
**Type:** Exterior — commercial waterfront district
**Location:** Eastern shore of Kingsport harbor
**Associated NPCs:** [[Brother Ezra]], Dockmaster Hayes (not yet interviewed)
**Current State:** Active commercial port with suspicious nighttime activity

## Description

The Kingsport docks stretch for a quarter mile along the harbor's eastern edge, a working waterfront of weathered timber piers, stone quays, and corrugated iron warehouses. During the day, it is a place of honest labor — fishing boats unloading their catch, freighters taking on cargo, the shouts of longshoremen and the screech of gulls.

At night, it transforms. The fog rolls in from the harbor thick enough to muffle sound and reduce visibility to a few yards. The gas lamps along the quay create pools of amber light separated by deep darkness. The warehouses become looming shapes. The water slaps against the pilings with a hollow, rhythmic sound. It is a place where things can arrive and depart unseen.

### Key Areas

**Dock 7:** The focus of the investigation. A stone-and-timber pier extending forty feet into the harbor, wide enough for a small launch to moor alongside. The warehouse behind it (Warehouse 7) is leased to a company called "Atlantic Maritime Imports" — a front for the [[Order of the Silver Twilight]]. The warehouse is padlocked when not in use. Inside: mostly empty, with some old crates and the faint chemical smell noted at [[Ashford Study]].

**The Alleyways:** Narrow passages between the warehouses, cluttered with rope coils, barrel staves, and discarded netting. These were the site of the chase with [[Brother Ezra]] in Session 2. The footing is treacherous — wet cobblestones and patches of fish oil.

**The Harbormaster's Office:** A small clapboard building at the north end of the docks. Dockmaster Hayes works here. The office contains shipping logs that could reveal the frequency and origin of deliveries to Warehouse 7.

**The Seawall:** A low stone wall at the end of the quay, overlooking the open harbor. On clear nights, you can see the lighthouse on the point. On foggy nights, you can see nothing at all.

## Sensory Details
- **Sight:** Fog. Amber lamplight. The dark shapes of warehouses. Masts swaying against the sky.
- **Sound:** Water against pilings. Foghorns in the distance. The creak of rope and timber. A buoy bell somewhere out in the harbor.
- **Smell:** Salt, fish, tar, engine oil, and wet rope. Near Warehouse 7, that faint chemical undertone.
- **Feel:** Cold. The fog leaves a film of moisture on everything — skin, clothes, metal.

## Events Here
- **Session 2:** Investigators staked out Dock 7, observed a delivery, encountered [[Brother Ezra]], survived a chase through the alleyways, recovered two stone tablets marked with the [[Strange Symbol]] from abandoned crates.
