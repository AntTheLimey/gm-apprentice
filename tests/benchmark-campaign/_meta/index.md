# The Ashford Case — Campaign Index

A Call of Cthulhu 7th Edition scenario for 3-4 investigators, set in Kingsport, Massachusetts during the autumn of 1923.

## Overview

Professor Henry Ashford, a respected antiquarian at Miskatonic University, has gone missing under mysterious circumstances. His colleagues and family are desperate for answers, and the local police are out of their depth. The investigators are drawn into a web of occult conspiracy that leads from the professor's ransacked study to the fog-shrouded docks of Kingsport, and finally into the hidden sanctum beneath an old church.
