# Player Characters

## Dr. Eleanor Voss
- **Player:** Sarah
- **Occupation:** Physician
- **Age:** 34
- **Key Skills:** Medicine (65%), First Aid (55%), Psychology (50%), Library Use (60%)
- **Background:** A doctor at St. Mary's Hospital in Arkham. She knew Professor Ashford socially — he was a patient of hers, and she noticed his declining health in the weeks before his disappearance. Fiercely rational, she is struggling with the impossible things she's seen.
- **Connections:** Treated Ashford for insomnia in September. Has access to hospital records. Knows [[Inspector Crane]] from a previous coroner's case.
- **Current Status:** Shaken by the chase at the docks (Session 2). Lost 3 SAN from witnessing Brother Ezra's unnatural speed.

## Malcolm "Mal" Bridges
- **Player:** Tom
- **Occupation:** Private Investigator
- **Age:** 41
- **Key Skills:** Spot Hidden (70%), Stealth (55%), Firearms (Handgun) (60%), Fast Talk (50%)
- **Background:** A former Pinkerton man, now freelance. Hired by Ashford's sister to find the professor. Cynical and methodical, he trusts evidence over intuition. Carries a .38 revolver and a notebook full of observations.
- **Connections:** Has contacts in the Kingsport underworld. Knows [[Tommy Flanagan]] from a previous case.
- **Current Status:** Took a minor wound during the dock chase (Session 2). Suspicious of Inspector Crane's slow progress on the case.

## Reverend Silas Marsh
- **Player:** Jamie
- **Occupation:** Clergy (Episcopal)
- **Age:** 52
- **Key Skills:** Persuade (65%), History (55%), Occult (45%), Library Use (50%)
- **Background:** Pastor of a small church in Arkham. An old friend of Ashford's from their university days. Deeply troubled by the occult elements emerging in the investigation. His faith is being tested.
- **Connections:** Knows the history of local churches, including rumors about older structures beneath them. Has access to church records and the diocesan archive.
- **Current Status:** Becoming increasingly convinced that something genuinely supernatural is at work. Gained 1 Cthulhu Mythos from examining the [[Strange Symbol]].
