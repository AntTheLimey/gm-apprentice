# The Ashford Case — Campaign Timeline

## Background Events (Before Play)

### September 1923
- **Sep 5** — Professor Ashford acquires a collection of medieval manuscripts from an estate sale in Salem. Among them is a Latin text he calls "the Revelations of Glaaki" — a fragmentary copy.
- **Sep 12** — Ashford begins translating the manuscript. Notes in his journal mention "extraordinary claims" and "geometries that defy Euclid."
- **Sep 20** — Ashford contacts [[Brother Ezra]] through intermediaries, seeking information about a symbol found in the manuscript.
- **Sep 28** — Ashford attends a private gathering at the Silver Twilight Lodge. He is introduced to several members of the [[Order of the Silver Twilight]].

### October 1923
- **Oct 3** — Ashford's journal entries become erratic. He writes about "doors between the angles" and "the sound beneath the church."
- **Oct 8** — [[Mrs Whitmore]] notices Ashford hasn't been sleeping. He has moved a cot into his study and refuses meals.
- **Oct 12** — **Ashford disappears.** Mrs Whitmore arrives at the house to find the study ransacked, papers scattered, and the professor gone. She contacts the police.
- **Oct 13** — [[Inspector Crane]] is assigned to the case. Initial investigation finds no signs of forced entry.
- **Oct 14** — Investigators are contacted by Ashford's colleague at Miskatonic and asked to look into the disappearance. **Session 1 begins.**
- **Oct 15** — Investigators visit [[Ashford Study]], interview [[Mrs Whitmore]], meet [[Tommy Flanagan]] at the Red Hook tavern. They discover the [[Coded Letter]] hidden in a false drawer.
- **Oct 16** — Professor Ashford seen giving a lecture at the university on "Mediaeval Symbolism in New England Architecture." Several students confirm attendance.
- **Oct 17** — Investigators head to [[Kingsport Docks]] following a lead from the [[Coded Letter]]. **Session 2 begins.** They encounter [[Brother Ezra]] near warehouse 7. Chase scene ensues. Investigators find crates marked with the [[Strange Symbol]].

## Future Events (GM Notes — Not Yet In Play)
- The Silver Twilight's ritual is scheduled for the night of October 20 (the new moon).
- If not stopped, the ritual will open a gate beneath the church, with catastrophic consequences for Kingsport.
