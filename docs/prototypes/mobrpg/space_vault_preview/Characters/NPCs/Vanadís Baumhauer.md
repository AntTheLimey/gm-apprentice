## Overview

A resident of Processing I, Vanadís grew up on the station. Her mother long since disappeared, and her father spends most of his time off station working for whoever will take him. In the meantime Vanadís grew up in the underclass of the station, learning tricks and scams to survive. Holger Graeme's arrested her a few times, but she always has a bit of information to trade, something worth more than the fine or jail time she is facing. Opeyemi's banned her from his businesses, as well as told his crews to steer clear of her, having his operations effected by her information trades. For the right price, Vanadís can get you any piece of information you need.

You can usually find her on the Main Line of Processing I, working angles, trading information, and making deals.
