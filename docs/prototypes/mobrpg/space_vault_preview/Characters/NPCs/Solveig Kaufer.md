## Overview

Assistant to the Station Manager of MacMillian IV, she is the main person running the station as her boss Wilton offloaded his work on her to add to her work total. Because of this overt trust by Wilton, Solveig is given a free hand running the station. While she might be the ultimate power of the station, she has gone out of her way to run the station efficiently and fairly. All actions appear reasoned and justified, and bringing no rebuke from corporate or the feds.
