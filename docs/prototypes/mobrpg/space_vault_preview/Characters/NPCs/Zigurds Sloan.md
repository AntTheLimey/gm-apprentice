## Overview

Ziguads is the man to find if you have a mechanical problem on the station. He knows most of the inner working of the station, and it's equipment, as well as some other things. He's become jaded over the years due to what he perceives as a lack of respect of his talents by the powers that be at the MacMillian corporation. As such he's been known to use his knowledge of the station to line his pockets and to do off book work for the right price. Security won't touch him as he's the only man keeping things running around here.

He's been in a relationship with Solveig Kaufer for the last year,

He is a degenerate gambler and can be found often during his time off in some of the entertainment district establishments that offer gambling. It's suspected he's in debt to some of the establishments, and even some of the less reputable residents of the station.
