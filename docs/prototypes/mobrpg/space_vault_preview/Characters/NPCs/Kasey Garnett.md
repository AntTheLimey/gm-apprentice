## Overview

Owner and chief of Garnett's Reach, she runs the largest illegal outpost in the Thides System. Space in her outpost is rented to different individuals to conduct their own operations. There is one rule, no strangers at the outpost. Because of this most of those operating out of the reach act only as middle men in transactions, arranging the exchange of goods between to parties. This can be inter and intra system deals.

Another offer by Kasey is her ability to acquire the swarm loading key codes. Allowing people to hide shipments arriving/leaving the system in the swarm load. She offers this access for a few, if you can get in contact with her.
