## Overview

Owner and operator of Stamp Supply, he's managed to corner the market in supplying most of those still operating in the Thides System by either muscling out the competition or the disinterest in business on a failing station. He is known as the biggest fence in the station, and will usually buy anything of value. Station security has yet to figure out how he manages to get the items off station, but believes he might have an agreement with Opeyemi to facilitate this.

While his connections allow him to move items off station and out of the system, he lacks the ability to smuggle items in. It is unknown if this is due to some inability, or a lack of desire to conduct that sort of business.
