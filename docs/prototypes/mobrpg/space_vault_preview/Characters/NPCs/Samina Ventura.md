## Overview

A retired prostitute that once worked in some of the highest class pleasure houses in the Federal Republic, Samina took her life savings and bought The Silk Abbey on MacMillian Station IV. She attempted to raise the quality, and the class, but there is only so much to do with the station in the state it is. She manages to attract the best girls on the station, with a higher level of cliental, and fairer operating fees.

She maintains a special relation with the Station Manager, and this allows her to quietly offer more risky services for more income. This includes looking the other ways as the dealers ply their drugs in the establishment, provided she gets a kickback. Station security is on to her, but as long as the illegal activities are confined to The Silk Abbey, Samina has nothing to worry about as security has other bigger problems.
