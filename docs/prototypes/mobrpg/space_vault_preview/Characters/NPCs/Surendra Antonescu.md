## Overview

A priest of the Orthodox faith that services the needy on MacMillian Station IV. He's taken to multiple acts to being the end to the suffering of the downtrodden of the station. While he will maintain the formal aspects of his position by holding regular mass, and offering the other required sacraments, most of the time is spent on missions of service throughout the station.

Rumor is some action in his past lead to his assignment to this bleak outpost. His actions back this up, making many that know of the faith to believe he is seeking redemption for some past action. Before his arrival only the occasional passing priest of the Orthodox faith would pass through the system, usually at the request of some resident for a specific reason (last rights, marriage, burial, etc.
