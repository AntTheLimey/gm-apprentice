## Overview

Pia is a small time reporter and content distributor in the Thides System. She reports on the local news as best she can, while trying to find a big story that will get her recognized by the bigger networks. She once worked as an intern for FRNC, but left after her opportunities for promotion dried up, at least that is the story she tells. A few years ago she came to Thides and setup her operation, more that she just took over the local abandoned setup that the company ignored for decades.
