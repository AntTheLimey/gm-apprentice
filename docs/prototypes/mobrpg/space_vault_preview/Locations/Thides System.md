## Overview

A dead end system with one jump route charted in/out of the system. It is primarily owned by the MacMillian Mining and Extraction Corporation for purposes of exploiting any and all resources found in the system. The MacMillian operates the primary processing, shipping, and management station in the system. All current exploiting operations are managed by subcontractors to MacMillian. MacMillian maintains control by managing travel to/from the system.
