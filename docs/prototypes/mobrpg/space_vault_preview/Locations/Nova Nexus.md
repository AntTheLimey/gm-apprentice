## Overview

A gambling establishment and nightclub located on MacMillian Station IV. It is owned by the biggest known criminal boss in the sector, Opeyemi Tichá. While intended to be a front operation, it's surprise popularity on the station makes it as valueable as any other of Opeyemi's enterprises.While considered upscale, its just as dirty as any other establishment on the station, this just has music, neon, and a dance floor.
