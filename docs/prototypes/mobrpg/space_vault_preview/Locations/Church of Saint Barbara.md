## Overview

A chapel located on the MacMillian Station IV, it is a small space built out of a former residence. The chapel is a place of refuge for members of the Orthodox religion. Setup by Surendra Antonescu after his arrival at the station a decade ago, it acts as the central hub for his outreach. While he will preform masses in the chapel as required, or requested, there are few regular attendees.
