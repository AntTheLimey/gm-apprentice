## Overview

One of the last mining operation on Thides I that operated until the planet almost reached planetary collapse. The operation was abandoned by the MacMillian Company and left to rot, without maintance the solar winds, heat, and radiation began to break down the remains. Some of the facilities would be operational with a bit of effort, and some remain still pressure sealed and safe to inhabit. Scavengers will occasionally seek out the location to harvest for supplies and equipment left behind.
