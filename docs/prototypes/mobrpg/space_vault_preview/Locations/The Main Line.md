## Overview

The one time main path of the processing facility, it has become a sort of main street to the slums of MacMillian Station IV. Over the years people carved out places for stores along what used to be conveyer belts carrying the ore to be processed by the plant. You can find a mix of people selling legitimate or illegitimate product along its length.

While it's not a place to find the more formal members of society, those people that scrape out survival on the station it's their home. Some of those on the station, as well as those from the nearby system, will frequently visit the Main Line looking for a deal.
