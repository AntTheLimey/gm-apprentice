## Overview

An upper medium tier startship created by Horizon Aerospace. It is designed to land on planets with an atmosphere. These are used by companies that want to provide a more luxurious experience than the bear bones offered by other starships. While they are not luxury yachts, they do contain more features than some more limited designs.
