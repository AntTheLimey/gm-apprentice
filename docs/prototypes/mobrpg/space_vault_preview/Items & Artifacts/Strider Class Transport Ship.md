## Overview

This atmospheric enabled ship is designed for military use to land troops and supplies on planets. They are medium distance ships and are able to travel between systems and through gates, even if most are attached to larger troop carriers or supply ships. They are favored by the Federal Republican Marines due to their rugged nature and dependability.

Decommissioned Strider Class ships are desired on the resale marked due to their platform being highly customizable. You will often find these operating in inter-planetary use as well as some short to medium term hauling. Their ability to land on planets with thicker atmospheres make them particularly valuable on planets without a space elevator.
