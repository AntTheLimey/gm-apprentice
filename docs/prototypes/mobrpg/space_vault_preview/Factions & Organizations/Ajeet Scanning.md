## Overview

A company based in the Thides sector, it's a one man operation. Focused mostly on asteroid scanning, the company earns by finding the raw materials the miners seek. The company operates one ship that they hire out to scan asteroids, and the occasional moon, for materials their clients request. While most companies want their full fee up front, they have been known to take partial or no-fee and a percent of the recovery. This has allowed the company to grow their funds quicker that expected, but is always one bad result from bankruptcy.
