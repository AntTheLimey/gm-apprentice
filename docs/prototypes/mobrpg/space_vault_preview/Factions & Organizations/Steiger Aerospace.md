## Overview

Builder of the Steiger Bulk Cargo Hauler Tug, the military Strider class atmospheric enabled transport ship, and the military Sentinel class corvette. Their expertise is in building rugged simplistic ships for the military. Because of this, many people look to purchase decommissioned Strider and Sentinel ships since their chassis offer a customizable platform.
