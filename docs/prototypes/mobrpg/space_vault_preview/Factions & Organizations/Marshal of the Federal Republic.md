## Overview

The most common federal official people have interactions with, they represent the law and order of the Federal Republic. There will be at least one assigned to every occupied system in the Federal Republic. Marshals are authorized to deputize locals to serve under them as needed. In addition, Marshals are authorized to enforce federal law even against the local authorities. When a citizen is arrested by local authorities, one of the first contacts is the local Marshals.
