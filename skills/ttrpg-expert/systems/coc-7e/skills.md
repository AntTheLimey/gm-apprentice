# Call of Cthulhu 7e -- Skills Reference

Complete CoC 7e skill list with base chances and usage notes.
For character creation (allocating points) and play (quick
lookup). Uses bonus/penalty dice, not flat modifiers. Success
levels: Regular (≤ skill), Hard (≤ half), Extreme (≤ fifth),
Critical (01), Fumble (96-00 if skill < 50, or 00 if ≥ 50).

## Bonus and Penalty Dice

Roll extra tens die alongside D100. **Bonus:** keep lower tens
(better). **Penalty:** keep higher tens (worse). Stack up to 2;
opposite types cancel one-for-one.

## Proficiency Benchmarks

| Rating | Level |
|--------|-------|
| 01-05% | Novice — complete amateur |
| 06-25% | Beginner — knows basics |
| 26-50% | Competent — reliable at routine tasks |
| 51-75% | Professional — expert, makes a living |
| 76-90% | Expert — advanced, respected authority |
| 91%+ | Master — world-class |

## Combat Skills

**Dodge** (DEX/2) — evade attacks. One free/round; additional
dodges get penalty die. Can dodge melee and first visible
ranged attack (with penalty die). No action cost.

**Fighting (Brawl)** (25%) — unarmed + improvised weapons.
Can "fight back" as defensive reaction.

**Fighting (specialisation)** (varies) — melee weapon categories
(Sword, Axe, etc.). Same fight-back mechanic.

**Firearms (Handgun)** (20%) — pistols, revolvers. No damage
bonus applies.

**Firearms (Rifle/Shotgun)** (25%) — long arms. Shotgun damage
varies by range.

**Firearms (specialisation)** (varies) — SMG (15%), MG (10%),
Heavy Weapons (10%).

**Throw** (20%) — hit target with thrown object. Range based
on STR.

## Communication Skills

**Charm** (15%) — win over through warmth/attractiveness.
Lasting effects but requires time/interaction.

**Fast Talk** (05%) — quick verbal deception/misdirection.
Temporary effects — target may realise within minutes.

**Intimidate** (15%) — frighten/coerce through threats. Target
obeys from fear. May backfire if target has power/numbers.

**Persuade** (10%) — reasoned argument/negotiation. Lasting
effects — target genuinely changes mind. Slower than Fast Talk.

## Investigation and Research Skills

**Accounting** (05%) — financial records, embezzlement, money
trails, business structures.

**Anthropology** (01%) — human cultures, artefacts, unfamiliar
customs, ritual origins. Context for cult activities.

**Appraise** (05%) — value/age/authenticity of objects. Identify
fakes, assess antiques, reveal hidden significance.

**Archaeology** (01%) — ancient civilisations, excavation, artefact
ID, dating ruins, interpreting inscriptions.

**History** (05%) — recorded history, events, figures. Research
and contextualise current events.

**Law** (05%) — legal codes, procedures, rights. Navigate legal
system, police interactions, contracts.

**Library Use** (20%) — find info in libraries, archives,
catalogues. Primary research skill. Typically takes several hours.

**Natural World** (10%) — plants, animals, weather. Recognise
when something in nature is wrong or unnatural.

**Occult** (05%) — folk magic, superstitions, secret societies.
NOT Mythos knowledge. Covers astrology, tarot, spiritualism.
Distinguish genuine threats from charlatanism.

**Science (specialisation)** (01%) — specific field: Biology,
Chemistry, Physics, Geology, Astronomy, Cryptography, Forensics,
Pharmacy, Zoology, etc. Each is separate skill.

## Interpersonal and Perception Skills

**Listen** (20%) — hear/interpret sounds. Active and passive
(Keeper rolls). Opposed by Stealth.

**Psychology** (10%) — read emotions, motives, truthfulness.
Detect lies, hidden agendas. CoC equivalent of BRP Insight.

**Spot Hidden** (25%) — notice hidden objects, concealed doors,
lurking figures, scene clues. Active and passive. Opposed by
Stealth/Disguise. Primary observation skill.

**Track** (10%) — follow trail over ground. Conditions affect
difficulty (rain/hard = penalty; snow/soft = bonus).

## Technical and Manipulation Skills

**Art/Craft (spec)** (05%) — specialisations: Acting, Forgery,
Photography, Writing, etc. Each separate. Photography useful
for documenting evidence.

**Disguise** (05%) — alter appearance. Opposed by Spot Hidden
or Psychology. Impersonating specific individual = penalty die.

**Drive Auto** (20%) — operate automobile. Rolls only for
chases, dangerous conditions, evasive manoeuvres.

**Electrical Repair** (10%) — repair/jury-rig electrical
devices: wiring, generators, radios.

**Locksmith** (01%) — open locks, crack safes. Requires tools;
without proper picks = penalty die.

**Mechanical Repair** (10%) — fix engines, clocks, plumbing,
machinery. Covers basic auto repair.

**Navigate** (10%) — find way using maps, compass, stars.

**Operate Heavy Machinery** (01%) — cranes, steam shovels,
trains. Each type may be separate specialisation.

**Pilot (spec)** (01%) — aircraft, boats, etc. Each separate.

**Sleight of Hand** (10%) — pick pockets, palm objects, cheat
at cards. Opposed by Spot Hidden.

## Physical Skills

**Climb** (20%) — walls, trees, cliffs, drainpipes. Penalty
for wet/crumbling/featureless. Fumble = fall.

**Jump** (20%) — height or distance. Running start extends
range. Also reduces falling damage on success.

**Ride** (05%) — control mount. Rolls for galloping, obstacles,
combat, spooked animal.

**Stealth** (20%) — move quietly. Opposed by Listen and Spot
Hidden. Moving quickly = penalty die.

**Survival (spec)** (10%) — food, water, shelter in specific
environment (Desert, Sea, Arctic, Forest). Each separate.

**Swim** (20%) — move through water. Rough water/currents
require rolls. Heavy equipment = penalty die.

## Medical Skills

**First Aid** (30%) — stabilise, stop bleeding, treat shock.
Heals 1 HP if applied within 1 round (1 hour non-combat).
One attempt per injury. Not disease/poison.

**Medicine** (01%) — diagnose/treat serious injuries, disease,
poison. Surgery. Heals 1D3 HP with proper facilities. Without
equipment = penalty die.

**Psychoanalysis** (01%) — treat mental illness. Restores 1D3
SAN per month of treatment. Can snap investigator out of
temporary insanity during session. Primary SAN recovery tool.

## Language Skills

**Language (Own)** (EDU) — native language. Rolls for obscure
dialects, archaic forms, damaged texts, technical material.

**Language (Other: specify)** (01%) — each language separate.
30%+ = basic conversation. 50%+ = academic texts. 70%+ = fluent.

## Special Skills

**Credit Rating** (00%) — social standing, financial resources,
respectability. Set during chargen within occupation range.
Not improved by experience — only narrative events. Used as
social skill: high CR opens doors Persuade cannot.

| Credit Rating | Level |
|:---:|-------|
| 0 | Penniless |
| 1-9 | Poor |
| 10-49 | Average |
| 50-89 | Wealthy |
| 90-98 | Rich |
| 99 | Super-rich |

**Cthulhu Mythos** (00%) — true cosmic horror knowledge.
- Cannot be raised by skill points or experience checks
- Only increases via Mythos tomes, rituals, entity encounters
- Permanently reduces max SAN: max = 99 minus Mythos skill
- Roll to recall lore, identify creatures/artefacts
- Central doom spiral: more knowledge = less possible sanity

## Luck

Derived attribute, spendable resource (not a skill):
- Starting: 3D6 × 5
- Spend after failed roll: pay difference between roll and
  skill to turn failure into success (e.g., skill 45%, rolled
  52 → spend 7 Luck)
- Cannot spend on SAN, damage, or pushed rolls
- Recovery: typically 1D10 between sessions
- Group Luck: lowest current Luck sets group threshold

## Pushing Rolls

After failure, player may push (one re-attempt): describe
how they try differently. If pushed roll also fails,
consequences are much worse. Each roll pushed once only.
Cannot push combat or SAN rolls.

## Experience and Improvement

Skill succeeds under stress → mark experience check. Between
adventures, roll D100 per checked skill: if roll > current
rating, skill increases by 1D10. Cthulhu Mythos never gets
experience checks.
