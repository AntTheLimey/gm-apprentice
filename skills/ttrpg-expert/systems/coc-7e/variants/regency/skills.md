# Regency Cthulhu — Skills

Regency (1811-1820) overlay for CoC 7e. Use alongside base
`../../skills.md`. Only differences listed here; all unlisted
base CoC skills remain available.

## Unavailable Skills

These base CoC 7e skills do not exist in Regency:

- Computer Use
- Drive Auto (replaced by Drive Carriage/Cart)
- Electrical Repair
- Electronics
- Hypnosis (replaced by Mesmerism)
- Pilot (most specialisations)
- Psychoanalysis (replaced by Reassure)
- Operate Heavy Machinery
- Science (all specialisations) (replaced by Natural Philosophy)

## New Skills

| Skill | Base | Note |
|-------|:----:|------|
| Astronomy | 01% | Replaces Science (Astronomy). Star gazing, navigation, astrology. |
| Dancing | DEX/5 | Social skill. Impress partners on dancefloor. Reputation consequences on failure. |
| Drive Carriage/Cart | 20% | Replaces Drive Auto. Any carriage from buggy to four-horse coach. |
| Etiquette | INT/5 | Replaces Credit Rating for social influence. Forms of address, introductions, social acceptance. |
| Fashion | 10% | Knowing correct styles and dressing well. Reputation consequences on failure. |
| Gaming | 10% | Card games (Whist, Loo, Speculation). Opposed by Gaming or Psychology. |
| Mesmerism | 01% | [Uncommon] Replaces Hypnosis. Trancelike state: alleviate pain (1D6 rounds), suppress trauma (+1D3 SAN), plant misinformation. Opposed by POW or Psychology. |
| Natural Philosophy | 01% | Replaces Science (all). No specialisations. Undisciplined physics, biology, chemistry, alchemy. |
| Reassure | APP/5 | Replaces Psychoanalysis. Calm words: +3 SAN on success, halves temporary insanity recovery, suppress phobia/mania for 1D6 minutes. |
| Religion | 10% | Adjusted from base CoC. Core religions at Regular difficulty; fringe/obscure requires Hard unless specialised. |

## Adjusted Skills

**Credit Rating** (00%) — determines wealth AND social class via
occupational bands. See `character-generation.md` for band rules.

## Specialisation Changes

**Art/Craft** — Art (gentry): Acting, Art Appreciation, Embroidery,
Fine Art, Forgery, Music, Poetry, Sculpture. Craft (working class):
Blacksmithing, Carpentry, Cooking, Pottery, Sewing.

**Fighting** — Axe, Brawl, Flail, Garrote, Spear, Sword (Rapier or
Saber), Whip.

**Firearms** — Bow, Pistol (replaces Handgun), Rifle/Blunderbuss
(replaces Rifle/Shotgun).

## Uncommon Skills

Skills available by Keeper permission or in specific circumstances:

- Animal Handling (05%) — common for lower classes only
- Artillery (01%) — cannon; add to all military careers
- Demolitions (01%) — available but explosives less powerful
- Diving — part of Swimming; no separate skill
- Lore (01%) — available but mostly covered by Occult and Natural Philosophy
- Pilot (Boat) (01%) — lower classes and navy only
- Read Lips (01%) — exceptionally rare; Keeper permission required
