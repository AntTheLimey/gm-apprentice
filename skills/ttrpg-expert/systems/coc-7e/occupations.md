# Call of Cthulhu 7e -- Occupations

Occupation templates with skill allocations, formulas, and
Credit Rating ranges. For chargen (skill point distribution)
and NPC building.

Each occupation provides: 8 occupation skills (incl. Credit
Rating), a skill point formula, and a Credit Rating range.
Personal interest points (INT × 2) go to any skill except
Cthulhu Mythos. Occupations are starting points, not limits.
Swap skills for era/setting with Keeper approval.

## Skill Point Formulas

| Formula | Typical Occupations |
|---------|-------------------|
| EDU × 4 | Academic (Professor, Librarian, Author) |
| EDU × 2 + APP × 2 | Social (Dilettante, Entertainer) |
| EDU × 2 + DEX × 2 | Manual (Mechanic, Criminal) |
| EDU × 2 + STR × 2 | Physical (Soldier, Police Officer) |
| EDU × 2 + (STR or DEX) × 2 | Mixed (PI, Police Detective) |
| EDU × 2 + POW × 2 | Willpower (Clergy, Occultist) |

All characteristic values are percentile (×5), not raw 3-18.

## Occupations A-Z

**Antiquarian** — rare books/maps/artefact dealer.
EDU × 4. CR 30-70. Skills: Appraise, Art/Craft, History,
Library Use, Language (Other), one interpersonal (Charm/Fast
Talk/Intimidate/Persuade), Spot Hidden, + one of Archaeology/
Law/Navigate/Science.

**Artist** — painter, sculptor, musician, creative.
EDU × 2 + DEX × 2 or POW × 2. CR 9-50. Skills: Art/Craft,
two more Art/Craft or one + related, History, Listen, Natural
World or Spot Hidden, Psychology, + one interpersonal.

**Author** — fiction/non-fiction/journalism writer.
EDU × 4. CR 9-30. Skills: Art/Craft (Writing), History,
Library Use, Language (Own), Natural World or Occult, one
interpersonal, Psychology, + any one.

**Clergy** — priest, minister, rabbi, religious leader.
EDU × 2 + POW × 2. CR 9-60. Skills: Accounting, History,
Library Use, Listen, Language (Other), one interpersonal,
Psychology, + any one.

**Criminal** — burglar, forger, con artist.
EDU × 2 + DEX × 2 or STR × 2. CR 5-65. Skills: Disguise or
Electrical Repair, Fighting or Firearms, Locksmith or
Mechanical Repair, Sleight of Hand, Spot Hidden, Stealth,
one interpersonal, Psychology.

**Dilettante** — independently wealthy, pursues interests.
EDU × 2 + APP × 2. CR 50-99. Skills: Art/Craft, Firearms,
Language (Other), Ride, one interpersonal, + any three.

**Doctor of Medicine** — physician, surgeon, specialist.
EDU × 4. CR 30-80. Skills: First Aid, Language (Latin),
Medicine, Psychology, Science (Biology), Science (Pharmacy),
+ two of Accounting/Library Use/Persuade/Science.

**Drifter** — wanderer, hobo, itinerant worker.
EDU × 2 + (STR or DEX) × 2 or APP × 2. CR 0-5. Skills:
Climb, Jump or Swim, Listen, Natural World, Navigate, one
interpersonal, Stealth, + any one.

**Engineer** — mechanical, electrical, structural systems.
EDU × 4. CR 30-60. Skills: Art/Craft (Technical Drawing),
Electrical Repair, Library Use, Mechanical Repair, Operate
Heavy Machinery, Science (Engineering/Physics), + two of
Accounting/Climb/Drive Auto/Science.

**Entertainer** — actor, singer, dancer, comedian.
EDU × 2 + APP × 2. CR 9-70. Skills: Art/Craft (Acting),
Disguise, two interpersonal, Listen, Psychology, + any two.

**Farmer/Rancher** — works land or raises livestock.
EDU × 2 + (STR or DEX) × 2. CR 9-30. Skills: Art/Craft
(Farming) or Natural World, Drive Auto or Ride, Fighting or
Firearms, First Aid, Mechanical Repair, Natural World, Track,
+ any one.

**Journalist** — reporter, columnist, photojournalist.
EDU × 4. CR 9-30. Skills: Art/Craft (Photography), History,
Library Use, Language (Own), one interpersonal, Psychology,
+ two of Accounting/Law/Stealth/Language.

**Lawyer** — attorney, barrister, legal scholar.
EDU × 4. CR 30-80. Skills: Accounting, Law, Library Use,
two interpersonal, Psychology, + any two.

**Librarian** — keeper of books and archives.
EDU × 4. CR 9-35. Skills: Accounting, Library Use, Language
(Other), Language (Own), + four of Art/Craft/History/Science/
Language.

**Mechanic** — repairs autos, machinery, equipment.
EDU × 2 + DEX × 2. CR 9-40. Skills: Art/Craft, Climb,
Drive Auto, Electrical Repair, Mechanical Repair, Operate
Heavy Machinery, + two of Accounting/Locksmith/Science.

**Military Officer** — commissioned officer, leadership/strategy.
EDU × 2 + (STR or DEX) × 2. CR 20-70. Skills: Accounting,
Firearms, Navigate, two interpersonal, Psychology, + two of
Drive Auto/Fighting/First Aid/Law/Language/Survival.

**Missionary** — religious worker in foreign lands.
EDU × 2 + APP × 2. CR 0-30. Skills: Art/Craft, First Aid,
Mechanical Repair, Medicine, Natural World, one interpersonal,
+ two of Language/Listen/Navigate/Survival.

**Nurse** — medical professional, patient care.
EDU × 4. CR 9-30. Skills: First Aid, Listen, Medicine, one
interpersonal, Psychology, Science (Biology/Chemistry), Spot
Hidden, + any one.

**Occultist** — student of hidden lore, esoteric traditions.
EDU × 2 + POW × 2. CR 9-65. Skills: Anthropology or History,
Library Use, Language (Other), Occult, one interpersonal,
+ three of Archaeology/Art/Craft/Science/Spot Hidden.

**Parapsychologist** — investigates psychic phenomena/hauntings.
EDU × 4. CR 9-30. Skills: Anthropology, Art/Craft (Photography),
History, Library Use, Occult, Psychology, one interpersonal,
+ one of Language/Science/Spot Hidden.

**Police Detective** — police investigator, official resources.
EDU × 2 + (STR or DEX) × 2. CR 20-50. Skills: Art/Craft
(Acting) or Disguise, Firearms, Law, Listen, one interpersonal,
Psychology, Spot Hidden, + one of Accounting/Drive Auto/First
Aid/Stealth.

**Police Officer** — uniformed law enforcement.
EDU × 2 + (STR or DEX) × 2. CR 9-30. Skills: Drive Auto,
Fighting (Brawl), Firearms, First Aid, Law, one interpersonal,
Psychology or Spot Hidden, + any one.

**Private Investigator** — works outside official channels.
EDU × 2 + (STR or DEX) × 2. CR 9-50. Skills: Art/Craft
(Photography), Disguise, Law, Library Use, one interpersonal,
Psychology, Spot Hidden, + one of Accounting/Fighting/Firearms/
Locksmith/Stealth.

**Professor** — university academic and researcher.
EDU × 4. CR 20-70. Skills: Library Use, Language (Other),
Psychology, four field-appropriate skills, one interpersonal.

**Sailor** — merchant marine, naval rating, fisherman.
EDU × 2 + (STR or DEX) × 2. CR 9-30. Skills: Electrical or
Mechanical Repair, Fighting (Brawl), Firearms or Throw, First
Aid, Navigate, Pilot (Boat), Spot Hidden or Survival (Sea),
Swim.

**Soldier** — enlisted military, combat-trained.
EDU × 2 + (STR or DEX) × 2. CR 9-30. Skills: Climb or Swim,
Dodge, Fighting, Firearms, First Aid, Stealth, Survival,
+ one of Drive Auto/Language/Mechanical Repair/Navigate/Throw.

**Spy** — intelligence operative, covert agent.
EDU × 2 + (STR or DEX) × 2 or APP × 2. CR 20-60. Skills:
Disguise, Firearms, Listen, Language (Other), one interpersonal,
Psychology, Sleight of Hand or Locksmith, Stealth.

**Tribal Member** — traditional/indigenous community member.
EDU × 2 + (STR or DEX) × 2. CR 0-15. Skills: Climb, Fighting
(Brawl) or Throw, Listen, Natural World, Occult, Spot Hidden,
Survival, Swim.

## Custom Occupations

1. Title + brief description
2. Skill point formula matching primary characteristics
3. Eight occupation skills (one must be Credit Rating)
4. Appropriate Credit Rating range
5. Keeper approval

Adapt existing occupations for era/setting: 1920s Bootlegger =
Criminal + Drive Auto. Modern Hacker = Engineer + Computer Use
replacing Operate Heavy Machinery.
