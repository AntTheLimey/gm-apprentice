# Call of Cthulhu 7e — Professions

BRP profession templates with skill allocations and wealth
levels. Used during character creation to guide skill point
distribution and during prep to build NPCs with appropriate
skill sets.

## How Professions Work

A profession defines your investigator's job or vocation at
the start of play. It provides:

- **Ten skills** that are the core of the profession. Allocate
  professional skill points among these.
- **A wealth level** (Destitute to Wealthy) indicating economic
  starting position and access to equipment.
- **Special notes** about powers, if relevant to the setting.

Professions are starting points, not limits. Investigators
grow beyond their profession through experience. Swap skills
that do not fit the era or setting with Keeper approval.

## Wealth Levels

| Level | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| Destitute | Penniless, homeless, survival is a concern |
| Poor | Meagre income, humble dwelling, no luxury |
| Average | Comfortable middle class, major purchases weighed |
| Affluent | Well-off, can spend freely without worry |
| Wealthy | Vast resources, near-inexhaustible funds |

## Professions A-Z

**Artist** — You create art in a physical or digital medium.
Wealth: Any, usually Poor or Average.
Skills: Any two Art, any Craft, Insight, one Knowledge,
Language (Other), Language (Own), Listen, Research, Spot.

**Assassin** — A professional killer, skilled in secrecy.
Wealth: Average or Affluent.
Skills: Dodge, Hide, Listen, Spot, Stealth, plus five of:
Brawl, Disguise, Drive, Firearm (any), Fine Manipulation,
Grapple, Martial Arts, Melee Weapon (any), Missile Weapon
(any), Ride, Throw, Track.

**Athlete** — Professional or amateur sportsperson.
Wealth: Poor to Wealthy, usually Average or Affluent.
Skills: Climb, Dodge, Jump, Stealth, Throw, plus five of:
Brawl, First Aid, Grapple, Insight, Listen, Martial Arts,
Spot, Ride, Swim.

**Beggar** — Surviving by begging and wits on the streets.
Wealth: Destitute.
Skills: Bargain, Fast Talk, Hide, Insight, Knowledge
(Region: local), Listen, Persuade, Sleight of Hand, Spot,
Stealth.

**Clerk** — Desk worker in finance or administration.
Wealth: Average.
Skills: Bargain, Etiquette, Knowledge (Accounting), Knowledge
(Law), one other Knowledge, Language (Own), Persuade, Research,
Status, Technical (Computer Use) or Literacy.

**Computer Tech** — Software engineer or hacker.
Wealth: Average to Affluent.
Skills: Craft (Hardware or Code), one Knowledge, Language
(Other/programming), Repair (Electrical), Repair (Electronics),
Research, Science (Mathematics), Status, Technical (Computer
Use), plus one of: Accounting, Hide, Knowledge (Law).

**Crafter** — Makes trade goods by hand.
Wealth: Poor to Affluent, usually Average.
Skills: Appraise, any Art, Bargain, any two Craft, Spot,
Research, Status, plus two of: Fine Manipulation, Heavy
Machine, Repair (any).

**Criminal** — Makes a living breaking the law.
Wealth: Poor to Affluent, usually Average.
Skills: Bargain, Hide, Stealth, Drive or Ride, plus six of:
Appraise, Brawl, Climb, Fast Talk, Fine Manipulation, Firearm
(any), Gaming, Grapple, Insight, Jump, Knowledge (Law), Listen,
Martial Arts, Melee Weapon (any), Persuade, Spot, Throw.

**Detective** — Private or police investigator.
Wealth: Average or Affluent.
Skills: Firearm (Handgun), Knowledge (Law), Listen, Persuade,
Spot, Research, plus four of: Brawl, Disguise, Dodge, Drive,
Fast Talk, Firearm (any), Grapple, Hide, Insight, Knowledge
(any), Language (any), Medicine, Ride, Science (any), Stealth,
Track.

**Doctor** — Physician treating the sick and injured.
Wealth: Average to Affluent.
Skills: First Aid, Language (Other), Medicine, Persuade,
Research, Spot, plus four of: Insight, Language (various),
Psychotherapy, Science (any), Status.

**Engineer** — Builds, repairs, or maintains machines.
Wealth: Poor to Affluent, usually Average.
Skills: Craft (any), Repair (Mechanical), Repair (Structural),
Spot, Status, plus five of: Art (Drafting), Drive, Heavy
Machine, Knowledge (any), Pilot (any), Repair (Electrical),
Science (any), Technical (Computer Use).

**Entertainer** — Performer of any kind.
Wealth: Destitute to Wealthy, usually Average.
Skills: Art (any), Disguise, Fast Talk, Fine Manipulation,
Insight, Language (Other), Language (Other), Listen, Perform
(any), Persuade.

**Explorer** — Seeker of unknown places and knowledge.
Wealth: Affluent or Wealthy.
Skills: Climb, Language (Other), Language (Own), Persuade,
Research, Spot, plus four of: Knowledge (Anthropology, History,
Natural World, or Region), Drive, Fast Talk, Firearm (any),
Navigate, Pilot (Aircraft or Boat), Ride, Science (Geology),
Swim, Track.

**Farmer** — Works the land for a living.
Wealth: Poor to Affluent, usually Average.
Skills: Bargain, Craft (any), Knowledge (Natural History),
Listen, Spot, plus five of: Brawl, Drive, Firearm (Rifle or
Shotgun), First Aid, Heavy Machine, Knowledge (History), Repair
(Mechanical), Ride, Science (Biology, Botany, or Geology),
Track.

**Gambler** — Lives by chance and skill at games.
Wealth: Poor to Affluent, usually Average.
Skills: Bargain, Brawl, Dodge, Fast Talk, Gaming, Insight,
Knowledge (Accounting), Persuade, Sleight of Hand, Spot.

**Herder** — Tends herd animals on the open range.
Wealth: Poor to Average.
Skills: Craft (Knots), Firearm (Rifle), Knowledge (Natural
History), Knowledge (Region: the Range), Listen, Navigate,
Ride, Spot, Throw, Track.

**Hunter** — Tracks and kills wild game.
Wealth: Poor or Average (Wealthy for big game hunters).
Skills: Climb, Hide, Listen, Navigate, Spot, Stealth, Track,
plus three of: Firearm (Rifle or Shotgun), Knowledge (Natural
History or Region), Language (Other), Melee Weapon (Spear),
Missile Weapon (any), Ride.

**Journalist** — Reporter, columnist, or photojournalist.
Wealth: Average to Affluent.
Skills: Fast Talk, Insight, Language (Own), Listen, Persuade,
Research, Spot, plus three of: Art (Photography), Disguise,
Hide, Knowledge (any), Language (Other), Status, Stealth,
Technical (Computer Use).

**Laborer** — Blue-collar physical worker.
Wealth: Poor or Average.
Skills: Climb, Craft (any), Drive, Brawl, Grapple, Heavy
Machine, plus four of: Appraise, Fine Manipulation, Language
(Other), Literacy, Repair (Mechanical), Repair (Structural),
Technical (Computer Use).

**Lawkeeper** — Police, sheriff, or marshal.
Wealth: Average.
Skills: Brawl, Dodge, Fast Talk, Knowledge (Law), Listen,
Spot, plus four of: Drive, Firearm (any), First Aid, Grapple,
Insight, Knowledge (Region or Group), Language (Other), Martial
Arts, Melee Weapon (any), Missile Weapon (any), Ride, Status,
Track.

**Lawyer** — Legal professional.
Wealth: Any.
Skills: Bargain, Fast Talk, Insight, Knowledge (Law), one
other Knowledge, Language (Own), Perform (Oratory), Persuade,
Research, Status.

**Mechanic** — Maintains and repairs machines and vehicles.
Wealth: Poor to Average.
Skills: Bargain, Craft (Metalwork), Drive, Fine Manipulation,
Heavy Machine, Repair (Electrical), Repair (Electronics),
Repair (Mechanical), Repair (Structural), Spot.

**Merchant** — Buyer and seller of goods.
Wealth: Average to Wealthy, usually Affluent.
Skills: Appraise, Bargain, Fast Talk, Knowledge (Accounting),
Knowledge (Business), Persuade, Research, Status, plus any
two others appropriate to trade.

**Noble** — Born into wealth and a ruling class.
Wealth: Affluent to Wealthy.
Skills: Bargain, Drive, Etiquette, Language (Own), Language
(Other), Literacy, Status, plus any three as hobbies.

**Occultist** — Student of hidden lore and arcane secrets.
Wealth: Poor to Wealthy, usually Affluent.
Skills: Fast Talk, Insight, Knowledge (Anthropology), Knowledge
(History), Knowledge (Occult), Language (Other), Language (Own),
Research, plus two of: Art (any), Craft (any), Knowledge
(Archaeology), Medicine, Science (any), Status.
Special: May have magic powers if appropriate to the setting.

**Pilot** — Guides vehicles through air, water, or space.
Wealth: Average to Affluent.
Skills: Drive, Heavy Machine, Listen, Navigate, Pilot (any),
Spot, plus four of: Bargain, Climb, Command, Craft (any),
Knowledge (Region), Repair (any), Language (Other), Persuade,
Science (any), Technical (Computer Use).

**Politician** — Elected or appointed authority.
Wealth: Affluent to Wealthy.
Skills: Bargain, Etiquette, Fast Talk, Insight, Knowledge
(Law), Persuade, Status, plus three of: Knowledge (Accounting,
Group, History, or Region), Listen, Language (any), Perform
(Oratory), Research.

**Priest** — Religious leader and spiritual guide.
Wealth: Poor to Affluent, usually Average.
Skills: Fast Talk, Insight, Knowledge (History), Knowledge
(Philosophy), Knowledge (Religion), Language (Own), Perform
(Ritual), Persuade, plus two of: Knowledge (Occult), Language
(Other), Listen, Literacy, Perform (Oratory), Research, Status,
Teach.
Special: Faith may grant access to supernatural powers.

**Sailor** — Works aboard a sea vessel.
Wealth: Poor or Average.
Skills: Climb, Craft (any), Dodge, Grapple, Navigate, Pilot
(Boat), Swim, plus three of: Artillery (any), Command, Language
(Other), Listen, Repair (Mechanical), Repair (Structural),
Spot.

**Scholar** — Devoted to study and learning.
Wealth: Average or Affluent.
Skills: Language (Other), Language (Own), Persuade, Research,
Teach, plus five Knowledge or Science skills.

**Scientist** — Researcher and experimenter.
Wealth: Average or Affluent.
Skills: Craft (any), Persuade, Research, Status, Technical
(Computer Use) or Heavy Machine, plus five Knowledge or
Science skills.

**Servant** — Domestic employee of an affluent household.
Wealth: Poor to Average.
Skills: Craft (any), Etiquette, Hide, Language (Own), Listen,
Stealth, plus four of: Bargain, Drive, First Aid, Insight,
Knowledge (Accounting), Language (Other), Persuade.

**Shaman** — Tribal magician and spirit-worker.
Wealth: Poor or Average.
Skills: Art (any), Insight, Knowledge (History), Knowledge
(Occult), Language (Own), Listen, Perform (Rituals), Persuade,
plus two of: Craft (any), Fast Talk, First Aid, Hide,
Knowledge (Anthropology), Language (Other), Medicine, Science
(Pharmacy), Status.
Special: May use magic if appropriate to the setting.

**Soldier** — Professional combatant.
Wealth: Poor or Average (access to expensive equipment).
Skills: Brawl, Climb, Dodge, First Aid, plus six of:
Artillery, Command, Drive, Firearm (any), Grapple, Heavy
Weapon (any), Hide, Language (Other), Listen, Jump, Medicine,
Melee Weapon (any), Missile Weapon (any), Navigate, Repair
(Mechanical), Ride, Spot, Stealth, Throw.

**Spy** — Skilled in infiltration and subterfuge.
Wealth: Average or Affluent.
Skills: Dodge, Fast Talk, Hide, Listen, Research, Spot,
Stealth, plus three of: Art (Photography), Brawl, Disguise,
Etiquette, Firearm (any), Grapple, Knowledge (any), Language
(any), Martial Arts, Navigate, Pilot (any), Repair (any),
Ride, Swim, Technical (Computer Use), Throw, Track.

**Student** — Full-time learner or apprentice.
Wealth: Poor to Wealthy, usually Average.
Skills: Language (Own), Research, plus eight others as courses
of study. Common choices: Art (any), Craft (any), First Aid,
Insight, Knowledge (any), Language (Other), Listen, Medicine,
Perform, Persuade, Science (any), Technical (Computer Use),
one Physical skill.
Special: Arcane students may have magic or sorcery.

**Teacher** — Instructor of one or more subjects.
Wealth: Average or Affluent.
Skills: Language (Own), Persuade, Research, Status, Teach,
plus five others as areas of expertise.

**Thief** — Takes what is wanted through stealth or deception.
Wealth: Any.
Skills: Appraise, Dodge, Fast Talk, Hide, Stealth, plus five
of: Bargain, Brawl, Climb, Disguise, Fine Manipulation,
Firearm (Pistol, Revolver, or Shotgun), Grapple, Insight,
Listen, Jump, Knowledge (Law), Persuade, Repair (Mechanical),
Spot.

**Tribesperson** — Member of a primitive culture.
Wealth: Destitute to Poor.
Skills: Craft (any), Dodge, Grapple, Hide, Knowledge (Natural
History), Spot, Throw, Track, plus two of: Brawl, Climb, First
Aid, Listen, Jump, Knowledge (Occult), Melee Weapon (Spear or
Club), Missile Weapon (Bow), Language (Other), Ride, Stealth,
Swim.

**Warrior** — Individual combatant and weapon specialist.
Wealth: Destitute to Average, usually Poor.
Skills: Brawl, Dodge, Grapple, Melee Weapon (any), Missile
Weapon (any), plus five of: Climb, Firearm (any), Hide,
Listen, Jump, Language (Other), Martial Arts, Ride, Spot,
Stealth, Swim, Throw, Track.

**Wizard** — Practitioner of magic or sorcery.
Wealth: Poor to Wealthy, usually Affluent.
Skills: Craft (any), Insight, Knowledge (Occult), two other
Knowledge skills, Language (Other), Listen, Perform (Rituals),
Persuade, Research.
Special: Uses magic or sorcery. Work with the Keeper to
determine starting spells.

**Writer** — Communicates through prose, poetry, or scripts.
Wealth: Poor to Wealthy, usually Average.
Skills: Art (Writing), Insight, Language (Own), Persuade,
Research, plus five of: Fast Talk, Knowledge (any), Language
(Other), Listen, Status, Technical (Computer Use).

## Creating New Professions

1. Decide on a title and describe the profession.
2. Set an appropriate wealth level or range.
3. Choose ten skills essential to the profession (may include
   "pick X from the following" options).
4. Get Keeper approval.

Existing professions can be adapted by swapping a few skills
to fit the era or setting. A constable in 12th century England
might swap Firearms for Melee Weapon (Sword).
