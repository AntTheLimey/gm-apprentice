# Call of Cthulhu 7e -- Weapons Reference

Melee, missile, and firearm tables with damage, special
effects, range, and requirements. Used during character
creation (equipping investigators) and play (damage lookups
during combat).

## Reading the Tables

- **Skill**: Weapon class specialty used to attack.
- **Base**: Default skill chance for untrained users.
- **Dmg**: Damage rolled. "+dm" = add full damage modifier.
  "+1/2dm" = add half damage modifier (round up).
- **Attk**: Attacks per round. Fractions (1/2, 1/3) mean one
  attack every 2 or 3 rounds.
- **Special**: Type of enhanced damage on an Extreme success
  (bleeding, crushing, entangling, impaling, knockback).
- **Rng**: Base range. Long range (2x base) warrants a
  penalty die. Extreme range (4x base) requires an Extreme
  success.
- **Hands**: 1H = one-handed, 2H = two-handed.
- **HP**: Weapon hit points.
- **STR/DEX**: Minimum requirements to wield without penalty.

## Special Success Types

**Bleeding** — Slashing wound severs a vein or artery. 1 HP
additional damage per round on DEX rank 1 until staunched.
First Aid or Stamina roll stops the bleeding.

**Crushing** — Blunt trauma. Doubles the damage modifier
(negative becomes none, none becomes +1D4). Target must make
Stamina roll or be stunned 1D3 rounds.

**Entangling** — Pins or snares the target. Trapped limbs
cannot act until freed.

**Impaling** — Deep piercing wound. Weapon does normal damage
on the way in, and must be pulled free (Effort roll or First
Aid) doing half rolled damage again on removal. Firearms and
pointed weapons impale.

**Knockback** — Unbalances the target. Compare damage rolled
vs target's SIZ on resistance table. Failure = knocked back
1 metre per 5 excess damage. Agility roll to stay standing.

## Firearm Spot Rules

**Reloading**: 1 round per 2 shells; 1 round to swap a clip.
Machine gun belt change takes 2 rounds.

**Malfunctions**: Roll equal to or above Mal number = weapon
jams or misfires. Revolvers/bolt-actions: dud round, cycle
past in 1 round. Automatics: jam, 1D6 rounds and a Firearm
or Repair roll to clear.

**Point-blank range**: Attacks within DEX/3 metres are Easy.

**Extended range**: Base range = unmodified. 2x base = Difficult.
4x base = 1/5 skill chance.

**No damage modifier**: Firearms never apply the attacker's
damage modifier.
