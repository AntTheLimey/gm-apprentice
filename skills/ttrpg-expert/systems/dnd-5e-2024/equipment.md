## Weapon Properties

| Property | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| Ammunition | Requires ammo; drawing is part of attack; recover half after fight |
| Finesse | Use STR or DEX for attack and damage (same for both) |
| Heavy | Disadv if STR < 13 (melee) or DEX < 13 (ranged) |
| Light | Extra attack as Bonus Action with different Light weapon (no ability mod to damage) |
| Loading | Fire only once per action/bonus/reaction regardless of Extra Attack |
| Range (X/Y) | Normal range X; long range Y with Disadv; can't attack beyond Y |
| Reach | +5 ft reach for attacks and Opportunity Attacks |
| Thrown | Throw for ranged attack; use same ability mod as melee |
| Two-Handed | Requires two hands to attack |
| Versatile (XdY) | One or two hands; parenthetical damage when used with two |

## Shield

Shield: +2 AC. 6 lb. 10 GP. Utilize action to don or doff. Requires training.

**Armor training:** Without training, Disadv on all STR/DEX D20 Tests and can't cast spells. Shield without training gives no AC bonus.
