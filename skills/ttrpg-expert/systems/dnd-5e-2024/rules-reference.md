# D&D 5e 2024 -- Rules Reference

Combat, spellcasting, death saves, resting, environment, and advanced mechanics.

## Combat Flow

1. **Determine Surprise:** Creatures caught unawares have Disadvantage on Initiative.
2. **Roll Initiative:** d20 + DEX mod (or use Initiative score: 10 + DEX mod). Descending order.
3. **Take Turns:** Each turn: one Action, one Bonus Action (if granted), Movement (up to Speed), one free object interaction.
4. **Reactions:** One per round, resets at start of your turn. Common: Opportunity Attack.
5. **Next Round:** After all combatants act, new round begins.

### Action Economy per Turn

| Resource | Usage |
|----------|-------|
| Action | One of the 12 actions below |
| Bonus Action | Light weapon extra attack, certain spells/features |
| Reaction | Opportunity attack, certain spells (one per round) |
| Movement | Up to Speed in feet; splittable around actions |

## Actions in Combat (12)

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Attack** | One attack roll (weapon or Unarmed Strike). Equip/unequip one weapon as part of action. |
| **Dash** | Gain extra movement equal to your Speed this turn. |
| **Disengage** | Movement doesn't provoke Opportunity Attacks for rest of turn. |
| **Dodge** | Attacks against you have Disadv (if you see attacker); Adv on DEX saves. Lost if Incapacitated or Speed 0. |
| **Help** | Give ally Adv on next ability check or next attack vs creature within 5 ft of you. Expires start of your next turn. |
| **Hide** | DC 15 DEX (Stealth) while Heavily Obscured or behind 3/4 or Total Cover. Success = Invisible condition. |
| **Influence** | Urge monster to act. GM sets Willing/Unwilling/Hesitant. Hesitant requires ability check; DC 15 or monster's INT (higher). |
| **Magic** | Cast a spell with casting time of one action, or activate a magic item. |
| **Ready** | Set trigger + action (or movement) as Reaction. Readied spells require Concentration until released. |
| **Search** | WIS check: Insight (mind), Medicine (ailment), Perception (hidden), Survival (tracks). |
| **Study** | INT check: Arcana (spells), History (events), Investigation (traps), Nature (terrain), Religion (deities). |
| **Utilize** | Use an object requiring an action (potion, shield don/doff, device). |

### Opportunity Attacks

Triggered when a creature you can see moves out of your reach without Disengaging. Uses your Reaction for one melee attack.

### Extra Attack

Some classes (Fighter, Barbarian, Monk, Paladin, Ranger) gain Extra Attack at level 5: two attacks when taking the Attack action. Fighter gets more at 11 and 20.

## Spellcasting

### Spell Slots

Finite resource; recovered on Long Rest (Short Rest for Warlock Pact Magic). Levels 1-9.

**One spell slot per turn:** Can't cast two slot spells in the same turn (e.g., action + bonus action both using slots).

### Cantrips

Cast at will, no slot. Damage scales at character levels 5, 11, 17.

### Concentration

Only one Concentration spell active at a time. Damage forces CON save: DC = max(10, half damage taken), max DC 30. Ends if Incapacitated, dead, or you cast another Concentration spell.

### Ritual Casting

Add 10 minutes to casting time; no slot expended. Must have spell prepared (or in spellbook for Wizards). Cannot upcast.

### Components

- **V (Verbal):** Must speak; fails if gagged or in Silence
- **S (Somatic):** Needs a free hand
- **M (Material):** Specific materials; Spellcasting Focus can replace non-consumed, non-costed materials

## Death Saving Throws

At 0 HP, roll d20 at start of each turn (DC 10):

| Roll | Result |
|------|--------|
| 10+ | One success (3 successes = stabilised at 0 HP) |
| 1-9 | One failure (3 failures = death) |
| Natural 20 | Regain 1 HP immediately |
| Natural 1 | Two failures |
| Damage at 0 HP | One auto failure per hit; crit within 5 ft = two failures |

Stabilised creature regains 1 HP after 1d4 hours unless healed sooner.

## Resting

### Short Rest (1 hour)

- Spend Hit Dice to heal: roll each HD + CON mod, regain that many HP (min 1)
- Some features recharge on Short Rest
- Interrupted by: Initiative, non-cantrip spell, taking damage. No benefit if interrupted.

### Long Rest (8 hours; minimum 6 sleeping)

- Regain **all** lost HP
- Regain **all** spent Hit Dice (2024 revision; previously half)
- Reduced ability scores return to normal
- Exhaustion level decreases by 1
- Most features recharge
- Must wait 16 hours before another Long Rest
- Interrupted by: Initiative, non-cantrip spell, damage, or 1 hr walking. If 1+ hour rested before interruption, gain Short Rest benefits. Each interruption adds 1 hour to required time.

## Light and Visibility

| Lighting | Obscurement | Effect |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| Bright Light | None | Normal vision |
| Dim Light | Lightly Obscured | Disadv on Perception (sight) |
| Darkness | Heavily Obscured | Effectively Blinded |

**Darkvision:** Dim Light as Bright, Darkness as Dim (greyscale). Range varies by species/feature.
**Blindsight:** See within range without sight; unaffected by Blinded or Darkness.
**Tremorsense:** Detect creatures/objects in contact with same surface within range.
**Truesight:** See in Darkness, see Invisible creatures, auto-detect illusions, see into Ethereal Plane.

## Difficult Terrain

Every foot of movement costs 1 extra foot. Sources: hostile creatures, furniture, heavy undergrowth, rubble, snow/ice, shin-deep liquid, narrow openings, steep slopes (20+ degrees).

## Falling

1d6 Bludgeoning damage per 10 feet fallen (max 20d10). Land Prone. Fall instantly up to 500 feet.

## Suffocating

Can hold breath for 1 + CON mod minutes (min 30 seconds). When out of breath: drop to 0 HP and dying. Can't regain HP or stabilise until breathing again.

## Damage Types

Acid, Bludgeoning, Cold, Fire, Force, Lightning, Necrotic, Piercing, Poison, Psychic, Radiant, Slashing, Thunder

**Resistance** = half damage. **Vulnerability** = double. **Immunity** = none. Applied once per instance.
