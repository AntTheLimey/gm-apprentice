# D&D 5e 2024 -- Feats

All feats from SRD 5.2, organised by category.

**Categories:** Origin (available at level 1 via background), General (level 4+), Fighting Style (requires Fighting Style feature), Epic Boon (level 19+).

## Origin Feats

| Feat | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Alert** | Add PB to Initiative rolls. After rolling, swap Initiative with one willing ally (neither Incapacitated). |
| **Magic Initiate** | Learn 2 cantrips + 1 level 1 spell from Cleric, Druid, or Wizard list. INT/WIS/CHA casting (choose). Free cast 1/LR or use slots. Swap one spell on level-up. *Repeatable* (different list each time). |
| **Savage Attacker** | Once per turn on a weapon hit, roll damage dice twice, use either result. |
| **Skilled** | Gain proficiency in any 3 skills or tools. *Repeatable.* |

## General Feats

| Feat | Prerequisite | Effect |
|------|-------------|--------|
| **Ability Score Improvement** | Level 4+ | +2 to one ability or +1 to two abilities (max 20). *Repeatable.* |
| **Grappler** | Level 4+, STR/DEX 13+ | +1 STR or DEX. Punch and Grab (damage + grapple in one Unarmed Strike, 1/turn). Adv on attacks vs creatures you grapple. No extra movement to drag grappled creature your size or smaller. |

## Fighting Style Feats

| Feat | Prerequisite | Effect |
|------|-------------|--------|
| **Archery** | Fighting Style feature | +2 to attack rolls with Ranged weapons. |
| **Defense** | Fighting Style feature | +1 AC while wearing Light, Medium, or Heavy armor. |
| **Great Weapon Fighting** | Fighting Style feature | Reroll 1s and 2s on damage dice with Two-Handed/Versatile melee weapons (treat as 3). |
| **Two-Weapon Fighting** | Fighting Style feature | Add ability modifier to damage of Light weapon extra attack. |

## Epic Boon Feats

All require level 19+. Each grants +1 to one ability score (max 30) plus a signature ability.

| Feat | Prerequisite | Effect |
|------|-------------|--------|
| **Boon of Combat Prowess** | Level 19+ | +1 ability. Peerless Aim: turn a miss into a hit (1/turn). |
| **Boon of Dimensional Travel** | Level 19+ | +1 ability. Blink Steps: teleport 30 ft after Attack or Magic action. |
| **Boon of Fate** | Level 19+ | +1 ability. Improve Fate: roll 2d4, apply as bonus/penalty to any D20 Test within 60 ft (1/SR or LR). |
| **Boon of Irresistible Offense** | Level 19+ | +1 STR or DEX. Bludg/Pierce/Slash always ignores Resistance. Nat 20 deals extra damage = increased ability score. |
| **Boon of Spell Recall** | Level 19+, Spellcasting | +1 INT/WIS/CHA. Free Casting: when casting with 1st-4th slot, roll 1d4; if result matches slot level, slot not expended. |
| **Boon of the Night Spirit** | Level 19+ | +1 ability. Merge with Shadows: Invisible (BA) in Dim/Dark; ends on action/BA/reaction. Resistance to all damage except Psychic/Radiant in Dim/Dark. |
| **Boon of Truesight** | Level 19+ | +1 ability. Truesight 60 ft. |
