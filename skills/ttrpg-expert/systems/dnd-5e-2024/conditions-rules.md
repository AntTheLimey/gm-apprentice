# D&D 5e 2024 -- Conditions, Rules & Hazards

Conditions, damage types, cover, rest, travel, environmental hazards, poisons, and diseases from the SRD 5.2 Rules Glossary and Gameplay Toolbox.

## Conditions (15)

| Condition | Effects |
|-----------|---------|
| **Blinded** | Can't see; auto-fail sight checks. Attacks against have Adv; own attacks have Disadv. |
| **Charmed** | Can't attack charmer. Charmer has Adv on social ability checks against you. |
| **Deafened** | Can't hear; auto-fail hearing checks. |
| **Exhaustion** | Cumulative levels (1-6). Each level: -2 on D20 Tests, -5 ft Speed. Level 6 = death. Long Rest removes 1 level. |
| **Frightened** | Disadv on ability checks and attacks while source of fear is in line of sight. Can't willingly move closer to source. |
| **Grappled** | Speed becomes 0. Ends if grappler is Incapacitated or target is forced out of grappler's reach. Escape: Athletics or Acrobatics vs grappler's Athletics. |
| **Incapacitated** | Can't take actions, bonus actions, or reactions. Disadv on Initiative. |
| **Invisible** | Can't be seen without special sense. Heavily Obscured for Hide purposes. Attacks against have Disadv; own attacks have Adv. Location can be detected by noise/tracks. |
| **Paralyzed** | Incapacitated. Auto-fail STR and DEX saves. Attacks against have Adv. Hits within 5 ft are Critical Hits. |
| **Petrified** | Incapacitated. Weight x10. Immune to poison and disease. Resistance to all damage. Unaware of surroundings. |
| **Poisoned** | Disadv on attack rolls and ability checks. |
| **Prone** | Disadv on attack rolls. Melee attacks within 5 ft have Adv against you; ranged attacks have Disadv. Stand up costs half your Speed. |
| **Restrained** | Speed 0. Attacks against have Adv; own attacks have Disadv. Disadv on DEX saves. |
| **Stunned** | Incapacitated. Auto-fail STR and DEX saves. Attacks against have Adv. |
| **Unconscious** | Incapacitated + Prone. Drop held items. Auto-fail STR/DEX saves. Attacks against have Adv. Hits within 5 ft are Critical Hits. Unaware of surroundings. |

### Condition Interactions

- **Incapacitated** is a prerequisite for Paralyzed, Stunned, and Unconscious
- **Exhaustion** stacks (cumulative levels); all other conditions do not stack with themselves
- **Prone** costs half movement to stand from
- **Grappled** sets Speed to 0; escaping requires an action

### Bloodied

A creature is **Bloodied** while it has half its HP or fewer remaining. No mechanical effect by default, but some features reference it.

| Type | Examples |
|------|---------|

| Bludgeoning | Blunt force, constriction, falling |

## Resting

### Short Rest (1 hour)

- Spend Hit Dice to heal (roll HD + CON mod, min 1 HP)
- Some features recharge
- Interrupted by: Initiative, non-cantrip spell, damage (no benefit)

### Long Rest (8 hours; min 6 sleeping)

- Regain all HP
- Regain all Hit Dice (2024 revision)
- Exhaustion -1 level
- Reduced ability scores return to normal
- Most features recharge
- 16-hour gap required between Long Rests
- Interrupted by: Initiative, non-cantrip spell, damage, 1 hr walking. If 1+ hr rested, gain Short Rest benefits.

## Travel

### Travel Pace

| Pace | /Min | /Hour | /Day | Effect |
|------|------|-------|------|--------|
| Slow | 200 ft | 2 mi | 18 mi | Adv on Perception/Survival |
| Normal | 300 ft | 3 mi | 24 mi | Disadv on Stealth |
| Fast | 400 ft | 4 mi | 30 mi | Disadv on Perception/Survival + Stealth |

### Extended Travel

After 8 hours/day: each additional hour requires CON save (DC 10 + 1/hr past 8) or gain 1 Exhaustion level.

### Special Movement

Miles/hour = Speed / 10. Miles/day (Normal) = miles/hour x hours traveled. Fast = x1.33. Slow = x0.67.

## Difficult Terrain

Every foot of movement costs 1 extra foot. Sources: hostile creatures, furniture, heavy undergrowth, rubble, snow/ice, shin-deep liquid, narrow openings, slopes 20+ degrees.

## Falling

1d6 Bludgeoning per 10 ft fallen (max 20d6). Land Prone. Fall instantly up to 500 ft.

## Suffocating

Hold breath: 1 + CON mod minutes (min 30 sec). Out of breath: drop to 0 HP and dying. Can't regain HP until breathing.

## Environmental Hazards

| Hazard | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Burning** | 1d4 Fire at start of each turn. Extinguish: Prone + roll, or douse/submerge. |
| **Deep Water** (100+ ft) | Each hour swimming without Swim Speed: DC 10 CON save or 1 Exhaustion |
| **Extreme Cold** (0 F or lower) | Each hour exposed: DC 10 CON save or 1 Exhaustion. Cold Resistance/Immunity = auto-pass. |
| **Extreme Heat** (100 F+) | Each hour without water: CON save or 1 Exhaustion (DC 5 + 1/hr). Med/Heavy armor = Disadv. Fire Resistance/Immunity = auto-pass. |
| **Frigid Water** | Safe for CON score minutes. Then: DC 10 CON/min or 1 Exhaustion. Cold Resistance = auto-pass. |
| **Heavy Precipitation** | Lightly Obscured; Disadv on Perception. Rain extinguishes open flames. |
| **High Altitude** (10,000+ ft) | Each hour counts as 2 for travel duration. Acclimate after 30 days. |
| **Slippery Ice** | Difficult Terrain. DC 10 DEX save when entering or starting turn, or fall Prone. |
| **Strong Wind** | Disadv on ranged weapon attacks. Extinguishes flames, disperses fog. Flyers must land at end of turn or fall. |
| **Thin Ice** | Tolerance: 3d10 x 10 lb per 10-ft square. Excess = break through into frigid water. |

## Magical Contagions

| Disease | Save | Effect | Cure |
|---------|------|--------|------|
| **Cackle Fever** | DC 13 CON | 1 Exhaustion. On taking damage: DC 13 CON or Incapacitated (laughing). Spreads 10-ft Emanation (DC 10 CON). | 3 successful LR saves; immune 1 year. |
| **Sewer Plague** | DC 11 CON | 1 Exhaustion. Half healing from HD. LR doesn't restore HP or reduce Exhaustion. Daily save: fail = +1 Exhaustion, success = -1 Exhaustion (0 = cured). | Reduce Exhaustion to 0. |
| **Sight Rot** | DC 15 CON | Blinded. | Heal/Lesser Restoration, or 3 doses Herbalism Kit ointment (72 hr total). Spreads via skin contact (DC 15 CON). |

**Recuperation:** 3 days rest, then DC 15 CON save. Success = Adv on saves vs the contagion for 24 hr.

## Curses

- **Bestow Curse:** Level 3 spell. 1 min duration = level 3; until dispelled = level 9 equivalent.
- **Cursed Creatures:** e.g. werewolves. Remove Curse may or may not destroy permanently (GM discretion).
- **Cursed Magic Items:** Deliberately created or supernatural origin.
- **Narrative Curses:** Triggered by taboo violation. Creature knows why; can learn how to end it.
- **Environmental Curses:** e.g. Demonic Possession (DC 15 CHA save; on roll of 1 on D20 Test, demon takes control; repeat save end of each turn; ends on LR success or Dispel Evil and Good).
