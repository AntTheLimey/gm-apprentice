# Pathfinder 2e (Remaster) — Monsters Level 11-16

A curated Monster Core selection (40 creatures) for high-tier PF2e
encounters: adult and ancient dragons, greater giants, powerful fiends and
celestials, lich-tier undead, kraken-scale beasts, greater elementals and
constructs, and sphinx-tier magical monsters. Abilities are paraphrased; all
game numbers, names, and traits are as printed.

**Legend:** Per = Perception; AC / Fort / Ref / Will are the four defenses;
HP line notes imm (immunities), res (resistances), weak (weaknesses).
Attacks give strike bonus and damage; reach in feet where it isn't 5. Spell
traditions (arcane / divine / occult / primal) flag casters — see the source
for full spell lists and DCs. `[r]` = reaction, `[1]/[2]/[3]` = action cost;
"recharge" abilities return after 1d4 rounds.
