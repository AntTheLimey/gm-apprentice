# Pathfinder 2e (Remaster) — Rank 9 Spells

Player Core + Player Core 2 spells (ORC). Mechanics are facts; all effect text is paraphrased.

**Format:** Name | Traditions | Traits | Cast | Range/Area/Targets | Effect (damage · save · heighten). **Cast:** number = actions, R = reaction, else a listed time. **Saves:** Fort/Ref/Will.

- **Detonate Magic** | Arcane/Primal | concentrate, manipulate | 2 | 120 ft, 1 spell or item | Uncommon. Counteract a target's magic; on success it bursts for 8d6 force, basic Ref, deactivating an item for a week (destroyed on a crit) or ending a spell and blasting its area. Heighten +1: +2d6.
- **Falling Stars** | Arcane/Primal | concentrate, manipulate | 2 | 500 ft, four 40-ft bursts | Call four meteors (choose sonic, fire, cold, or electricity): 6d10 bludgeoning in each 10-ft center plus 14d6 energy across each 40-ft burst, one basic Ref for all. Heighten +1: +1d10 bludgeoning, +2d6 energy.
- **Foresight** | Arcane/Divine/Occult | concentrate, manipulate, mental, prediction | 2 | 30 ft, 1 creature | Grants the target a +2 status bonus to initiative, denies Off-Guard from undetected foes and flanking, and gives you a warning reaction; only one Foresight can be active at a time.
- **Implosion** | Arcane/Primal | concentrate, manipulate | 2 | 30 ft, 1 creature | 75 damage as the target collapses inward, basic Fort; Sustain to strike a new target each round (never the same one, one per turn). No effect on incorporeal, gaseous, or liquid forms. Heighten +1: +10 damage.
- **Massacre** | Arcane/Divine/Primal | concentrate, death, manipulate, void | 2 | 60-ft line | Living creatures level 17 or lower, Fort; reduction to 0 HP kills instantly, and if the spell slays no one the void recoils, dealing 30 void to all in the line and to you. Heighten 10th: affects level 19 and lower, more damage.
- **Metamorphosis** | Arcane/Primal | concentrate, manipulate, polymorph | 2 | Self | Transform into any 8th-rank-or-lower polymorph form you know, gaining 40 temporary HP; Sustain to shift into a new form and refresh those temporary HP.
- **Nature's Enmity** | Primal | concentrate, manipulate, plant, wood | 2 | 500 ft, up to 6 creatures | Nearby flora and fauna turn hostile: -10-ft Speed near plants, a DC 8 flat each turn or be attacked for 2d10 slashing (basic Ref, Off-Guard), and DC 5 flat checks to cast primal spells.
- **Overwhelming Presence** | Divine/Occult | auditory, concentrate, incapacitation, manipulate, mental, visual | 2 | 40-ft burst | Appear as a majestic god; targets that fail a Will save are compelled to venerate you, wasting actions to pay tribute. Temporarily immune 1 minute afterward.
- **Phantasmagoria** | Arcane/Occult | concentrate, death, illusion, manipulate, mental | 2 | 500 ft, up to 10 creatures | Floods each target's mind with colliding visions for 16d6 mental, Will save.
- **Resplendent Mansion** | Arcane/Occult | concentrate, manipulate, structure | 2 | 500 ft | Conjure a mansion up to four stories tall and 300 ft per side, furnished and decorated as you imagine, stocked with food and servants, its doors and windows warded by Alarm.
- **Seize Soul** | Divine/Occult | concentrate, manipulate, unholy | 2 | 30 ft, 1 soul + vessel | Uncommon. Trap a slain target's soul in an item before it passes on, blocking all resurrection until the item is destroyed or the spell is counteracted.
- **Telepathic Demand** | Arcane/Divine/Occult | concentrate, incapacitation, linguistic, manipulate, mental | 2 | Planetary, 1 creature | Send a 25-word message carrying a Suggestion effect to a creature you've contacted telepathically; it may reply. Immune 1 day on a success, 1 month on a critical success.
- **Unfathomable Song** | Occult | auditory, concentrate, emotion, fear, incapacitation, manipulate, mental | 2 | 30 ft, up to 5 creatures | A Will save on casting and each first Sustain inflicts a random affliction: Frightened, Confused, Stupefied, Blinded, or Stunned.
- **Wails of the Damned** | Divine/Occult | auditory, concentrate, death, manipulate, void | 2 | 40-ft emanation | 8d10 void to living enemies, basic Fort. Heighten +1: +1d10.
- **Weapon of Judgment** | Divine | concentrate, manipulate, sanctified, spirit | 2 | 120 ft, 1 creature | A spectral weapon strikes the target for 4d10 (using your spell attack) whenever it breaks a named "war" edict (fails to attack a chosen foe) or "peace" edict (harms chosen allies). Heighten 10th: +1d10.
- **Wrathful Storm** | Primal | air, cold, concentrate, electricity, manipulate | 2 | 500 ft, 60-ft burst | A storm cloud you Sustain, choosing each round: Blizzard (4d8 cold, no save), Hail (4d10 bludgeoning, basic Fort), Lightning (7d6 electricity to 10 creatures, basic Ref), or Tornado (throw creatures 40 ft up). Heighten 10th: larger area and range.
