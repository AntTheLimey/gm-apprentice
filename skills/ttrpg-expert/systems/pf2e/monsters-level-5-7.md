# Pathfinder 2e (Remaster) — Monsters Level 5-7

A curated Monster Core selection of 32 mid-tier threats: drakes, trolls
and cyclopes, fiends and genies, hags, major undead, apex animals, and the
first young dragon. Ability text is paraphrased; names, traits, and all game
numbers are as printed.

**Legend** — each block runs four lines:

```text
**Name** — Creature L (traits)
Per +X; AC Y, Fort +a, Ref +b, Will +c; HP Z (immunities / resistances / weaknesses)
Speed; melee/ranged Strikes at bonus and damage; caster tradition if any
Signature abilities, one line each
```

Attack bonuses omit the multiple-attack penalty; ability save DCs are inline.
Spell lists are omitted — note the tradition and look them up in the source.

## Level 5

**Basilisk** — Creature 5 (beast)
Per +11 darkvision; AC 22, Fort +14, Ref +8, Will +11; HP 75 (imm petrified)
Speed 20; jaws +15, 2d8+4 piercing
Gaze slows a target (DC 22 Fort), then petrifies on a repeat failure; a reactive glance slows creatures starting their turn within 30 ft (DC 20 Fort).

**Brimorak** — Creature 5 (demon, fiend, unholy)
Per +12 darkvision; AC 22, Fort +15, Ref +12, Will +10; HP 80 (imm fire; weak cold-iron 5, holy 5)
Speed 30; flaming sword +15, 2d8+4 slashing plus 1d6 fire; hoof +15, 2d4+4 bludgeoning plus 1d6 fire; divine innate spells
Frothing spew (20-ft line, 6d6 fire, DC 21 basic Ref); boiling blood sprays melee attackers; water splashes deal 3d6 mental; belches smoke.

**Cyclops** — Creature 5 (giant, humanoid)
Per +12 low-light; AC 21, Fort +13, Ref +8, Will +12; HP 80
Speed 30; greataxe +14 (reach 10, sweep), 1d12+9 slashing; heavy crossbow +8, 1d10+4 piercing
Flash of Insight (1/day, auto-crit foresight before a d20 roll); Swipe hits two adjacent foes with one attack; Ferocity.

**Flame Drake** — Creature 5 (dragon, fire)
Per +12 darkvision, scent; AC 22, Fort +12, Ref +10, Will +10; HP 75 (imm fire; weak cold 10)
Speed 20, fly 50; fangs +14, 2d8+5 piercing plus 1d6 fire; tail +14 (reach 10), 2d6+5 bludgeoning
Fireball Breath (20-ft burst at 180 ft, 6d6 fire); Draconic Frenzy (two fangs + one tail); Speed Surge 3/day.

**Forest Troll** — Creature 5 (giant, humanoid, troll, wood)
Per +11 darkvision; AC 20, Fort +17, Ref +11, Will +7; HP 125 (weak fire 10, electricity 10)
Speed 30; jaws +14 (reach 10), 2d10+5 piercing; claw +14 (agile, reach 10), 2d8+5 slashing
Regeneration 20 (deactivated by fire/electricity); reactive Furious Flailing when burned or shocked; Chase Prey on all fours.

**Harpy** — Creature 5 (air, beast, humanoid)
Per +12 darkvision; AC 21, Fort +9, Ref +15, Will +12; HP 75
Speed 20, fly 60; talon +15 (agile, finesse), 2d6+4 slashing; jaws +15 (finesse), 2d8+4 piercing
Stench aura (DC 21 Fort, sickened); Hungry Winds pulls prey adjacent (DC 21 Fort); its talons carry Putrid Plague disease.

**Jaathoom** — Creature 5 (air, elemental, genie)
Per +15 darkvision; AC 22, Fort +9, Ref +14, Will +11; HP 55
Speed 25, fly 40; scimitar +15 (reach 10), 1d6+10 slashing; fist +16 (finesse, reach 10), 1d4+10 bludgeoning; arcane innate spells
Permanently invisible (hidden when it attacks); turbulent-skies aura is difficult terrain; Change Shape; Hurricane Blast shoves; sends Ominous Dreams.

**Poltergeist** — Creature 5 (incorporeal, spirit, undead, unholy)
Per +11 darkvision; AC 22, Fort +9, Ref +14, Will +13; HP 55 (imm death, disease, paralyzed, poison, precision, unconscious, bleed; resist all 5)
Speed fly 20; telekinetic object +13, 2d12 damage; occult innate spells
Site-bound; naturally invisible until it Frightens; rejuvenates in 2d4 days unless its anchor is resolved; Telekinetic Storm flings debris.

**Redcap** — Creature 5 (fey)
Per +12 low-light; AC 21, Fort +11, Ref +15, Will +10; HP 60 (weak cold-iron 5)
Speed 50; iron boot +13 (agile), 1d6+8 piercing; halberd +15 (reach 10), 1d10+10 piercing
Fast Healing 10 while wearing its blood-dyed cap (lost with a penalty if the cap is removed); Deadly Cleave on a killing blow; Stomp against the prone.

**Venedaemon** — Creature 5 (daemon, fiend, unholy)
Per +12 darkvision; AC 21, Fort +9, Ref +11, Will +14; HP 75 (imm death; weak holy 5)
Speed 25; tentacle +13 (finesse, reach 10), 2d6+5 bludgeoning; arcane spontaneous + divine innate spells
Telepathy 100 ft; smells active magic; Twisted Whispers stupefies (DC 22 Will); Residual Force adds damage after casting.

**Vordine** — Creature 5 (devil, fiend, unholy)
Per +12 greater darkvision; AC 22, Fort +14, Ref +13, Will +10; HP 60 (imm fire; weak holy 5; resist physical 5, poison 5)
Speed 25; trident +15, 1d8+10 piercing; whip +15 (reach 10, trip), 1d4+10 bludgeoning; hoof +15; divine innate spells
Reactive Strike; Burning Hoofprints leave a burning trail; Trident of Dis extends reach when a fiend ally flanks.

**Yeti** — Creature 5 (humanoid)
Per +15 darkvision, scent; AC 21, Fort +15, Ref +11, Will +13; HP 115 (imm cold; weak fire 10)
Speed 35, climb 20; claw +15, 2d10+5 slashing
Snowblind (concealed in any snowfall); reactive Vanish to break line of sight; Grizzly Arrival ambush bonus; Rend.

## Level 6

**Hydra** — Creature 6 (beast)
Per +17 low-light, scent; AC 23, Fort +15, Ref +12, Will +10; HP 90
Speed 25, swim 25; fangs +16 (reach 10), 2d6+7 piercing
Regeneration 3× its head count; severed heads regrow two-for-one unless cauterized with acid/fire; extra reactive Strike per head; Storm of Jaws hits many targets.

**Iron Hag** — Creature 6 (hag, humanoid)
Per +14 darkvision; AC 24, Fort +16, Ref +12, Will +14; HP 80 (resist physical 3)
Speed 25; claw +16 (agile, cold-iron, reach 10), 2d8+6 slashing; jaws +16 (cold-iron), 2d6+6 piercing; coven spells
Coven leader DC 24; Bonds of Iron cages a foe (1/day); Change Shape (any female humanoid); Embrace of Iron shreds a grabbed victim.

**Jungle Drake** — Creature 6 (dragon, wood)
Per +13 darkvision, scent; AC 23, Fort +17, Ref +13, Will +11; HP 90 (imm disease, paralyzed, poison, sleep)
Speed 20, fly 50; fangs +17, 2d10+7 piercing; stinger +17 (reach 10), 2d6+7 piercing
Jungle Drake Venom (DC 24 Fort, enfeebled); reactive Twisting Tail on nearby movement; Predatory Grab carries prey; Spit Venom at 50 ft.

**Lamia** — Creature 6 (beast, humanoid)
Per +13 darkvision; AC 24, Fort +12, Ref +15, Will +15; HP 95
Speed 40; spear +17, 1d6+10 piercing; tail +16 (agile), 1d6+10 bludgeoning; occult innate spells
Lamia's Caress stupefies on a touch (DC 23 Will) with stacking failures; Grab.

**Manticore (Scorpion Tail)** — Creature 6 (beast)
Per +14 darkvision, scent; AC 23, Fort +16, Ref +12, Will +12; HP 90
Speed 25, fly 40; jaws +17, 2d8+8 piercing; claw +17 (agile), 2d6+8 slashing; stinger +17 (reach 10), 1d8+8 piercing
Manticore Venom (DC 22 Fort, escalating poison and enfeeblement). Quill-tail variant swaps the stinger for ranged spikes.

**Mummy Guardian** — Creature 6 (mummy, undead, unholy)
Per +16 darkvision; AC 23, Fort +14, Ref +10, Will +16; HP 125 (imm bleed, death, disease, paralyzed, poison, unconscious; weak fire 5, alchemical 5)
Speed 20; fist +16 (agile), 2d10+7 bludgeoning
Void Healing; reactive Blighted Consumption fouls food and drink nearby; Choking Pain curses a struck target with despair after a hit.

**Nightmare** — Creature 6 (beast, fiend, unholy)
Per +14 darkvision; AC 24, Fort +15, Ref +15, Will +12; HP 100 (resist fire 10)
Speed 40, fly 90; jaws +16, 2d10+8 piercing; hoof +16 (agile), 1d8+8 bludgeoning plus 1d6 fire; divine innate spells

**Smilodon** — Creature 6 (animal)
Per +14 low-light, scent; AC 23, Fort +15, Ref +12, Will +10; HP 110
Speed 30; fangs +16, 2d10+6 piercing; claw +16 (agile), 2d8+6 slashing
Pounce (Stride + Strike, stays hidden); Pierce Armor knocks a grabbed foe prone; Sneak Attack 1d6 vs off-guard; Grab.

**Vampire Count** — Creature 6 (undead, unholy, vampire)
Per +17 darkvision; AC 24, Fort +11, Ref +14, Will +17; HP 65 (imm death, disease, paralyzed, poison, sleep)
Speed 25, climb 25; rapier +17 (deadly d8), 1d6+11 piercing; claw +17 (agile), 1d8+8 slashing; divine innate spells
Fast Healing 7; Drink Blood heals it; Dominate at will (gaze); Turn to Mist and Mist Escape at 0 HP toward its coffin; Children of the Night.

**Will-o'-Wisp** — Creature 6 (aberration, air)
Per +16 darkvision; AC 27, Fort +10, Ref +16, Will +14; HP 50
Speed fly 50; shock +17, 2d8+4 electricity
Naturally invisible but glows (Go Dark to hide); Magic Immunity to all but a few spells; Feed on Fear heals it near frightened or dying foes.

**Wraith** — Creature 6 (incorporeal, undead, unholy, wraith)
Per +14 darkvision, lifesense 60; AC 24, Fort +8, Ref +14, Will +14; HP 80 (imm death, disease, paralyzed, poison, precision, unconscious, bleed)
Speed fly 40; wraith touch +17 (finesse), 3d8 void
Blinded and slowed in sunlight; Void Healing; Grip of Fear deals 6d6 mental (DC 24 basic Will); Robes of Welcome inflicts a spreading death-curse.

**Wyvern** — Creature 6 (dragon)
Per +13 darkvision, scent; AC 24, Fort +16, Ref +12, Will +13; HP 95 (imm paralyzed, sleep)
Speed 20, fly 60; fangs +17, 2d12+5 piercing; claw +17, 2d8+5 slashing; stinger +15 (agile, reach 10), 2d6+5 piercing
Wyvern Venom (DC 22 Fort); Powerful Dive slams from the air; Savage on a botched Escape; Grab; Reactive Strike.

## Level 7

**Dullahan** — Creature 7 (undead, unholy)
Per +14 lifesense 60; AC 28, Fort +13, Ref +15, Will +17; HP 95 (imm death, disease, fear, paralyzed, poison, unconscious, bleed; weak holy 5)
Speed 20; keen longsword +18, 1d8+10 slashing; returning hatchet +17 (agile), 1d6+10 slashing
Fast Healing 5; Frightful Presence (DC 23 Will); Head Hunter grants Keen/Returning runes; Reap decapitates the dead to terrify; Summon Steed.

**Frost Drake** — Creature 7 (cold, dragon)
Per +14 darkvision, scent; AC 25, Fort +17, Ref +15, Will +14; HP 115 (imm cold, paralyzed, sleep; weak fire 10)
Speed 20, burrow 20, climb 20, fly 50; fangs +17, 2d12+8 piercing plus 1d6 cold; tail +17 (reach 10), 2d10+8 bludgeoning
Freezing Mist Breath (20-ft burst at 60 ft, 8d6 cold); Draconic Frenzy; reactive Retaliatory Strike; Speed Surge.

**Greater Shadow** — Creature 7 (incorporeal, undead, unholy)

Speed fly 30; shadow hand +18 (finesse), 2d10+6 void; divine innate spells

**Jabali** — Creature 7 (earth, elemental, genie)

**Medusa** — Creature 7 (humanoid)
