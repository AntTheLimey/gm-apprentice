# Pathfinder 2e (Remaster) — Monsters (Index)

Level-grouped index for routing. 243 curated creatures from
Monster Core (with a few Monster Core 2 additions), chosen for
table-frequency and role diversity. Compact statblocks live in
the level-band detail files; thousands more creatures live in
the source books and the ORC dataset.

**Detail files:**

| Band | File | Creatures |
|------|------|-----------|
| Level -1 to 1 | `monsters-level-neg1-1.md` | 55 |
| Level 2-4 | `monsters-level-2-4.md` | 60 |
| Level 5-10 | `monsters-level-5-10.md` | 63 |
| Level 11-16 | `monsters-level-11-16.md` | 40 |
| Level 17+ | `monsters-level-17-plus.md` | 25 |

**Statblock format** (4 lines per creature): name/level/traits;
Perception, AC, saves, HP with immunities/resistances/weaknesses;
Speed and Strikes with damage; signature abilities paraphrased.

For encounter math (XP budgets, creature XP by relative level),
read `session-procedures.md`.

## Response Templates

**Monster Lookup** — single-creature questions:

> **Wight** — Creature 3 (undead, unholy) — Monster Core
> Per +10 darkvision; AC 18, Fort +12, Ref +8, Will +10; HP 50 (imm death effects; weak holy water)
> Speed 25; claw +11, 1d6+4 slashing plus drain life
> Drain Life heals the wight and drains a spirit on a hit; slain victims rise as wights.
> Note: pick when the party needs an undead threat that punishes attrition.

Omit the Note line on pure lookups; include it (one sentence)
only when the user asks for encounter advice.
