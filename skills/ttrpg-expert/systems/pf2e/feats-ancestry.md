# Pathfinder 2e (Remaster) — Ancestry Feats

Curated ancestry feats from *Player Core* and *Player Core 2*, the core Remaster
ancestry sources. Covers all sixteen ancestries in those books plus the
versatile heritages, ~8 build-defining picks each (favouring levels 1–9).
Effects are paraphrased summaries — check the source for exact wording.

**Player Core:** Dwarf, Elf, Gnome, Goblin, Halfling, Human, Leshy, Orc.
**Player Core 2:** Catfolk, Hobgoblin, Kholo, Kobold, Lizardfolk, Ratfolk,
Tengu, Tripkee. **Versatile heritages:** Nephilim, Dragonblood, Duskwalker,
Dhampir, Changeling.

The two books hold ~403 ancestry feats; this file curates roughly a third. The
rest — level 13–17 capstones and every feat past ~8 per ancestry — live in the
full ORC dataset. "Prereq" lists feat/skill/heritage prerequisites only; the
level column carries the level requirement, and each feat needs its ancestry.

## Versatile Heritages

Versatile-heritage feats attach to a heritage that a character of any ancestry
can take. The "Prereq" column notes the required lineage or heritage.

| Feat | Level | Prereq | Effect |
|------|-------|--------|--------|
| **Angelkin** | 1 | Nephilim lineage | Trained in Society; learn Empyrean and gain Multilingual. |
| **Bestial Manifestation** | 1 | Nephilim | Gain a claw, hoof, jaws, or tail unarmed attack. |
| **Arcane Dragonblood** | 1 | Dragonblood lineage | Trained in Arcana; gain Arcane Sense. |
| **Breath of the Dragon** | 1 | Dragonblood | *Two actions:* breathe a cone or line of scaling energy damage (recharge 1d4 rounds). |
| **Callow May** | 1 | Changeling lineage | Gain Charming Liar; foes are Off-Guard when you roll Deception for initiative. |
| **Changeling Lore** | 1 | Changeling | Trained in Deception and Occultism; gain Hag Lore. |
| **Chance Death** | 1 | Duskwalker | *Reaction:* reroll a lethal recovery check or save once per day (1st level only). |
| **Deliberate Death** | 1 | Duskwalker | *Reaction:* make a melee Strike just before falling to Dying (1st level only). |
| **Blessed Blood (Nephilim)** | 5 | Nephilim | Your blood deals holy damage to fiends/undead that bite or drink it. |
| **Bloodletting Fangs** | 9 | Dhampir | Fangs crits deal 1d4 persistent bleed damage. |
