# Pathfinder 2e (Remaster) — Monsters Level 2-4

A curated Monster Core selection of 60 creatures for building low-tier
encounters — bruisers, undead, lycanthropes, fiends, celestials, animals,
dinosaurs, oozes, constructs, drakes, hags, and humanoid threats. Numbers are
verbatim from the ORC dataset; ability descriptions are paraphrased.

**Legend:** Each entry is a compact stat block.

- Line 1: **Name** — Creature Level (traits)
- Line 2: Perception; AC, saves (Fort/Ref/Will); HP (immunities / resistances /
  weaknesses)
- Line 3: Speed; melee/ranged attacks with attack bonus and damage; spell
  tradition if a caster
- Line 4+: signature abilities, one line each

Attack traits (agile, finesse, reach, thrown, deadly, etc.) and the void-healing
undead trait carry their standard PF2e meanings. "Void healing" means the
creature is healed by void/negative energy and harmed by vitality/positive.
