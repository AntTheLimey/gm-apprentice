# Pathfinder 2e (Remaster) — Cantrips

32 cantrips (rank-0 spells) from Player Core + Player Core 2. Cantrips are always
cast at your highest spell rank and are never expended.

**Format:** Name | Traditions | Traits | Cast | Range/Area | Effect

**Cast:** action count for casting; most cantrips are 2 actions (verbal + somatic).
**Saves** use a basic save (crit success none, success half, failure full, crit fail double) unless noted.

- **Bullhorn** | Arcane, Divine, Occult | auditory, concentrate, illusion, manipulate | 2 actions | Self (voice 500 ft) | Your voice carries clearly to 500 ft for 10 min without turning harsh; helps Coerce and Perform. No heightening.
- **Caustic Blast** | Arcane, Primal | acid, concentrate, manipulate | 2 actions | 30 ft, 5-ft burst | Bursting acid glob, 1d8 acid, basic Reflex; crit fail adds persistent acid. Heightened +2: +1d8.
- **Daze** | Arcane, Divine, Occult | concentrate, manipulate, mental, nonlethal | 2 actions | 60 ft, 1 target | Mental jolt, 1d6 mental, basic Will; crit fail also Stunned 1. Heightened +2: +1d6.
- **Detect Magic** | Arcane, Divine, Occult, Primal | concentrate, detection, manipulate | 2 actions | 30-ft emanation | Senses whether magic is present nearby (no detail); higher ranks reveal rank/school. Heightened 3rd/9th.
- **Divine Lance** | Divine | attack, concentrate, manipulate, sanctified, spirit | 2 actions | 30 ft | Ranged spell attack for 2d4 spirit (double on crit). Heightened +1: +1d4.
- **Electric Arc** | Arcane, Primal | concentrate, electricity, manipulate | 2 actions | 30 ft, 1–2 targets | Arc of lightning, 2d4 electricity, basic Reflex. Heightened +1: +1d4.
- **Figment** | Arcane, Occult | concentrate, illusion, manipulate, subtle | 2 actions | 30 ft | Creates a minor illusory sound or small crude vision; can aid Create a Diversion (+2 Deception). No heightening.
- **Forbidding Ward** | Divine, Occult | concentrate, manipulate | 2 actions | 30 ft | An ally gains +1 status to AC and saves against one chosen enemy (sustained up to 1 min). Heightened 6th: +2.
- **Frostbite** | Arcane, Primal | cold, concentrate, manipulate | 2 actions | 30 ft | 2d4 cold, basic Fortitude; crit fail adds weakness 1 to bludgeoning. Heightened +1: +1d4.
- **Gale Blast** | Arcane, Primal | air, concentrate, manipulate | 2 actions | 5-ft emanation | Wind swirls around you, 1d6 bludgeoning, Fortitude; failure pushes 5 ft, crit fail 10 ft. Heightened +1: +1d6.
- **Gouging Claw** | Arcane, Primal | attack, concentrate, manipulate, morph | 2 actions | melee (reach) | Morphed claw, melee spell attack for 2d6 slashing or piercing plus 2 persistent bleed. Heightened +1: +1d6.
- **Guidance** | Divine, Occult, Primal | concentrate | 1 action | 30 ft | Grants +1 status to one roll before your next turn ends, then the target is immune 1 hr. No heightening.
- **Haunting Hymn** | Divine, Occult | auditory, concentrate, manipulate, sonic | 2 actions | 15-ft cone | Jarring hymn, 1d8 sonic, basic Fortitude; crit fail Deafened 1 min. Heightened +2: +1d8.
- **Ignition** | Arcane, Primal | attack, concentrate, fire, manipulate | 2 actions | 30 ft or melee | Spell attack, 2d4 fire (d6s if melee); crit adds persistent fire. Heightened +1: +1d4.
- **Know the Way** | Divine, Occult, Primal | concentrate, detection, manipulate | 2 actions | Self | Learn which way is north and the direction to a place you visited in the last 24 hrs. Heightened 3rd/7th: longer recall.
- **Light** | Arcane, Divine, Occult, Primal | concentrate, light, manipulate | 2 actions | 30 ft | Orb sheds 20-ft bright light (20 ft dim); can attach to a creature, sustain to move it. Heightened 4th: brighter/second color.
- **Live Wire** | Arcane, Primal | attack, concentrate, electricity, manipulate, metal | 2 actions | 30 ft | Copper filament, spell attack for 1d4 slashing + 1d4 electricity; crit adds persistent electricity. Heightened +2.
- **Message** | Arcane, Divine, Occult | auditory, concentrate, illusion, linguistic, mental, subtle | 1 action | 120 ft | Whisper a private message to one seen creature; they may reply briefly. Heightened 3rd: greater range.
- **Prestidigitation** | Arcane, Divine, Occult, Primal | concentrate, manipulate | 2 actions | 10 ft | Minor tricks while sustained: cook, lift, make a fragile trinket, or tidy. No heightening.
- **Puff of Poison** | Arcane, Primal | concentrate, manipulate, poison | 2 actions | 30 ft | Toxic breath, 1d4 poison plus persistent poison, Fortitude. Heightened +2: +1d4 each.
- **Read Aura** | Arcane, Divine, Occult, Primal | concentrate, detection, manipulate | 2 actions | 30 ft | Tells whether an item is magical and grants +2 to Identify Magic on it. Heightened 3rd/6th: more objects.
- **Scatter Scree** | Arcane, Primal | concentrate, earth, manipulate | 2 actions | 30 ft, 5-ft burst | Rockfall, 2d4 bludgeoning, basic Reflex; leaves difficult terrain. Heightened +1: +1d4.
- **Shield** | Arcane, Divine, Occult | concentrate, force | 1 action | Self | +1 circumstance AC until your next turn; can Shield Block (Hardness 5), then 10-min cooldown. Heightened: higher Hardness.
- **Sigil** | Arcane, Divine, Occult, Primal | concentrate, manipulate | 1 action | Touch | Mark a creature or object with your personal sigil (visible or hidden); fades over a week. Heightened 3rd/5th: lasts longer.
- **Spout** | Arcane, Primal | concentrate, manipulate, water | 2 actions | 30 ft, 5-ft burst | Geyser of water, 2d4 bludgeoning, basic Reflex; crit fail Off-Guard. Heightened +1: +1d4.
- **Stabilize** | Divine, Primal | concentrate, healing, manipulate, vitality | 2 actions | 30 ft | Removes the Dying condition from a creature at 0 HP (it stays Unconscious). No heightening.
- **Summon Instrument** | Arcane, Divine, Occult | concentrate, manipulate | 1 action | — | Conjures a handheld musical instrument that plays only for you. Heightened 5th: virtuoso instrument.
- **Tangle Vine** | Arcane, Primal | attack, concentrate, manipulate, plant, wood | 2 actions | 30 ft | Vine lashes a target (spell attack): Speed penalty, or Immobilized on crit; Escape vs your DC. No heightening.
- **Telekinetic Hand** | Arcane, Occult | concentrate, manipulate | 2 actions | 30 ft | Floating force hand levitates a light object up to 20 ft; sustain to move it further. Heightened 3rd/5th/7th: more Bulk/range.
- **Telekinetic Projectile** | Arcane, Occult | attack, concentrate, manipulate | 2 actions | 30 ft | Hurl an unattended object (spell attack) for 2d6 bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing. Heightened +1: +1d6.
- **Vitality Lash** | Divine, Primal | concentrate, manipulate, vitality | 2 actions | 30 ft | 2d6 vitality, basic Fortitude; crit fail also Enfeebled 1. Heightened +1: +1d6.
- **Void Warp** | Arcane, Divine, Occult | concentrate, manipulate, void | 2 actions | 30 ft | 2d4 void, basic Fortitude; crit fail also Enfeebled 1. Heightened +1: +1d4.
