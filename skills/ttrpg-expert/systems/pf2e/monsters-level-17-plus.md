# Pathfinder 2e (Remaster) — Monsters Level 17+

A curated selection of 25 apex threats from Pathfinder Monster Core (with a
few gaps filled from Monster Core 2) — ancient dragons, fiend generals,
linnorms, and campaign-capstone monsters for levels 17 to 25. Use these as
solo bosses or the centerpiece of a climactic encounter.

## Legend

- **Creature N (traits)** — level and verbatim traits. All game numbers
  (AC, HP, saves, attack bonuses, damage dice, DCs) are quoted from the
  source; every ability description is paraphrased.
- **Per** = Perception, with special senses in parentheses. Saves are Fort/Ref/Will.
- Attacks list to-hit bonus then damage; **P** piercing, **S** slashing, **B** bludgeoning.
- **Basic save** — PF2e degrees: crit fail = double, fail = full, success =
  half, crit success = none.
- **Recharge Nd4** — a breath weapon is usable again after that many rounds.
- **Caster** — the creature casts innate spells of the noted tradition; consult
  the source for its spell list and DC.

## Level 17

**Conspirator Dragon (Ancient)** — Creature 17 (dragon, occult)
