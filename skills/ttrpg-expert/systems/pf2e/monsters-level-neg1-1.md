# Pathfinder 2e (Remaster) — Monsters Level -1 to 1

A curated selection of 55 common creatures from Pathfinder Monster Core,
spanning creature levels -1 through 1. Chosen for table utility and role
diversity: animals, goblinoids, kobolds, undead, giant vermin, low fey,
fiends, elementals, and humanoid rank-and-file. Numbers are printed as
published; ability text is paraphrased.

**Legend:** `Per` = Perception (senses in parens). Defenses read AC, then
Fortitude / Reflex / Will saves, then HP with any immunities (imm),
resistances (res), or weaknesses (weak). Attack lines give the modifier and
damage, with weapon traits in parens — agile, finesse, reach-N, thrown-N,
deadly-dN, propulsive, reload-N, forceful, sweep, versatile, and so on.
"Innate/spontaneous spells" flags a caster; consult Monster Core for the full
list and DCs. Damage codes: B bludgeoning, P piercing, S slashing.
