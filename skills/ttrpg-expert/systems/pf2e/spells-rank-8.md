# Pathfinder 2e (Remaster) — Rank 8 Spells

Player Core + Player Core 2 spells (ORC). Mechanics are facts; all effect text is paraphrased.

**Format:** Name | Traditions | Traits | Cast | Range/Area/Targets | Effect (damage · save · heighten). **Cast:** number = actions, R = reaction, else a listed time. **Saves:** Fort/Ref/Will.

- **Arctic Rift** | Arcane/Primal | cold, concentrate, manipulate | 2 | 60-ft line | 12d8 cold, basic Fort; failures Slowed 1, crit-fail Immobilized in ice (60 HP, fire vulnerability 10). Heighten +1: +1d8 and +5 ice HP.
- **Canticle of Everlasting Grief** | Divine/Occult | auditory, concentrate, curse, emotion, fear, manipulate, mental | 2 | 30 ft, 1 creature | 10d6 mental, Will; the curse blocks circumstance and status bonuses and inflicts Frightened, escalating on a crit-fail to an unlimited curse that spreads to nearby allies.
- **Confusing Colors** | Arcane/Occult | illusion, incapacitation, manipulate, subtle, visual | 2 | 120 ft, 10-ft burst | A shifting cloud Dazzles creatures inside and within 20 ft; a Will save on entering or ending a turn in it wards off Blinded/Stunned effects on failure.
- **Desiccate** | Arcane/Primal | concentrate, manipulate, void | 2 | 30-ft cone | 10d10 void, basic Fort; water and plant creatures fare one degree worse, moisture-less creatures are immune. Heighten +1: +1d10.
- **Disappearance** | Arcane/Occult | illusion, manipulate, subtle | 2 | Touch, 1 creature | Target becomes Invisible to every precise sense, not just sight, for 10 minutes; Seeking can still locate it indirectly.
- **Divine Inspiration** | Divine | concentrate, manipulate, mental | 2 | Touch, 1 creature | Refreshes a caster's magic: recover one spent 6th-rank-or-lower prepared spell or slot, or restore a focus pool as if it had Refocused.
- **Dream Council** | Arcane/Occult | concentrate, illusion, manipulate, mental, sleep | 1 min | Planetary, chosen creatures | Willing targets fall asleep into a shared dream where they converse as if in the same room; waking removes a sleeper.
- **Earthquake** | Arcane/Primal | concentrate, earth, manipulate | 2 | 500 ft, 60-ft burst | Ground becomes difficult terrain (-2 to attacks, AC, skills), Reflex each turn to avoid 40-ft fissures, and structures may collapse for 11d6 bludgeoning, basic Ref. Heighten 10th: half-mile range, quarter-mile burst.
- **Migration** | Primal | concentrate, manipulate, polymorph | 2 | 30 ft, willing creatures | Targets take fitting animal forms gaining land, burrow, climb, fly, and swim Speed 40 ft, temperature immunities, and 20-mph travel, but can't Strike or cast while shifted.
- **Moment of Renewal** | Divine/Primal | concentrate, healing, manipulate | 2 | Touch, up to 6 creatures | Grants an instant day of recovery: ends 24-hour effects and heals/recovers as if rested (without re-preparation), then immune for 1 day.
- **Monstrosity Form** | Arcane/Primal | concentrate, manipulate, polymorph | 2 | Self | Become a Huge phoenix, cave worm, or sea serpent (AC 20+level, 20 temp HP, +28 attacks, +30 Athletics) with form-specific strikes and abilities. Heighten 9th: better stats and +1 damage die.
- **Pinpoint** | Arcane/Divine/Occult | concentrate, detection, manipulate | 10 min | Planetary | Uncommon. Learn a target's exact location, community, and plane if you've seen it or hold a piece of it; auto-overcomes lower-rank protections against detection.
- **Punishing Winds** | Primal | air, concentrate, manipulate | 2 | 100 ft; 30-ft-radius, 100-ft cylinder | A cyclone forces flyers down 40 ft, turns the area into (greater) difficult terrain, and walls creatures in behind Athletics or Acrobatics checks. Sustained.
- **Quandary** | Arcane/Occult | concentrate, extradimensional, manipulate, teleportation | 2 | 30 ft, 1 creature | Banish a foe into an extraplanar puzzle room; once per turn it may attempt Occultism, Perception, or Thievery vs your DC to solve its way out.
- **Spirit Song** | Divine/Occult | concentrate, manipulate, spirit | 2 | 60-ft cone | 14d6 spirit, basic Fort, momentarily freezing bodies; penetrates into but not through barriers, harming incorporeal creatures hiding within them. Heighten +1: +2d6.
- **Spiritual Epidemic** | Divine/Occult | concentrate, curse, manipulate, spirit | 2 | 30 ft, 1 creature | Will save; a contagious curse that also afflicts anyone who later casts a divine or occult spell on the target, spreading onward.
- **Uncontrollable Dance** | Arcane/Occult | concentrate, incapacitation, manipulate, mental | 2 | 30 ft, 1 creature | The target is compelled to dance: Off-Guard, unable to use reactions, and limited to Striding at half Speed. Duration scales with the save.
- **Unrelenting Observation** | Arcane/Occult | concentrate, manipulate, scrying | 2 | 30 ft; 20-ft burst | Up to five allies perfectly track one chosen creature or object through most barriers, ignoring concealment and invisibility; unwilling targets get a Will save, willing ones last 1 hour.
