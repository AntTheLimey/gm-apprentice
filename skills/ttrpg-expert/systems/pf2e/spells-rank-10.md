# Pathfinder 2e (Remaster) — Rank 10 Spells

Player Core + Player Core 2 spells (ORC). Mechanics are facts; all effect text is paraphrased.

**Format:** Name | Traditions | Traits | Cast | Range/Area/Targets | Effect (damage · save · heighten). **Cast:** number = actions, R = reaction, else a listed time. **Saves:** Fort/Ref/Will.

- **Avatar** | Divine | concentrate, manipulate, polymorph | 2 | Self | Transform into a Huge avatar of your deity (AC 25+level, 30 temp HP, +33 attacks, +35 Athletics) that retains hands for manipulate actions, with deity-specific movement and abilities.
- **Cataclysm** | Arcane/Primal | acid, air, cold, concentrate, earth, electricity, fire, manipulate, water | 3 | 500 ft, 60-ft burst | Seven disasters strike at once for 3d10 each (acid, quake/wind/tsunami bludgeoning, cold, electricity, fire), one basic Ref, treating targets' resistances as 10 lower.
- **Fabricated Truth** | Occult | concentrate, incapacitation, manipulate, mental | 2 | 30 ft, up to 10 creatures | Implant a chosen statement as unshakable belief; targets reshape behavior around the "truth" per their Will save, and recasting can counteract an existing Fabricated Truth.
- **Freeze Time** | Arcane/Occult | concentrate, manipulate | 2 | Self | Halt time for all but you, taking up to 9 actions in three sets of three; only you and effects you create feel the passing rounds, and others can't be targeted until time resumes.
- **Gate** | Arcane/Divine/Occult | concentrate, manipulate, teleportation | 3 | 120 ft | Uncommon. Tear open a 40-ft-radius two-way portal to a plane you can clearly picture; a deity or comparable power can prevent a gate into its realm.
- **Indestructibility** | Arcane/Divine/Occult/Primal | concentrate, manipulate | 2 | Self | Sever yourself from cause and effect, becoming immune to all damage and effects (except artifacts, deific power, and similar), while selectively allowing anything you choose.
- **Manifestation** | Arcane/Divine/Occult/Primal | concentrate, manipulate | varies | Varies | Duplicate any 9th-rank-or-lower spell of your casting tradition, or any 7th-rank-or-lower spell of any tradition; casting time and targets match the copied spell.
- **Nature Incarnate** | Primal | concentrate, manipulate, polymorph | 2 | Self | Become a Medium green man or Gargantuan kaiju battle form (AC 25+level, 30 temp HP, +34 attacks, +36 Athletics) with form-specific reach attacks and abilities.
- **Primal Herd** | Primal | concentrate, manipulate, polymorph | 2 | 30 ft, willing creatures | Transform targets into Huge mammoths (AC 22+level, 20 temp HP, Speed 40 ft) with tusk, trunk, and foot Strikes and the Trample action.
- **Remake** | Arcane/Divine/Occult/Primal | concentrate, manipulate | 1 hr | 1 remnant | Uncommon. Fully re-create a non-artifact object of level 20 or lower from even a speck of its remnant, restoring constant (not temporary) magical properties.
- **Revival** | Divine/Primal | concentrate, healing, manipulate, vitality | 3 | 30-ft emanation | Heal every living target 10d8+40 HP and temporarily return the recently dead to life (as Raise Dead) with temp HP that vanish when the spell ends; no effect on the Disintegrated or death-slain.
