# Pathfinder 2e (Remaster) — Monsters Level 8-10

A curated Monster Core selection of 31 upper-mid-tier threats: young dragons,
true giants, daemons and devils, elementals and genies, constructs, powerful
undead, sea monsters, and megafauna. Ability text is paraphrased; names,
traits, and all game numbers are as printed.

**Legend** — each block runs four lines:

```text
**Name** — Creature L (traits)
Per +X; AC Y, Fort +a, Ref +b, Will +c; HP Z (immunities / resistances / weaknesses)
Speed; melee/ranged Strikes at bonus and damage; caster tradition if any
Signature abilities, one line each
```

Attack bonuses omit the multiple-attack penalty; ability save DCs are inline.
Spell lists are omitted — note the tradition and look them up in the source.
