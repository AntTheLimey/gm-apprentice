# Pathfinder 2e (Remaster) — Ancestries, Heritages & Backgrounds

Character origin in PF2e comes from three choices made at level 1: an **ancestry** (the lineage your character is born into), a **heritage** (a sub-lineage refining that ancestry), and a **background** (life experience before adventuring). This reference covers all 31 ancestries in the ORC dataset, detailed heritage options for the Player Core and Player Core 2 ancestries, and a curated set of Player Core backgrounds.

## How ancestry works

An ancestry sets a character's baseline body and grants a small package of traits:

- **Hit Points** — a flat amount added to your total at level 1 (typically 6, 8, or 10).
- **Size** — usually Small or Medium; this affects reach, squeezing, and some effects.
- **Speed** — base land Speed in feet (commonly 25; Dwarves move 20).
- **Attribute boosts** — most ancestries grant two boosts (a bonus to an attribute modifier), one or both of which may be fixed, plus at least one free boost you assign anywhere.
- **Attribute flaw** — some ancestries impose one flaw (a penalty to a specific attribute); many Remaster ancestries have none.
- **Languages** — a set of starting languages, often plus bonus languages equal to your Intelligence modifier.
- **Traits** — always the ancestry's own trait plus `humanoid` (or another type such as `plant` for leshys).
- **Special senses/abilities** — such as darkvision or low-light vision.

At level 1 you also choose a **heritage** from your ancestry, which layers on an extra ability. You then gain an **ancestry feat** at level 1 and again at **levels 5, 9, 13, and 17**, letting the lineage grow more distinct as you advance.

## Detailed ancestries

Each block lists the standard stat line, signature senses, the ancestry's heritage options (one line each, paraphrased), and a note on typical builds.

### Dwarf

**HP 10 · Medium · Speed 20 ft · Boosts Con, Wis, Free · Flaw Cha · Darkvision**

Stout, tradition-minded folk suited to defensive, resilient builds. Heritages:

- **Ancient-Blooded** — inherited resistance to magic, gained as a defensive reaction.
- **Death Warden** — successes against void or undead effects become critical successes.
- **Forge** — fire resistance scaling with level and reduced severity of environmental heat.
- **Rock** — hard to move: bonuses against forced movement and Reposition/Shove/Trip.
- **Strong-Blooded** — poison resistance and faster recovery from poison afflictions.

Typical builds lean into Fighter, Champion, or Cleric — durable front-liners who exploit high Con and Wis.

### Elf

**HP 6 · Medium · Speed 30 ft · Boosts Dex, Int, Free · Flaw Con · Low-light vision**

Long-lived and quick, with the fastest base Speed among the core ancestries. Heritages:

- **Ancient** — centuries of dabbling grant a multiclass dedication feat early.
- **Aquatic** — amphibious with a swim Speed; must periodically rest underwater.
- **Arctic** — cold resistance and reduced severity of environmental cold.
- **Cavern** — raised underground, granting darkvision.
- **Seer** — innate Detect Magic at will plus bonuses to identify magic.
- **Whisper** — sharpened hearing improves finding hidden or undetected creatures.
- **Woodland** — climbs foliage swiftly and can Take Cover in forests.

Typical builds favor Wizard, Rogue, or Ranger — mobile skirmishers and casters keyed off Dex or Int.

### Gnome

**HP 8 · Small · Speed 25 ft · Boosts Con, Cha, Free · Flaw Str · Low-light vision**

Curious, fey-touched folk drawn to novelty and magic. Heritages:

- **Chameleon** — shifting skin color grants a Stealth bonus when matching surroundings.
- **Fey-Touched** — gain the fey trait and an at-will primal cantrip you can swap daily.
- **Kijimuna** — Tian Xia lineage granting either exceptional climbing or a swim Speed.
- **Sensate** — imprecise scent and a bonus to pinpoint undetected creatures by smell.
- **Umbral** — connection to shadow or the deep grants darkvision.
- **Wellspring** — an alternate tradition supplies an at-will cantrip and re-flavors innate spells.

Typical builds favor Bard, Sorcerer, or Druid — Charisma casters who lean on innate magic.

### Goblin

**HP 6 · Small · Speed 25 ft · Boosts Dex, Cha, Free · Flaw Wis · Darkvision**

Fast, scrappy survivors. Heritages:

- **Charhide** — fire resistance and easier recovery from persistent fire.
- **Dokkaebi** — an at-will illusion cantrip plus a bonus against illusions.
- **Irongut** — can stomach spoiled food and resist ingested afflictions.
- **Razortooth** — a jaws unarmed attack dealing piercing damage.
- **Snow** — cold resistance and reduced severity of environmental cold.
- **Unbreakable** — 10 HP from ancestry instead of 6 and reduced falling damage.

Typical builds favor Rogue, Alchemist, or Sorcerer — nimble, high-Dex characters.

### Halfling

**HP 6 · Small · Speed 25 ft · Boosts Dex, Wis, Free · No flaw · Keen Eyes**

Plucky, lucky wanderers. Heritages:

- **Gutsy** — successes against emotion effects become critical successes.
- **Hillock** — recover extra HP overnight and when treated with a snack.
- **Jinxed** — forgo halfling luck to instead hex others with the Jinx action.
- **Nomadic** — extra languages, with more gained each time you take Multilingual.
- **Twilight** — dusk-adapted eyes grant low-light vision.
- **Wildwood** — ignore difficult terrain caused by plants and fungi.

Typical builds favor Cleric, Ranger, or Rogue — reliable, self-sufficient support characters.

### Human

**HP 8 · Medium · Speed 25 ft · Boosts two Free · No flaw**

The most flexible ancestry, with two free boosts that fit any class. Heritages:

- **Skilled** — trained in a chosen skill, becoming an expert in it at level 5.
- **Versatile** — a general feat of your choice at character creation.
- *(The Beginner Box also offers Ambitious and Battle-Trained heritages with non-standard features.)*

Typical builds fit any class; the free boosts and heritage flexibility make Human the default "build anything" choice.

### Leshy

**HP 8 · Small · Speed 25 ft · Boosts Con, Wis, Free · Flaw Int · Low-light vision · Plant**

Small plant-spirit constructs animated by primal magic. Heritages:

- **Cactus** — a spine unarmed attack dealing piercing damage.
- **Chrysanthemum** — poison-save bonus and a daily brewed antidote.
- **Fruit** — grows a daily healing fruit an ally can eat.
- **Fungus** — gain darkvision and the fungus trait in place of plant.
- **Gourd** — a hollow head that stores objects securely.
- **Leaf** — take no damage from falling.
- **Lotus** — walk on the surface of still water.
- **Peachchild** — commune with and befriend household animals and livestock.
- **Root** — 10 HP from ancestry, sunlight tolerance, and resistance to forced movement.
- **Seaweed** — swim Speed and constant underwater breathing at a slight land-Speed cost.
- **Vine** — climb with no hands free and turn climb successes into criticals.

Typical builds favor Druid, Champion, or Ranger — nature-themed characters who lean on Wisdom.

### Orc

**HP 10 · Medium · Speed 25 ft · Boosts two Free · No flaw · Darkvision**

Hardy, powerful folk built to take and deal punishment. Heritages:

- **Badlands** — sustained Hustle while exploring and reduced heat severity.
- **Battle-Ready** — trained in Intimidation with the Intimidating Glare feat.
- **Deep** — Terrain Expertise (underground) and Combat Climber.
- **Grave** — void resistance and a save bonus against death and void effects.
- **Hold-Scarred** — 12 HP from ancestry instead of 10 and the Diehard feat.
- **Rainfall** — bonuses to Climb, Swim, and disease saves.
- **Winter** — trained in Survival with reduced cold severity.

Typical builds favor Barbarian, Fighter, or Champion — heavy-hitting front-liners with two free boosts.

### Catfolk

**HP 8 · Medium · Speed 25 ft · Boosts Dex, Cha, Free · No flaw · Low-light vision**

Agile, curious feline folk who always land on their feet. Heritages:

- **Clawed** — a claw unarmed attack (agile, finesse) dealing slashing damage.
- **Hunting** — imprecise scent plus a bonus to track by smell.
- **Jungle** — ignore or reduce difficult terrain from undergrowth.
- **Liminal** — an at-will occult Detect Magic and bonus to recall planar creatures.
- **Nine Lives** — easier recovery while Dying plus the Diehard feat.
- **Sharp-Eared** — bonus to locate creatures by sound and free Point Out.
- **Winter** — cold resistance and reduced cold severity.

Typical builds favor Rogue, Swashbuckler, or Bard — nimble, high-Charisma skirmishers.

### Hobgoblin

**HP 8 · Medium · Speed 25 ft · Boosts Con, Int, Free · No flaw · Darkvision**

Disciplined martial folk bred for war. Heritages:

- **Elfbane** — a reaction granting resistance to magic ("elf magic").
- **Runtboss** — Group Coercion and improved Intimidation against goblins.
- **Shortshanks** — the Ride feat and no off-guard penalty while climbing.
- **Smokeworker** — fire resistance and easy targeting of smoke-concealed creatures.
- **Warmarch** — sustained Hustle and reliable foraging on the march.
- **Warrenbred** — echo-sensitive ears aid targeting concealed foes underground.

Typical builds favor Fighter, Investigator, or Wizard — tactical characters using high Con and Int.

### Kholo

**HP 8 · Medium · Speed 25 ft · Boosts Con, Int, Free · No flaw · Low-light vision**

Hyena-like pack folk (formerly gnolls) with a natural bite. Heritages:

- **Ant** — Small size, trained in Deception, with bonuses to lie about innocence.
- **Cave** — dark-adapted eyes grant darkvision.
- **Dog** — drop to all fours to move at 30 feet with both hands free.
- **Great** — 10 HP from ancestry and a bonus to Reposition, Shove, or Trip.
- **Sweetbreath** — pleasant breath aids Diplomacy at close range.
- **Winter** — cold resistance and reduced cold severity.
- **Witch** — an at-will occult Figment cantrip and vocal-trick bonuses.

Typical builds favor Ranger, Rogue, or Investigator — pack-minded skirmishers with a bite attack.

### Kobold

**HP 6 · Small · Speed 25 ft · Boosts Dex, Free · Darkvision**

Small draconic reptiles shaped by the creature they hatched near. Heritages:

- **Cavernstalker** — climb natural stone quickly and squeeze deftly.
- **Dragonscaled** — 10 HP from ancestry and saves against dragon breath, sleep, and paralysis.
- **Elementheart** — resistance to an elemental damage type of your choice.
- **Heavenscribe** — speak Draconic; failed social criticals become ordinary failures.
- **Mightyfall** — 10 HP and an alternate Str/Cha boost with an Int flaw.
- **Spellhorn** — an at-will arcane cantrip with Charisma spellcasting.
- **Strongjaw** — a jaws unarmed attack dealing piercing damage.
- **Tunnelflood** — a swim Speed.
- **Venomtail** — a tail spur delivering venom via the Tail Toxin action.

Typical builds favor Sorcerer, Rogue, or Alchemist — trap-savvy, high-Dex characters.

### Lizardfolk

**HP 8 · Medium · Speed 25 ft · Boosts Str, Wis, Free · No flaw**

Cold-blooded reptilian folk with strong survival instincts. Heritages:

- **Bakuwa** — natural bony plates that function as etchable medium armor.
- **Cliffscale** — Combat Climber and sticky-footed climbing with hands free.
- **Cloudleaper** — skin flaps let you avoid falling damage.
- **Frilled** — a visual Demoralize that ignores language plus Threatening Approach.
- **Makari** — lizardfolk spells become divine, with a divine cantrip granted.
- **Sandstrider** — fire resistance and drought endurance, at the cost of worse cold.
- **Unseen** — color-shifting skin grants a Stealth bonus when matching terrain.
- **Wetlander** — a swim Speed.
- **Woodstalker** — Take Cover in forests and Terrain Stalker in underbrush.

Typical builds favor Champion, Druid, or Monk — sturdy, Wisdom-forward characters.

### Ratfolk

**HP 6 · Small · Speed 25 ft · Boosts Dex, Int, Free · Low-light vision**

Small, clever, communal rodent folk. Heritages:

- **Deep** — subterranean lineage granting darkvision.
- **Desert** — run on all fours at 30 feet and endure heat and thirst, at the cost of cold.
- **Longsnout** — imprecise scent plus a bonus to Seek by smell.
- **Sewer** — immunity to putrid plague and improved disease/poison saves.
- **Shadow** — trained in Intimidation and able to Demoralize animals.
- **Snow** — cold resistance and reduced cold severity.
- **Tunnel** — Quick Squeeze and easier movement through tight spaces.

Typical builds favor Alchemist, Inventor, or Rogue — inventive, high-Int skirmishers.

### Tengu

**HP 6 · Medium · Speed 25 ft · Boosts Dex, Free · Low-light vision**

Corvid folk with a sharp beak and a knack for adaptation. Heritages:

- **Dogtooth** — the beak gains the deadly d8 trait.
- **Jinxed** — curse-save successes become criticals and a chance to reduce Doomed.
- **Mountainkeeper** — an at-will Vitality Lash cantrip switchable between divine and primal.
- **Skyborn** — take no damage from falling.
- **Stormtossed** — electricity resistance and easy targeting through rain or fog.
- **Taloned** — a talons unarmed attack dealing slashing damage.
- **Wavediver** — a swim Speed.

Typical builds favor Swashbuckler, Monk, or Bard — versatile, Dex-based characters.

### Tripkee

**HP 6 · Small · Speed 25 ft · Boosts Dex, Wis, Free · Sticky climbing**

Small amphibian folk (formerly grippli) adapted to wetlands. Heritages:

- **Camouflage** — hide or sneak in a chosen terrain without cover or concealment.
- **Poisonhide** — a reaction coating you in defensive toxin.
- **Riverside** — a swim Speed.
- **Snaptongue** — a long tongue delivers touch spells and simple Interact actions.
- **Stickytoe** — bonuses against forced movement and improved tree climbing.
- **Thickskin** — 8 HP from ancestry instead of 6 and a bonus to disease/poison saves.
- **Windweb** — webbing between the digits negates falling damage.

Typical builds favor Ranger, Druid, or Rogue — stealthy, wilderness-adapted skirmishers.

## Backgrounds

A background represents what your character did before adventuring. Every background grants the same three things:

- **Two attribute boosts** — one chosen from a listed pair of attributes, and one free boost assigned anywhere. (You can't apply both to the same attribute.)
- **Skill training** — trained proficiency in one regular skill plus one **Lore** skill (a narrow specialty such as Warfare Lore or Underworld Lore).
- **A skill feat** — a level-1 skill feat tied to the background's theme.

Backgrounds carry no attribute flaw and no HP. The table below curates 25 common Player Core backgrounds; the dataset holds many more (see Coverage).

## Coverage note

**Detailed here:** all 8 Player Core ancestries (Dwarf, Elf, Gnome, Goblin, Halfling, Human, Leshy, Orc) and all 8 Player Core 2 ancestries (Catfolk, Hobgoblin, Kholo, Kobold, Lizardfolk, Ratfolk, Tengu, Tripkee) — with stat lines, senses, and every heritage listed.

**Table-only here:** the 15 uncommon and rare ancestries from *Howl of the Wild*, *Guns & Gears*, *Battlecry!*, *Lost Omens Draconic Codex*, and the *Lost Omens Tian Xia Character Guide*. Their full stat blocks are not carried in this ORC ancestry dataset; use the cited sourcebook for HP, Size, Speed, boosts, and flaw.

**In the dataset:** the source repository holds all 31 ancestry entries (`data/ancestries/`, flavor text), all 203 heritages organized per ancestry (`data/heritages/<ancestry>/`, including versatile heritages like Nephilim, Dhampir, and Changeling that any ancestry can take), and all 202 backgrounds (`data/backgrounds/`, including 40 Player Core backgrounds plus Adventure Path–specific options). Everything paraphrased above is drawn from those files.
