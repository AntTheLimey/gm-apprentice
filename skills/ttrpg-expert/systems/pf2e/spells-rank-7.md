# Pathfinder 2e (Remaster) — Rank 7 Spells

Player Core + Player Core 2 spells (ORC). Mechanics are facts; all effect text is paraphrased.

**Format:** Name | Traditions | Traits | Cast | Range/Area/Targets | Effect (damage · save · heighten). **Cast:** number = actions, R = reaction, else a listed time. **Saves:** Fort/Ref/Will.

- **Contingency** | Arcane | concentrate, manipulate | 10 min | Self | Pre-store a 4th-rank-or-lower spell that can affect you, then trigger it on yourself as a reaction under a Ready-style condition you set; recasting replaces the stored spell.
- **Divine Decree** | Divine | concentrate, manipulate, sanctified, spirit | 2 | 40-ft emanation | 7d10 spirit to enemies, basic Fort; failures Enfeebled 2, crit-fail can banish, paralyze, or slay low-level foes on your home plane. Heighten +1: +1d10.
- **Duplicate Foe** | Arcane/Occult | concentrate, manipulate | 2 | 30 ft, 1 creature | Fort resists; conjures a 70-HP minion copy of the target (its attacks, AC, and saves but no special abilities) that fights for you and decays 4d6 HP each turn. Sustained up to 1 min.
- **Eclipse Burst** | Arcane/Divine/Primal | cold, concentrate, darkness, manipulate, void | 2 | 500 ft, 60-ft burst | 8d10 cold plus 8d4 void to living, basic Ref; also counteracts magical light in the area. Heighten +1: +1d10 cold, +1d4 void.
- **Energy Aegis** | Arcane/Divine/Occult/Primal | concentrate, manipulate | 2 | Touch, 1 creature | Grants resistance 5 to acid, cold, electricity, fire, force, sonic, vitality, and void for 1 day. Heighten +2: resistance increases by 5.
- **Execute** | Divine/Primal | concentrate, death, manipulate, void | 2 | 30 ft, 1 living creature | 70 void, basic Fort; deals vitality instead against void-healing or undead targets. Heighten +1: +10 damage.
- **Fiery Body** | Arcane/Primal | concentrate, fire, manipulate, polymorph | 2 | Self (1 min) | Become living flame: fire immunity, precision resistance 10, cold/water weakness 5, 3d6 fire retaliation on touch, +1d4 fire on unarmed strikes, a bonus fire-spell die, and fly 40 ft. Heighten 9th: larger bonuses.
- **Interplanar Teleport** | Arcane/Divine/Occult/Primal | concentrate, manipulate, teleportation | 3 | You + willing creatures | Uncommon. Travel to another plane using a planar key as focus; arrival is imprecise (about 1d20×25 miles off) and offers no built-in return trip.
- **Lifewood Cage** | Arcane/Primal | concentrate, manipulate, vitality, wood | 2 | 120 ft, 20-ft cube | Player Core 2. Ref save; failures are trapped in an invisible wooden prison (AC 10, Hardness 20, 40 HP, void weakness 5) that blocks incorporeal undead, while successes are pushed clear.
- **Mask of Terror** | Arcane/Occult/Primal | concentrate, emotion, fear, illusion, manipulate, mental, visual | 2 | 30 ft, 1 creature (1 min) | The target appears monstrous; any creature acting against it must pass a Will save or become Frightened 2, with a crit-fail also wasting that action.
- **Planar Palace** | Arcane/Occult | concentrate, extradimensional, manipulate | 2 | 30 ft | Uncommon. Grow an extradimensional mansion (up to 40 ft wide, 40 deep, 30 tall) with a concealable invisible door, 24 servants, and food for a banquet of 150.
- **Planar Seal** | Arcane/Divine/Occult | concentrate, manipulate | 2 | 500 ft, 60-ft burst | Uncommon. Raises a barrier that counteracts teleportation, planar travel, and summoning into or out of the area.
- **Possession** | Occult | concentrate, incapacitation, manipulate, mental, possession | 2 | 30 ft, 1 creature | Uncommon. Will save; project into a foe's body to control it while your own body lies unconscious, risking Paralysis if the host dies.
- **Project Image** | Arcane/Occult | concentrate, illusion, manipulate, mental | 2 | 30 ft | Create an illusory double sharing your AC and saves that you can cast non-emanation spells through; a single hit or failed save ends it.
- **Regenerate** | Divine/Primal | concentrate, healing, manipulate, vitality | 2 | 30 ft, 1 creature | Grants regeneration 15 (heals 15 HP each turn, regrows organs and reattaches limbs, staves off death); acid or fire suspends it briefly. Heighten 9th: regeneration 20.
- **Retrocognition** | Arcane/Occult | concentrate, manipulate | 1 min | Self | Read psychic impressions of past events at your location, one day of history per minute of concentration; witnessing a traumatic echo forces a Will save or the spell ends.
- **Spell Riposte** | Arcane/Divine/Occult | manipulate | R | Trigger: targeted by a spell you notice | Player Core 2. Attempt to counteract the triggering spell; if it would be counteracted, it instead redirects to strike its own caster (targeted spells only).
- **Sunburst** | Divine/Primal | concentrate, fire, light, manipulate, vitality | 3 | 500 ft, 60-ft burst | 8d10 fire to all creatures plus rank+1d10 vitality to undead, basic Ref; counteracts magical darkness.
- **True Target** | Arcane/Occult | concentrate, fortune, prediction | 2 | 30 ft | Designate a foe; each ally's first attack against it rolls twice and takes the better result, ignoring circumstance penalties and the flat check for concealment or being hidden.
- **Unfettered Pack** | Primal | concentrate, manipulate | 2 | 30 ft, allies | Targets ignore Speed penalties and difficult terrain from environmental hazards such as vegetation, rubble, and wind for the duration.
- **Visions of Danger** | Occult | auditory, concentrate, illusion, manipulate, visual | 2 | 500 ft, 30-ft burst | Player Core 2. An illusory swarm deals 8d8 mental, basic Will, to creatures inside; a critical success or successful disbelief negates it thereafter. Heighten +1: +2d8.
- **Volcanic Eruption** | Primal | concentrate, fire, manipulate | 2 | 500 ft; 5-ft-radius, 80-ft cylinder | 14d6 fire, basic Ref; failures are encased in cooling rock (Clumsy 1, -10-ft Speed) and everything nearby takes automatic heat damage. Heighten +1: +2d6.
- **Warp Mind** | Arcane/Occult | concentrate, emotion, incapacitation, manipulate, mental | 2 | 30 ft, 1 creature | Will save scrambles a target's senses and thoughts; the effect resolves instantly and resists most counteracting magic.
