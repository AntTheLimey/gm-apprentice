# Pathfinder 2e (Remaster) — Classes

24 ORC-remastered classes. Per class: key attribute, HP/level (add Con
modifier each level), spellcasting where relevant, a proficiency summary,
signature level-1 features with the defining mechanic, and the subclass
choice with its options. HP/level and progressions are game facts; all
descriptive text is paraphrased.

**Coverage note:** Magus, Summoner, and Kineticist have not yet received an
ORC-remastered release and are out of scope for this reference.

## Player Core

### Bard

**Key Attribute:** Cha | **HP:** 8 | **Tradition:** Occult, spontaneous
**Proficiency:** Light armor; simple weapons plus a few martial; strong Will.

Occult repertoire caster with composition focus spells (Inspire Courage and
kin) fuelled by a Focus Pool. Signature: **Composition Spells**, **Spell
Repertoire**, **Muses**. Support and enchantment specialist.

**Subclass — Muse:** Enigma, Maestro, Polymath, Warrior.

### Cleric

**Key Attribute:** Wis | **HP:** 8 | **Tradition:** Divine, prepared
**Proficiency:** Armor by doctrine; simple weapons plus deity's favored
weapon; strong Will.

Divine prepared caster serving a deity. Signature: **Deity**, **Divine
Font** (extra heal or harm slots), **Doctrine**. Font plus font-scaling
healing defines the archetype.

**Subclass — Doctrine:** Cloistered Cleric, Warpriest.

### Druid

**Key Attribute:** Wis | **HP:** 8 | **Tradition:** Primal, prepared
**Proficiency:** Light/medium armor (no metal); simple weapons; strong Will.

Primal prepared caster bound by an anathema. Signature: **Druidic Order**,
**Wildsong**, **Shield Block**, plus order-granted focus spells. Order sets
the flavor and bonus feat.

**Subclass — Order:** Animal, Leaf, Storm, Untamed.

### Fighter

**Key Attribute:** Str or Dex | **HP:** 10 | **Tradition:** —
**Proficiency:** All armor plus shields; simple, martial, and advanced
weapons; strong Fortitude and Reflex.

The weapon master: highest attack proficiency in the game. Signature:
**Reactive Strike**, **Shield Block**, wide feat access. Combat flexibility
lets it retrain feats daily at higher levels.

**Subclass:** None — defined by weapon choice and a deep martial feat list.

### Ranger

**Key Attribute:** Str or Dex | **HP:** 10 | **Tradition:** —
**Proficiency:** Light/medium armor; simple and martial weapons; strong
Fortitude.

Precision hunter. Signature: **Hunt Prey** (mark a target for tracking and
attack bonuses) and **Hunter's Edge**, which shapes how Hunt Prey pays off.

**Subclass — Hunter's Edge:** Flurry, Outwit, Precision.

### Rogue

**Key Attribute:** Dex (racket may vary) | **HP:** 8 | **Tradition:** —
**Proficiency:** Light armor; simple weapons plus agile/finesse martials;
strong Reflex and Will.

Skill-dense striker. Signature: **Sneak Attack**, **Surprise Attack**,
**Rogue's Racket**, plus extra skill increases. Off-guard foes take stacked
precision damage.

**Subclass — Racket:** Mastermind, Ruffian, Scoundrel, Thief.

### Witch

**Key Attribute:** Int | **HP:** 6 | **Tradition:** Set by patron, prepared
**Proficiency:** Unarmored; simple weapons; strong Will.

Prepared caster serving a mysterious patron through a familiar. Signature:
**Patron** (sets tradition and first lesson), **Familiar**, **Hexes** (focus
cantrips). Patron themes include Faith's Flamekeeper, Silence in Snow,
Starless Shadow, The Resentment, and Wilding Steward.

**Subclass — Patron/Lessons:** patron theme sets tradition and grants a
first witch's lesson and unique familiar ability.

### Wizard

**Key Attribute:** Int | **HP:** 6 | **Tradition:** Arcane, prepared
**Proficiency:** Unarmored; simple weapons and crossbow; strong Will.

Arcane prepared caster with the widest slot access. Signature: **Arcane
Bond** (drain a bonded item to recast), **Arcane School**, **Arcane
Thesis**. Thesis reshapes casting (e.g., spell substitution, spell
blending, staff nexus).

**Subclass — Curriculum:** Ars Grammatica, Battle Magic, Civic Wizardry,
Mentalism, Protean Form, the Boundary, Unified Magical Theory.

## Player Core 2

### Alchemist

**Key Attribute:** Int | **HP:** 8 | **Tradition:** — (alchemical)
**Proficiency:** Light/medium armor; simple weapons plus alchemical bombs;
strong Fortitude.

Crafts free daily infused reagents into bombs, elixirs, mutagens, and
poisons. Signature: **Alchemy**, **Research Field**, and vial-fuelled Quick
Alchemy at the table.

**Subclass — Research Field:** Bomber, Chirurgeon, Mutagenist, Toxicologist.

### Barbarian

**Key Attribute:** Str | **HP:** 12 | **Tradition:** —
**Proficiency:** Light/medium armor; martial weapons; strong Fortitude,
weak Will.

Rage-fuelled bruiser with the biggest hit die. Signature: **Rage** (bonus
damage, temporary HP, penalties while raging), **Instinct**,
**Quick-Tempered**. Instinct grants a unique rage benefit and anathema.

**Subclass — Instinct:** Animal, Dragon, Fury, Giant, Spirit, Superstition.

### Champion

**Key Attribute:** Str or Dex | **HP:** 10 | **Tradition:** Divine (focus)
**Proficiency:** All armor plus shields; simple and martial weapons; strong
Fortitude.

Deity-sworn defender with a sanctification (holy/unholy). Signature:
**Cause**, **Deity**, **Devotion Spells**, **Shield Block**, plus the
cause's protective champion's reaction.

**Subclass — Cause:** Desecration, Justice, Liberation, Obedience,
Redemption.

### Investigator

**Key Attribute:** Int | **HP:** 8 | **Tradition:** —
**Proficiency:** Light armor; simple weapons; strong Will.

Deductive skill-monkey. Signature: **Devise a Stratagem** (roll ahead to
attack with Int and add precision), **On the Case**, **Strategic Strike**,
**Methodology**. Turns clues into combat edges.

**Subclass — Methodology:** Alchemical Sciences, Empiricism, Forensic
Medicine, Interrogation.

### Monk

**Key Attribute:** Str or Dex | **HP:** 10 | **Tradition:** —
**Proficiency:** Unarmored; unarmed, simple, and monk weapons; strong in
all three saves.

Martial-arts specialist. Signature: **Flurry of Blows** (two unarmed
strikes as one action), **Powerful Fist**, later **Incredible Movement**
and Path to Perfection save boosts. Stances and ki feats customize it.

**Subclass:** None at level 1 — built through stances and feats.

### Oracle

**Key Attribute:** Cha | **HP:** 8 | **Tradition:** Divine, spontaneous
**Proficiency:** Light/medium armor; simple weapons; strong Will.

Spontaneous divine caster paying a price. Signature: **Mystery**,
**Revelation Spells**, **Oracular Curse** (escalating cursebound effects as
you tap deeper). Curse worsens through minor, moderate, major stages.

**Subclass — Mystery:** Ancestors, Battle, Bones, Cosmos, Flames, Life,
Lore, Tempest.

### Sorcerer

**Key Attribute:** Cha | **HP:** 6 | **Tradition:** By bloodline,
spontaneous
**Proficiency:** Unarmored; simple weapons; strong Will.

Innate spontaneous caster whose bloodline sets tradition and focus spells.
Signature: **Bloodline**, **Bloodline Spells**, **Spell Repertoire**,
**Sorcerous Potency**. Blood magic triggers on focus-spell casts.

**Subclass — Bloodline:** Aberrant, Angelic, Demonic, Diabolic, Draconic,
Elemental, Fey, Hag, Imperial, Undead.

### Swashbuckler

**Key Attribute:** Dex | **HP:** 10 | **Tradition:** —
**Proficiency:** Light armor; simple and martial weapons; strong Reflex.

Panache-driven duelist. Signature: **Panache** (a bravado state earned by
stylish acts), **Precise Strike** (bonus damage while it lasts), **Confident
Finisher**, **Swashbuckler's Style**. Style sets how panache is gained.

**Subclass — Style:** Battledancer, Braggart, Fencer, Gymnast, Wit.

## Guns & Gears (ORC)

### Gunslinger

**Key Attribute:** Dex | **HP:** 8 | **Tradition:** —
**Proficiency:** Light/medium armor; simple and martial weapons plus
firearms and crossbows; strong Reflex.

Firearm and crossbow specialist. Signature: **Gunslinger's Way** (grants an
initial deed and a slinger's reload) and **Slinger's Precision**. Deeds and
reload tricks scale by way.

**Subclass — Way:** Drifter, Pistolero, Sniper, Vanguard.

### Inventor

**Key Attribute:** Int | **HP:** 8 | **Tradition:** —
**Proficiency:** Light/medium armor; simple and martial weapons; strong
Reflex.

Gadgeteer built around one signature **Innovation**. Signature: **Explode**,
**Overdrive** (rev up for bonus damage), **Peerless Inventor**, **Shield
Block**, plus daily Reconfigure to retune modifications.

**Subclass — Innovation:** Armor, Construct, Weapon.

## Dark Archive (Remastered, ORC)

### Psychic

**Key Attribute:** Int or Cha | **HP:** 6 | **Tradition:** Occult,
spontaneous
**Proficiency:** Unarmored; simple weapons; strong Will.

Spontaneous occult caster with fewer slots but amplifiable cantrips.
Signature: **Conscious Mind** and **Subconscious Mind** (paired subclasses),
**Psi Cantrips and Amps**, **Unleash Psyche** for burst power.

**Subclass — Conscious Mind:** Distant Grasp, Infinite Eye, Oscillating
Wave, Silent Whisper, Tangible Dream, Unbound Step (paired with a
Subconscious Mind).

### Thaumaturge

**Key Attribute:** Cha | **HP:** 8 | **Tradition:** —
**Proficiency:** Light/medium armor; simple and martial weapons; strong
Will.

Occult-lore martial who exploits weaknesses with esoterica. Signature:
**Exploit Vulnerability**, **Esoteric Lore**, **First Implement and
Esoterica**, **Implement's Empowerment**. Implements grant scaling powers.

**Subclass — Implement:** Amulet, Bell, Chalice, Lantern, Mirror, Regalia,
Tome, Wand, Weapon.

## Battlecry! (ORC)

### Commander

**Key Attribute:** Int | **HP:** 8 | **Tradition:** —
**Proficiency:** Light/medium armor plus shields; simple and martial
weapons; strong Fortitude.

Tactical leader who buffs allies with Int-based commands. Signature:
**Commander's Banner**, **Drilled Reactions**, **Shield Block**, and
**Tactics** — a growing folio of squad maneuvers and a mobile banner aura.

**Subclass — Tactics:** choose starting tactics that expand into a personal
tactics folio (no fixed doctrine).

### Guardian

**Key Attribute:** Str | **HP:** 12 | **Tradition:** —
**Proficiency:** All armor including heavy plus shields; simple and martial
weapons; strong Fortitude.

Frontline protector built to soak hits for allies. Signature: **Guardian's
Armor**, **Taunt** (force foes to focus you), **Guardian's Techniques**
(Ever Ready, Intercept Attack), **Shield Block**.

**Subclass:** None — defined by Guardian's Techniques and defensive feats.

## War of Immortals (ORC)

### Animist

**Key Attribute:** Wis | **HP:** 8 | **Tradition:** Divine, prepared
**Proficiency:** Light/medium armor; simple weapons; strong Will.

Divine caster who bonds with spirits (apparitions) for swappable magic.
Signature: **Apparition Spellcasting**, **Apparition Attunement**,
**Animistic Practice**. Attuned apparitions grant vessel and apparition
spells that can be re-slotted each day.

**Subclass — Animistic Practice:** Liturgist, Medium, Seer, Shaman.

### Exemplar

**Key Attribute:** Str or Dex | **HP:** 10 | **Tradition:** Divine (focus)
**Proficiency:** Light/medium armor plus shields; simple and martial
weapons; strong Will.

Rising demigod who channels a divine spark between sacred ikons. Signature:
**Divine Spark and Ikons**, **Humble Strikes**, **Shield Block**. Shifting
the spark powers ikon abilities and transcendence actions; Epithets add
identity at later levels.

**Subclass — Ikons/Epithets:** choose ikons (immortal, weapon, worn, and
more) shaped by Root, Dominion, and Sovereignty epithets.
