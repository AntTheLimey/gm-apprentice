## Encumbrance

Basic Lift (BL) = ST x ST / 5 (round to nearest whole number).

| Level | Max Weight | Move Penalty | Dodge Penalty |
|-------|-----------|--------------|---------------|
| None (0) | up to BL | 0 | 0 |
| Light (1) | up to 2 x BL | -1 | -1 |
| Medium (2) | up to 3 x BL | -2 | -2 |
| Heavy (3) | up to 6 x BL | -3 | -3 |
| X-Heavy (4) | up to 10 x BL | -4 | -4 |

Cannot carry more than 10 x BL. Move = Basic Speed (drop fractions) minus encumbrance penalty.

### Common BL Values

| ST | BL | Light (1) max | Medium (2) max | Heavy (3) max |
|----|-----|---------------|----------------|---------------|
| 9 | 16 | 32 | 48 | 96 |
| 10 | 20 | 40 | 60 | 120 |
| 11 | 24 | 48 | 72 | 144 |
| 12 | 29 | 58 | 87 | 174 |
| 13 | 34 | 68 | 102 | 204 |

**Example:** ST 10 (BL 20) with tac vest (9) + helmet (3) + rifle (9) + web gear + ammo (~10) = ~31 lbs = Light encumbrance (Move -1, Dodge -1).

## Cinematic Combat Notes

The following require **Trained by a Master** [30] or **Weapon Master** [20-45]:

- Rapid Strike penalties halved (to -3 each)
- Access to cinematic skills: Power Blow, Kiai, Pressure Points, Blind Fighting, Flying Leap, Breaking Blow
- Chambara-style dramatic combat

**Gunslinger** [25] is the ranged equivalent:

- Eliminate Bulk penalty when shooting without Aiming
- Halve other ranged combat penalties
- Enables cinematic Gun Fu techniques (Dual-Weapon Attack at -4 each, unusual shooting positions)
