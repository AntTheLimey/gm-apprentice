> ¹ **Language costs:** Broken [1], Accented [2], Native [3] per component (Spoken/Written). Comprehension and literacy bought separately. Native language is free. See B24.
>
> ² **Wealth levels:** Comfortable [10], Wealthy [20], Very Wealthy [30], Filthy Rich [50], Multimillionaire 1 [75], +25/level thereafter. See B25.
>
> ³ **Reputation:** Base +1/level (max +4). Cost = base x frequency x scope. Per-reaction bonus [5/level], people affected and frequency modify cost. See B27.
>
> ⁴ **Status:** 5/level up or down. High Status grants reaction bonuses and social privileges. Each level of Status may require Wealth to maintain. See B28–29.
>
> ⁵ **Ally:** Base cost by power level — 25% [1], 50% [2], 75% [3], 100% [5], 150% [10] of PC points. Multiply by frequency: 6 or less (x0.5), 9 or less (x1), 12 or less (x2), 15 or less (x4). Groups multiply further. See B36–38.
>
> ⁶ **Contact:** Cost = skill bonus (12 [1], 15 [2], 18 [3], 21 [4]) x frequency (6 or less x0.5, 9 or less x1, 12 or less x2, 15 or less x4). Contact Group: base [5/10/15/20] x frequency. See B44–45.
>
> ⁷ **Patron:** Base cost by patron power — 150% of PC points [10], 200% [15], unlimited individual [20], extremely powerful org [25], national government/deity [30]. Multiply by frequency: 6 or less (x0.5), 9 or less (x1), 12 or less (x2), 15 or less (x4). See B72–74.

> ⁸ **Wealth (Negative):** Struggling [-10], Poor [-15], Dead Broke [-25]. See B25.
>
> ⁹ **Reputation (Negative):** Same structure as positive but with reaction penalties. See B27.
>
> ¹⁰ **Dependent:** Base cost by competence — 0-point [0], 25% [-1], 50% [-2], 75% [-5], 100% [-10], loved one x2. Multiply by frequency: 6 or less (x0.5), 9 or less (x1), 12 or less (x2), 15 or less (x4). See B131.
>
> ¹¹ **Duty:** Base by frequency — 6 or less [-2], 9 or less [-5], 12 or less [-10], 15 or less [-15]. Extremely Hazardous multiplies base cost. Involuntary adds -5. See B134.
>
> ¹² **Enemy:** Base by threat — less powerful [-5], equal [-10], more powerful [-20], entire government [-30]. Multiply by frequency: 6 or less (x0.5), 9 or less (x1), 12 or less (x2). See B135.
>
> ¹³ **Social Stigma:** Various sub-types at -5 to -20, each with different reaction penalties and social limitations. See B155.
