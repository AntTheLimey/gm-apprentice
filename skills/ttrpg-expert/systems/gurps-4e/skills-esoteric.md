### Cinematic Combat Prerequisites

**Trained by a Master** [30]: Halves Rapid Strike penalties (to -3
each), enables cinematic skills, enables cinematic techniques.
Required for most unarmed cinematic abilities.

**Weapon Master** [20-45]: Same benefits but weapon-focused. Cost
depends on scope: single weapon [20], small group [25], medium
group [30], large group [35], all weapons [45].

Both advantages grant access to the cinematic skills above and
allow Chambara-style dramatic combat rules from GURPS Martial Arts.

**Gunslinger** [25]: The firearms equivalent. Halves Dual-Weapon
Attack penalties, enables cinematic firearms techniques from
GURPS Gun Fu.
