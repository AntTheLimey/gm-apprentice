## How Powers Work

A "power" is a thematic group of advantages sharing a power
source modifier. Build procedure:

1. Choose Power Source (Divine, Magical, Psionic, Spirit,
   Super, Chi, or custom with GM approval).
2. Select Abilities -- pick advantages for the power's effects.
3. Apply the power source modifier to every advantage.
4. Layer additional enhancements/limitations per ability.
5. Calculate final cost: base x (1 + net %). Min 20% of base.
6. Optionally add a Power Talent for roll bonuses.

---

## Power Source Modifiers

| Power Source | Modifier | Description |
|-------------|----------|-------------|
| Divine | -10% | Requires faith and patron deity; revoked for moral violations |
| Magical | -10% | Can be dispelled; detected as magic; blocked by anti-magic |
| Psionic | -10% | Subject to anti-psi; detected as psionic |
| Spirit | -25% | Requires spirit cooperation; spirits may refuse or demand payment |
| Super | -10% | Innate metahuman ability; affected by Neutralize/Static vs supers |
| Chi | -10% | Internal energy; requires discipline; disrupted by injury |

## Limitations

| Limitation | Cost | Effect |
|------------|------|--------|
| Accessibility | varies | Limited conditions for use |
| Costs Fatigue | -5%/FP | Drains FP each use |
| Limited Use | -10%/-20% | 2/day [-10%], 1/day [-20%] |
| Nuisance Effect | -5% | Annoying side effect |
| Requires (Attr) Roll | -10% | Must roll attribute to activate |
| Takes Extra Time | -10%/level | Double activation time per level |
| Unreliable | -5% to -40% | Activation roll: 15 [-5%], 12 [-10%], 8 [-40%] |
| Emanation | -20% | Centered on self; can't exclude self |
| Melee Attack | -25% | Requires Reach C |
| Requires Concentrate | -15% | Must take Concentrate maneuver |
| Uncontrollable | -10% to -30% | Can't fully control activation |

**Net Modifier Rule:** Final cost = base x (1 + net %).
Minimum cost is always **20% of base** (round up). No
matter how many limitations, you pay at least 1/5 of the
unmodified price.

## Power Talents

All psionic talents are 5 pts/level (max 4). Each adds its
level to activation rolls, resistance rolls, and related
skill rolls. Also gives a reaction bonus.

| Talent | Cost/Level | Applies To |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| Antipsi | 5/level | Neutralize, Screen, Static |
| ESP | 5/level | Clairsentience, Precognition, Psychometry |
| PK | 5/level | TK, PK Shield, Cryokinesis, Pyrokinesis |
| Telepathy | 5/level | Telesend, Telereceive, Mind Control, Mental Blow |
| Psychic Healing | 5/level | Cure, Stabilize |
| Teleportation | 5/level | Teleportation-based abilities |

Non-psionic power talents: Narrow 5/level, Moderate
10/level, Broad 15/level.

## Quick-Build Examples (~150 pts in powers)

**Telepath** [143 pts]: Mind Reading (Psi -10%) [27] +
Mind Control (Psi -10%) [45] + Telesend (Psi -10%) [27] +
Mind Shield 4 (Psi -10%) [15] + Telepathy Talent 3 [15] +
Mental Blow 3d (Psi -10%) [14].

**Psychokinetic** [145 pts]: TK 15 (Psi -10%) [68] +
PK Shield/DR 8 (Force Field +20%; Psi -10%) [44] +
Burning Attack 4d (Psi -10%) [18] + PK Talent 3 [15].

DR 10 (Tough Skin -40%; Super -10%) [25] +
