## Perks (1-point Advantages)

Perks are minor advantages that cost exactly 1 point. They
provide small benefits or flavor. A character may take any
number of perks, subject to GM approval.

| Perk | Cost | Page | Notes |
|------|------|------|-------|
| Accessory | 1 | B100 | Built-in minor device (exotic races) |
| Alcohol Tolerance | 1 | B100 | +2 to HT rolls to resist alcohol |
| Autotrance | 1 | B100 | Enter hypnotic trance at will |
| Deep Sleeper | 1 | B100 | Sleep through anything; +1 to wake refreshed |
| Fur | 1 | B100 | Natural fur coat; +1 DR vs cold |
| Honest Face | 1 | B100 | +1 to Fast-Talk and Con attempts |
| No Hangover | 1 | B100 | Never suffer hangover effects |
| Penetrating Voice | 1 | B100 | Voice carries; heard clearly at distance |
| Sanitized Metabolism | 1 | B100 | No body odor; sweat is clean |
| Shtick | 1 | B100 | Minor "signature" ability (GM approval) |

## Quirks (-1-point Disadvantages)

Quirks are minor disadvantages worth -1 point each. They add
personality and flavor. A character may have up to 5 quirks
for a maximum of -5 points. Quirks do not count against the
campaign disadvantage limit.
