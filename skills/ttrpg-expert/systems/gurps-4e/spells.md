Basic Set includes a limited spell selection. This file grows
significantly when GURPS Magic (M) is integrated.

## Spell Basics

- Each spell is an IQ/Hard skill (Enchant and some others are IQ/VH)
- Effective level = IQ + Magery + relative level from points
- Requires Magery 0 [5] minimum
- Higher Magery adds to spell skill and IQ for learning
- Cost reduction: -1 FP per 2 full levels above 15 (min 1 FP)
- Maintaining spells: many allow maintenance at reduced cost
  per duration interval; each maintained spell gives -1 to new
  casting rolls

## Column Key

- **Diff**: Skill difficulty (IQ/H = IQ/Hard, IQ/VH = IQ/Very Hard)
- **Page**: Basic Set (B) and GURPS Magic (M) page references
- **Prereq**: Required spells, advantages, or attributes
- **Notes**: Key mechanical details (see page refs for full rules)

## Colleges Not Represented in Basic Set

The following colleges exist in the full GURPS Magic book but
have no spells in the Basic Set spell list:

- **Animal** -- Animal spells appear in GURPS Magic (M)
- **Enchantment** -- Additional enchantments in M
- **Food** -- Food spells appear in M
- **Illusion & Creation** -- Illusion spells appear in M
- **Making & Breaking** -- Appears in M
- **Plant** -- Plant spells appear in M
- **Sound** -- Sound spells appear in M
- **Technology** -- Technology spells appear in M (and
  GURPS Technomancer)
- **Weather** -- Some Weather spells appear above cross-listed
  with Air/Water; full college in M
