## Melee Damage Formula

Melee weapon damage in GURPS 4e is based on the wielder's ST,
expressed as **Swing (sw)** or **Thrust (thr)** plus a modifier
and a damage type.

- **Swing (sw)**: The stronger attack; consult the Damage Table
  (B16) for the base dice. A broadsword deals sw+1 cut.
- **Thrust (thr)**: The weaker but faster attack. A spear deals
  thr+2 imp (one-handed) or thr+3 imp (two-handed).
- **Damage Types**: cut (cutting), cr (crushing), imp (impaling),
  pi (piercing). Each type has different wounding multipliers
  after penetrating DR.

**Key Parry Codes**: 0 = weapon skill level; 0U = Unbalanced
(cannot parry again until next turn); 0F = Fencing weapon
(uses better fencing parry rules); No = cannot parry.

**ST Codes**: A plain number is minimum ST. The dagger (†)
means the weapon requires two hands but one hand can hold it
(add 1.5x listed ST for one-handed use). The double-dagger (‡)
means the weapon becomes unready after an attack.

## Ranged Weapon Stats Explained

Ranged weapons use these key statistics:

- **Acc (Accuracy)**: Bonus to hit when you take an Aim maneuver.
  Higher is better. A scope adds to this (shown as Acc+scope).
- **Range**: Listed as half-damage range / maximum range in yards.
  For muscle-powered weapons, multiply by the weapon's ST.
- **RoF (Rate of Fire)**: Shots per second. RoF 3 means three
  aimed shots or a burst of three. "x9" means each shot fires
  9 pellets (e.g., shotgun). "!" means the weapon fires
  four-round bursts.
- **Shots**: Magazine capacity and reload time in parentheses.
  "T(1)" means thrown, 1 second to ready another.
- **Bulk**: Handling penalty; applied when shooting without Aiming,
  or using Speed/Range penalties. More negative = bulkier.
- **Rcl (Recoil)**: Penalty to hit on each successive shot in a
  burst after the first. Rcl 2 means -1 per two shots.
- **ST**: Minimum ST to use; † means two-handed; B means must be
  braced or mounted.

## Quick Weapon Reference by Campaign Genre

**Fantasy (TL0-3)**: Broadsword, spear, axe, longbow, crossbow,
mail and plate armor.

**Swashbuckling (TL4)**: Rapier, saber, smallsword, flintlock
pistol, musket, buff coat.

**Wild West / Victorian (TL5)**: Revolver, lever-action carbine,
double shotgun, derringer.

**Modern (TL7-8)**: Auto pistol 9mm, assault rifle 5.56mm,
pump shotgun, SMG, ballistic vest.

**Near Future (TL9-10)**: ICW, gauss weapons, electrolaser,
tactical suit, ballistic suit.

**Far Future (TL11+)**: Blaster pistol/rifle, laser weapons,
force sword, force shield.
