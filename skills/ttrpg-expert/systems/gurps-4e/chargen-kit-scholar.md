## Build Framework

Point allocation guidance for scholar archetypes. Scale
proportionally for other point totals.

| Category | 150 pts | 175 pts | 250 pts |
|----------|---------|---------|---------|
| Attributes | 60-80 | 70-90 | 90-120 |
| Secondary mods | 0-5 | 0-5 | 5-10 |
| Advantages | 20-35 | 25-45 | 40-70 |
| Disadvantages | -40 to -50 | -50 to -70 | -50 to -75 |
| Quirks | -5 | -5 | -5 |
| Skills | 50-80 | 65-100 | 100-150 |

**Attribute priorities:** IQ 14+ is the foundation — more than
any other archetype, scholars live on IQ. Every +1 IQ saves
points across a huge skill list and improves all defaults.
ST 9-10 (scholars rarely need lifting power). DX 10-11 for
surgeons and field medics (Surgery defaults off IQ but DX
still matters). HT 10-11 for medics who need to keep up with
the action.

**Near-mandatory advantages:** Eidetic Memory [5] is the best
value in the game for any scholar — halve study time and +5 to
recall. Any IQ-heavy build recycles its cost within a session.
Healer Talent [10/level] if medic focus: it applies to
Diagnosis, First Aid, Pharmacy, Physician, Surgery. Mathematical
Ability [10/level] for scientists and engineers. Artificer
[10/level] for inventor/engineer builds.

**Skills budget is the largest category.** Scholars are defined
by their skill list, not raw attributes or combat ability.
Budget 50-100 points on skills. Prioritize 1-2 professional
skills at 16-18, then a broad supporting spread at 12-14.

## Basic Self-Defense

Scholars typically have little combat training. Budget 2-4 points for basic survival ability.

| Skill | Diff | Page | Cost for IQ 14 Scholar | Notes |
|-------|------|------|----------------------|-------|
| Brawling | DX/E | B182 | 1-2 pts | Parry = Skill/2+3; +1 dmg per 2 levels above 2. Best value |
| Knife | DX/E | B208 | 1-2 pts | Scalpel doubles as weapon at default |
| Staff | DX/A | B208 | 2-4 pts | Walking cane or field-improvised pole |
| First Aid | IQ/E | B195 | Already in kit | Stabilizing self or allies after a fight |

**Dodge:** Basic Speed / 4 (drop fractions) + 3. Scholar with DX 10, HT 10 has Basic Speed 5.00, Dodge 8. Retreating adds +3 (to 11). Advise players to run or hide rather than fight.

**Recommended spend:** 1-2 pts Brawling for unarmed Parry; rely on Dodge and retreating.
