## Cross-Category Notes

Many skills in this section appear across multiple categories.
The primary listing is determined by the GCA data's first
non-General category:

- **Observation** is listed here under Military but is equally
  useful for Police, Spy, and Criminal/Street characters.
- **Stealth** appears under Criminal/Street but is core for
  Military, Police, and Spy roles.
- **Interrogation** spans Military, Police, and Spy contexts.
- **Shadowing** and **Lockpicking** bridge Criminal/Street,
  Police, and Spy work.
- **Electronics Operation** specializations (Security,
  Surveillance, Electronic Warfare) are Technical skills used
  heavily in Military, Police, and Spy operations.

### Strategy vs Tactics

- **Strategy** (IQ/H): Planning campaigns and large-scale
  operations. Five specializations by domain (Air, Land, Naval,
  Space, Interstellar). Each defaults to the others at -4.
- **Tactics** (IQ/H): Small-unit combat and encounter planning.
  Complementary rolls for ambushes, defensive positions, etc.
  Both are Military/Police skills.

### Armoury Notes

Armoury specializations default to each other at -4. Each
specialization covers identification, maintenance, and minor
repair of its equipment type. For building new equipment, use
the appropriate Engineer specialization instead.
