# Forged in the Dark -- Crew Types

*GM activities: Table prep, Run, Track*

Each crew type determines the group's focus, special abilities,
available upgrades, contacts, and claim map. The crew type is
how the underworld identifies you. All crews begin at Tier 0,
strong hold, 0 rep, 2 coin.

---

## Assassins

*Professional murderers -- death is your business.*

**Crew XP trigger**: Execute a successful assassination or
accidental death.

**Hunting ground operations**: Accident, Disappearance, Murder,
Ransom

**Starting upgrades**: Prowess Training, a gang of Thugs

### Special Abilities

- **Deadly**: Each PC may add +1 action rating to Hunt, Prowl,
  or Skirmish (max 3).
- **Death Veil**: Due to hard-won experience or occult ritual,
  you don't take extra heat when killing is involved on a
  score.
- **Emberdeath**: Due to arcane ritual, you don't leave behind
  a traceable ghost when you kill. The Spirit Wardens don't
  investigate your victims.
- **No Traces**: When you keep an operation quiet or make it
  look like an accident, earn half the rep value of the target
  (round up) instead of zero. When you end downtime with zero
  heat, take +1 rep.
- **Patron**: When you advance your Tier, it costs half the
  coin it normally would.
- **Predators**: When you use a stealth or deception plan to
  commit murder, +1d to the engagement roll.
- **Vipers**: When you acquire assets during downtime, you may
  spend coin to increase the result by one level per coin
  (instead of the standard rate).
- **Hardened**: Each PC gets +1 trauma box. (Costs three upgrade
  boxes.)

### Crew Upgrades

- Assassin rigging (2 free load of weapons or supplies)
- Ironhook contacts (Tier is effectively +1 higher in prison)
- Elite Skulks (cohorts of that type get +1d)
- Elite Thugs (cohorts of that type get +1d)
- Hardened (+1 trauma box for each PC; costs 3 boxes)

### Contacts

Trevul Hadrathi (a bounty hunter), Casta (a magistrate),
Sevoy (a merchant lord), Leander (an information broker),
Hoxley (a smuggler)

### Claim Map Highlights

Claims include: Turf, Informants, Hagfish Farm (body disposal),
Victim Trophies (+1 rep per score), Cover Operation (-2 heat),
Infirmary (+1d healing), City Records (+1d engagement for
stealth), and Interrogation Chamber (+1d Command/Sway on-site).

## Bravos

*Thugs, ruffians, and killers for hire.*

**Crew XP trigger**: Execute a successful battle, extortion,
show of force, or sabotage operation.

**Hunting ground operations**: Battle, Extortion, Sabotage,
Show of Force

**Starting upgrades**: Resolve Training, a gang of Thugs

### Special Abilities

- **Dangerous**: Each PC may add +1 action rating to Skirmish,
  Wreck, or Attune (max 3).
- **Blood Brothers**: When you suffer harm during a score, you
  may clear 1 stress for each level of harm taken. When one of
  your cohorts is destroyed, each PC gains +1 stress.
- **Door Kickers**: When you execute an assault plan, +1d to
  the engagement roll.
- **Fiends**: Fear is as good as respect. You may count each
  wanted level as if it were turf.
- **Forged in the Fire**: Each PC gets +1d to resistance rolls.
- **War Dogs**: When you're at war (-3 status), your crew
  doesn't lose hold and PCs still get two downtime activities.
- **Ironforged**: Each PC gets +1 stress box. (Costs three
  upgrade boxes.)
- **Slayers**: When you defeat an enemy in battle, you may take
  1 of their possessions as a trophy, with GM approval.

### Crew Upgrades

- Bravos rigging (2 free load of weapons or armor)
- Ironhook contacts
- Elite Rovers (cohorts of that type get +1d)
- Elite Thugs (cohorts of that type get +1d)
- Ironforged (+1 stress box for each PC; costs 3 boxes)

### Contacts

Meg (a tavern owner), Conway (a Bluecoat), Keller (a
blacksmith), Tomas (a physicker), Petra (a spy)

### Claim Map Highlights

Claims include: Turf, Fighting Pits (earn coin), Terrorized
Citizens (+2 coin for battle/extortion), Training Rooms
(+1 scale for a cohort type), Infirmary, Protection Racket,
and Warehouses (+1d to acquire assets).

## Cult

*Acolytes of a dark or forgotten power.*

**Crew XP trigger**: Execute a successful acquisition,
consecration, sacrifice, or occult operation.

**Hunting ground operations**: Acquisition, Consecration,
Sacrifice, Occult

**Starting upgrades**: Insight Training, a gang of Adepts

### Special Abilities

- **Chosen**: Each PC may add +1 action rating to Attune,
  Study, or Sway (max 3).
- **Anointed**: +1d to resistance rolls against supernatural
  threats. +1d to healing rolls when you have supernatural
  harm.
- **Bound in Darkness**: You may use group Attune actions to
  share information and emotional states with members of your
  cult. This is a supernatural bond.
- **Conviction**: Each PC gains an additional xp trigger:
  "Advance the agenda of the deity." Mark xp for this trigger.
- **Glory Incarnate**: Your deity sometimes manifests in the
  physical world. When it does, your crew's Tier counts as +1
  for fortune rolls and quality checks.
- **Sealed in Blood**: Each PC may reduce the stress cost of
  resistance rolls by 1 (minimum 0).
- **Zealotry**: When you perform a score for your deity, take
  +1d to the engagement roll.
- **Ancient Obelisk**: Reduce the stress cost of ritual sorcery
  by 1 (minimum 1).

### Crew Upgrades

- Cult rigging (2 free load of documents or implements)
- Ironhook contacts
- Elite Adepts (cohorts of that type get +1d)
- Elite Thugs (cohorts of that type get +1d)
- Ancient Obelisk (reduce ritual stress cost by 1)

### Contacts

Gagan (an academic), Adikin (an occultist), Hutchins (an
antiquarian), Moriya (a spirit trafficker), Mateas Kline (a
noble)

### Claim Map Highlights

Claims include: Turf, Ancient Altar (+1d engagement for occult
plans), Ancient Tower (+1d Consort with arcane entities),
Offertory (+2 coin for occult operations), Spirit Well (gain
access to rare arcane power), and Cloister (training area
for cult members).

## Hawkers

*Vice dealers and merchants of illicit goods.*

**Crew XP trigger**: Execute a successful acquisition, sale,
supply, or show of force operation.

**Hunting ground operations**: Acquisition, Sale, Show of
Force, Supply

**Starting upgrades**: Insight Training, a gang of Thugs

### Special Abilities

- **Silver Tongues**: Each PC may add +1 action rating to
  Command, Consort, or Sway (max 3).
- **Accord**: You may treat up to three +3 faction statuses as
  if they are turf.
- **The Good Stuff**: Your product is special. When you push
  product, take +1d.
- **Ghost Market**: Through occult means, you have access to a
  market of supernatural entities. They trade in strange goods
  and services.
- **High Society**: -1 heat during downtime. +1d to gather
  information about the city's elite.
- **Hooked**: Your regular clients are addicted and reliable.
  +2 coin when collecting from them during downtime, minus heat.
- **Pack Rats**: Your lair is a jumble of stolen items. +1d to
  acquire asset rolls.
- **Patron**: When you advance your Tier, it costs half the
  coin it normally would.

### Crew Upgrades

- Hawker rigging (2 free load of supplies or gear)
- Ironhook contacts
- Elite Rooks (cohorts of that type get +1d)
- Elite Thugs (cohorts of that type get +1d)
- Composed (+1 stress box for each PC; costs 3 boxes)

### Contacts

Reyf (a drug dealer), Laroze (a Bluecoat), Lydra (a deal
broker), Hoxley (a smuggler), Anya (a dilettante)

### Claim Map Highlights

Claims include: Turf, Luxury Venue (+1d Consort/Sway on-site),
Surplus Cache (+2 coin for supply/sale), Vice Den (earn coin),
Tavern (+1d Consort/Sway on-site), Cover Operation, and Side
Business.

## Shadows

*Thieves, spies, and saboteurs.*

**Crew XP trigger**: Execute a successful burglary, espionage,
robbery, or sabotage operation.

**Hunting ground operations**: Burglary, Espionage, Robbery,
Sabotage

**Starting upgrades**: Prowess Training, a gang of Skulks

### Special Abilities

- **Everyone Steals**: Each PC may add +1 action rating to
  Prowl, Finesse, or Tinker (max 3).
- **Ghost Echoes**: From the ghost field, you may discover
  echoes of things that once existed but are now gone:
  buildings, roads, people, objects, etc.
- **Pack Rats**: Your lair is a jumble of stolen items. +1d to
  acquire asset rolls.
- **Patron**: When you advance your Tier, it costs half the
  coin it normally would.
- **Second Story**: When you execute a clandestine infiltration,
  +1d to the engagement roll.
- **Slippery**: When you roll entanglements, you may roll twice
  and keep the one you prefer.
- **Synchronized**: When you perform a group action, you may
  count multiple 6s from different rolls as a critical.
- **Underground Maps and Passkeys**: You have easy passage
  through the underground canals, tunnels, and basements.

### Crew Upgrades

- Thief rigging (2 free load of tools or gear)
- Ironhook contacts
- Elite Rooks (cohorts of that type get +1d)
- Elite Skulks (cohorts of that type get +1d)
- Underground Maps and Passkeys

### Contacts

Dowler (an explorer), Laroze (a Bluecoat), Amancio (a deal
broker), Fitz (a collector), Adelaide Phroaig (a noble)

### Claim Map Highlights

Claims include: Turf, Loyal Fence (+2 coin for burglary/
robbery), Covert Drop (+2 coin for espionage/sabotage), Secret
Pathways (+1d engagement for stealth plans), Lookouts (+1d
Hunt/Survey on your turf), Informants, and Hagfish Farm.

## Smugglers

*Transporters of contraband and people.*

**Crew XP trigger**: Execute a successful smuggling operation
or acquire new clients or contraband sources.

**Hunting ground operations**: Smuggling Arms, Smuggling
Contraband, Smuggling Passengers

**Starting upgrades**: Resolve Training, a Boat

### Special Abilities

- **Like the Wind**: Each PC may add +1 action rating to
  Finesse, Prowl, or Survey (max 3).
- **All Hands**: During downtime, one of your cohorts may
  perform a downtime activity to acquire an asset, reduce
  heat, or work on a long-term project.
- **Ghost Passage**: When you transport cargo or passengers,
  spirits and ghosts are less likely to notice or interfere.
  +1d to avoid supernatural encounters during transport.
- **Just Passing Through**: During downtime, -1 heat. When heat
  is 4 or less, +1d to deceive people when passing as ordinary
  citizens.
- **Kin**: When you make a desperate roll, you may call upon
  a spirit of a past crewmate for aid. +1d to the roll. You
  take 2 stress.
- **Leverage**: Your crew has dirt on people. When you use
  Consort or Sway on a target, you may spend 1 coin to gain
  +2d instead of the usual +1d from pushing.
- **Renegades**: Each PC may reduce the stress cost of a
  resistance roll by 1 (minimum 0).
- **Camouflage**: Your vehicles are perfectly concealed at rest.

### Crew Upgrades

- Smuggler rigging (2 free load of gear or tools)
- Ironhook contacts
- Elite Rovers (cohorts of that type get +1d)
- Barge (+2 scale for waterway transport)
- Camouflage

### Contacts

Elynn (a dock worker), Rolan (a drug dealer), Sera (an
information broker), Nyelle (a smuggler), Decker (an anarchist)

### Claim Map Highlights

Claims include: Turf, Side Business (earn coin), Luxury
Fence (+2 coin for high-end goods), Secret Routes (+1d
engagement for transport plans), Warehouses, and Fleet
(additional vehicles).

## Crew Advancement Summary

When the crew xp track fills (8 segments):
- Take a new crew special ability, OR
- Mark two crew upgrade boxes

Each advance gives every PC stash equal to Tier + 2.
