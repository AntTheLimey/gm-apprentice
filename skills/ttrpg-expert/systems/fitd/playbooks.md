# Forged in the Dark -- Character Playbooks

*GM activities: Table prep, Run*

Each playbook represents a character's reputation, focus, and
special abilities. Every playbook is a scoundrel at heart --
playbooks are areas of preference, not rigid classes. Any
character can attempt any action.

Playbooks come with 3 starting action dots. Players add 4 more
(max rating 2 at creation). Players also choose 1 special
ability and 5 personal items.

---

## Cutter

*A dangerous and intimidating fighter.*

**XP trigger**: Address a challenge with violence or coercion.

**Starting action dots**: Skirmish 2, Command 1

**Friends/Rivals**: Marlane (a pugilist), Chael (a vicious
thug), Mercy (a cold killer), Grace (an extortionist),
Sawtooth (a physicker)

### Special Abilities

- **Battleborn**: Expend special armor to reduce harm from
  combat or push yourself during a fight.
- **Bodyguard**: When you protect a teammate, +1d to resistance.
  When you gather info to anticipate threats, +1 effect.
- **Ghost Fighter**: You may imbue your hands, melee weapons,
  or tools with spirit energy. You gain potency in combat vs.
  the supernatural.
- **Leader**: When you Command a cohort in combat, they
  continue to fight when they would otherwise break. They gain
  +1 effect and 1 armor.
- **Mule**: Your load limits are higher. Light: 5. Normal: 7.
  Heavy: 8.
- **Not to be Trifled With**: Push yourself to perform a feat
  of superhuman physical force, or engage a small gang on equal
  footing in close combat.
- **Savage**: When you unleash physical violence, it's
  frightening. When you Command a frightened target, +1d.
- **Vigorous**: Recover from harm faster. Permanently fill one
  healing clock segment. +1d to healing treatment rolls.

**Starting items**: Fine hand weapon, Fine heavy weapon,
Scary weapon or tool, Manacles & chain, Rage essence vial

## Hound

*A deadly sharpshooter and tracker.*

**XP trigger**: Address a challenge with tracking or violence.

**Starting action dots**: Hunt 2, Survey 1

**Friends/Rivals**: Steiner (an assassin), Celene (a sentinel),
Melvir (a physicker), Veleris (a spy), Casta (a bounty hunter)

### Special Abilities

- **Sharpshooter**: Push yourself to make a ranged attack at
  extreme distance, or unleash a barrage of rapid fire to
  suppress the enemy.
- **Focused**: Expend special armor to resist a consequence of
  surprise or mental harm, or push yourself for ranged combat
  or tracking.
- **Scout**: When gathering info to discover a target's
  location, +1 effect. When hiding in a prepared position or
  using camouflage, +1d to avoid detection.
- **Tough as Nails**: Penalties from harm are one level less
  severe (level 4 is still fatal).
- **Vengeful**: Additional xp trigger: you got payback against
  someone who harmed you or someone you care about. If crew
  helped, also mark crew xp.
- **Ghost Hunter**: Your hunting pet is imbued with spirit
  energy. It gains potency when tracking or fighting the
  supernatural, and can see ghosts and other hidden spirits.
- **Survivor**: From physical harm or environmental danger, you
  may expend special armor to reduce severity or avoid it.
- **Methodical**: When you lead a group action, you may count
  multiple 6s from different rolls as a critical.

**Starting items**: Fine pair of pistols, Fine long rifle,
Electroplasmic ammunition, A trained hunting pet, Spyglass

## Leech

*A saboteur and technician.*

**XP trigger**: Address a challenge with technical skill or
calculation.

**Starting action dots**: Tinker 2, Wreck 1

**Friends/Rivals**: Stazia (an apothecary), Veldren (a
psychonaut), Eckerd (a corpse thief), Jul (a blood dealer),
Malista (a priestess)

### Special Abilities

- **Alchemist**: When you invent or craft an alchemical
  creation, +1 result level. You begin with one special
  formula already known.
- **Analyst**: During downtime, get two ticks to distribute
  among long-term project clocks involving investigation or
  learning a formula/design.
- **Artificer**: When you invent or craft a spark-craft
  creation, +1 result level. You begin with one special
  design already known.
- **Fortitude**: Expend special armor to resist fatigue,
  weakness, or chemical effects, or push yourself for
  technical skill or handling alchemicals.
- **Supernatural Ward**: When you Wreck an area with arcane
  substances, it becomes anathema or enticing to the
  supernatural (your choice).
- **Saboteur**: When you Wreck, your work is much quieter than
  expected and damage is well-hidden from casual inspection.
- **Venomous**: Choose a drug or poison (from your formulas).
  You can push yourself to secrete it through your skin or
  saliva, or to spray it as a fine mist.
- **Physicker**: You can Tinker with bones, blood, and bodily
  humors to treat wounds and stabilize the dying. You may
  Study a malady or corpse for clues.

**Starting items**: Fine tinkering tools, Fine wrecking tools,
Blowgun & darts (concealed), Bandolier of alchemicals,
Ghost key (arcane)

## Lurk

*A stealthy infiltrator and burglar.*

**XP trigger**: Address a challenge with stealth or evasion.

**Starting action dots**: Prowl 2, Finesse 1

**Friends/Rivals**: Telda (a beggar), Darmot (a Bluecoat),
Frake (a locksmith), Roslyn Kellis (a noble), Petra (a city
clerk)

### Special Abilities

- **Infiltrator**: You are not affected by quality or Tier when
  you bypass security measures.
- **Ambush**: When you attack from hiding or spring a trap,
  +1d.
- **Daredevil**: When you roll a desperate action, +1d if you
  also take -1d to resistance rolls against consequences from
  that action.
- **The Devil's Footsteps**: Push yourself to perform a feat of
  superhuman athletics, or maneuver to confuse enemies so they
  mistakenly attack each other.
- **Expertise**: Choose one action rating. When you lead a
  group action using that action, suffer only 1 stress at
  most.
- **Ghost Veil**: You may shift partially into the ghost field,
  becoming shadowy and insubstantial for a few moments. Take
  2 stress; you may pass through solid objects or become
  invisible.
- **Reflexes**: When there's a question about who acts first,
  the answer is you.
- **Shadow**: Expend special armor to resist detection or
  security consequences, or push yourself for athletics or
  stealth.

**Starting items**: Fine lockpicks, Fine shadow cloak,
Light climbing gear, Silence potion vial, Dark-sight goggles

## Slide

*A subtle manipulator and spy.*

**XP trigger**: Address a challenge with deception or
influence.

**Starting action dots**: Sway 2, Consort 1

**Friends/Rivals**: Bryl (a drug dealer), Bazso Baz (a gang
leader), Klyra (a tavern owner), Nyryx (a prostitute),
Harker (a jail-bird)

### Special Abilities

- **Rook's Gambit**: Take 2 stress to roll your best action
  rating while performing a different action. Describe how you
  adapt your skill.
- **Cloak & Dagger**: When you use a disguise or other
  deceptive cover, +1d to avoid detection or suspicion. When
  you throw off your disguise, the resulting surprise gives
  you potency.
- **Like Looking into a Mirror**: You can always tell when
  someone is lying to you.
- **A Little Something on the Side**: At the end of each
  downtime phase, earn +2 stash.
- **Mesmerism**: When you Sway someone, you may cause them to
  forget it happened until they next interact with you.
- **Subterfuge**: Expend special armor to resist suspicion or
  persuasion consequences, or push yourself for subterfuge.
- **Trust in Me**: +1d vs. a target with whom you have an
  intimate relationship.
- **Calculating**: During downtime, you may give yourself or
  another crew member +1 downtime activity.

**Starting items**: Fine disguise kit, Fine loaded dice or
cards, Trance powder, A forged document, Spiritbane charm

## Spider

*A devious mastermind and planner.*

**XP trigger**: Address a challenge with calculation or
conspiracy.

**Starting action dots**: Consort 2, Study 1

**Friends/Rivals**: Salia (an information broker), Augus (a
master architect), Jennah (a servant), Riven (a chemist),
Jeren (a Bluecoat archivist)

### Special Abilities

- **Foresight**: Two times per score you can assist a teammate
  without paying stress. Describe how you prepared.
- **Calculating**: During downtime, you may give yourself or
  another crew member +1 downtime activity.
- **Connected**: During downtime, +1 result level when you
  acquire an asset or reduce heat.
- **Mastermind**: Expend special armor to protect a teammate, or
  push yourself when you gather information or work on a
  long-term project.
- **Weaving the Web**: +1d to Consort when gathering info on a
  target for a score. +1d to the engagement roll for that
  operation.
- **Ghost Contract**: When you shake on a deal or make a
  promise, you and your partner both bear a mark. If either
  breaks the deal, they take level 3 harm ("Cursed").
- **Jail Bird**: When incarcerated, your wanted level counts as
  1 less, your Tier counts as 1 more, and you retain access
  to two of your special items.
- **Patron**: When you advance your Tier, it costs half the
  coin it normally would.

**Starting items**: Fine cover identity, Fine bottle of
whiskey, Blueprints, Small wrecking tools, Spiritbane charm

## Whisper

*An arcane adept and spirit trafficker.*

**XP trigger**: Address a challenge with knowledge or arcane
power.

**Starting action dots**: Attune 2, Study 1

**Friends/Rivals**: Nyryx (a possessor ghost), Scurlock (a
vampire), Setarra (a demon), Quellyn (a witch), Flint (a
spirit trafficker)

### Special Abilities

- **Compel**: You can Attune to the ghost field to force a
  nearby ghost to appear and answer questions. You aren't
  otherwise affected by the ghost field or ghosts.
- **Arcane Mind**: You're always aware of supernatural entities
  in your presence. +1d whenever you gather information about
  the supernatural.
- **Iron Will**: You are immune to supernatural terror. When
  you make a resistance roll with Resolve, +1d.
- **Ritual**: You know the arcane methods to perform ritual
  sorcery. You can Study an occult ritual (or create a new
  one) to summon a supernatural effect or being. You begin
  with one ritual already learned.
- **Strange Methods**: When you invent or craft an arcane
  creation, +1 result level. You begin with one arcane design
  already known.
- **Warded**: Expend special armor to resist a supernatural
  consequence, or push yourself when contending with arcane
  forces.
- **Tempest**: You can push yourself to do one of the
  following: unleash a blast of electroplasmic energy, or
  create an electroplasmic barrier.
- **Ghost Mind**: You're able to see and communicate with
  ghosts, even if they haven't manifested. Once per score,
  you may ask a ghost a question and receive a truthful
  (if cryptic) answer.

**Starting items**: Fine lightning hook, Fine spirit mask,
Electroplasm vials, Spirit bottles (2), Ghost key

## Playbook Advancement

When a playbook xp track fills (8 segments), clear it and
choose a new special ability. You may choose from:

1. Your own playbook's remaining abilities
2. Another playbook's abilities (mark it as a "veteran" advance)

When an attribute xp track fills (6 segments), clear it and
add one action dot to any action under that attribute.
