# Forged in the Dark -- Advanced Rules Reference

Core rules for effect, consequences, harm, armor, teamwork,
and information gathering.

## Effect Factors

Three factors modify effect above/below default:

**Potency** — situational advantages, extra time/risk, arcane
powers. Infiltrator more potent in darkness.

**Quality/Tier** — effectiveness of tools/resources, usually
Tier. Fine items = +1 quality, stacks with Tier.

**Scale** — number of opponents, area, scope. Larger scale
can be advantage or disadvantage by context.

**Beyond normal range:** effect can drop to **zero** or rise
to **extreme**. "+1 effect" abilities and pushing (2 stress)
apply after GM assessment.

## Position-for-Effect Trade

After GM sets position and effect, player may trade one for
the other (e.g., risky/standard → desperate/great). Always
available; player's choice, not GM's.

## Consequences

Five types from action rolls:

**Reduced Effect** — action underperforms; effect drops one
level. Don't negate a successful roll.

**Complication** — trouble, danger, new threat. Tick a clock:
minor (1), standard (2), serious (3). Don't inflict a
complication that negates a successful roll. If a PC corners
an enemy on 4-5, the enemy IS cornered — maybe they grab
the PC's weapon during the scuffle, but they don't escape.

**Lost Opportunity** — window closes; need new method or
changed circumstances to try again.

**Worse Position** — PC loses control, next attempt at worse
position (controlled → risky → desperate).

**Harm** — lasting debility or death. Record at appropriate
level. If row full, harm moves up. Level 3 full + new level 3
= level 4 (catastrophic/fatal).

## Harm

| Level | Severity | Penalty | Examples |
|:---:|----------|---------|----------|
| 1 | Lesser | Reduced effect | Battered, Drained, Distracted, Scared |
| 2 | Moderate | -1d | Exhausted, Deep Cut, Concussion, Panicked |
| 3 | Severe | Need help to act | Impaled, Broken Leg, Shot in Chest, Terrified |
| 4 | Fatal | Death | Electrocuted, Drowned, Stabbed in Heart |

Penalty applies when harm is relevant to the situation.
Push (2 stress) to act despite level 3 harm alone.

## Armor

**Standard/Heavy:** mark armor box to reduce/avoid consequence
instead of resisting. Heavy = mark second box. Restored at
next score's load selection.

**Special Armor:** from special abilities. Avoid consequence
of appropriate type completely, or push yourself without 2
stress. Restored at beginning of downtime.

## Recovery

Downtime activity. Healer rolls (Tinker for Physicker, or
quality for NPC):

| Result | Healing Clock Ticks |
|:---:|:---:|
| 1-3 | 1 |
| 4-5 | 2 |
| 6 | 3 |
| Crit | 5 |

Healing clock: 4 segments default. When filled, reduce all
harm by one level, clear clock. New harm clears healing ticks.
Self-heal with Physicker: +2 stress. No healer: 1 stress,
roll 0d.

## Death

Two paths: suffer level 4 fatal harm and don't resist, or
level 3 already full when level 3 harm recorded. Dead PC →
promote NPC gang member or create new character.

## Teamwork

| Maneuver | Mechanic |
|----------|----------|
| Assist | 1 stress → +1d to teammate. One per roll. |
| Group Action | All roll same action; best result counts. Leader takes 1 stress per 1-3 result. |
| Protect | Face consequence for teammate. Suffer it; may resist. |
| Set Up | Give teammate +1 effect or improved position on follow-up. |

Assist: one per roll; can assist group action only if not
participating. Group Action: about leadership, not personal
skill; cohorts can participate. Set Up: multiple follow-ups
benefit; provides at least limited effect even at zero.

## Devil's Bargain

Bonus die from GM or any player in exchange for complication
regardless of roll outcome. Player can reject and push (2
stress) instead — cannot get dice from both.

Common bargains: collateral damage, sacrifice coin/item,
betray friend, offend faction, start/tick clock, add heat,
suffer harm.

## Gathering Information

- **Common knowledge:** GM answers directly
- **Obstacle present:** action roll
- **No obstacle, not common:** fortune roll for quality

Effect determines detail: great (exceptional), standard
(good), limited (partial). Complex questions = long-term
project clock during downtime.

Common actions: Attune (supernatural), Command (compel
revelation), Consort (leverage connections), Hunt (track/
follow), Study (research/analyze), Survey (case/read
situation), Sway (charm into divulging).

## Wanted Level

Heat on 9-box track. When full: +1 wanted level, clear heat.
Only reduced by incarceration.

| Wanted | Sentence |
|:---:|----------|
| 4 | Life/execution |
| 3 | A year or two |
| 2 | Several months |
| 1 | A month or two |
| 0 | Few weeks or beating (level 3 harm, no resist) |

### Incarceration Roll (crew Tier dice)

| Result | Outcome |
|:---:|---------|
| Crit | +3 rep, 1 prison claim, +1 faction status |
| 6 | 1 prison claim, +1 faction status |
| 4-5 | No incident |
| 1-3 | Suffer a level of trauma |

Prison claims: Allied Claim, Cell Block Control, Guard Payoff,
Hardcase, Parole Influence, Smuggling.
